The bridge part on the new tooling is a different shape.

Take a look at http://www.internetmodeler.com/1999/september/first-looks/musashi.htm. It reviews and pictures the new tooling vs. the old.

On the boxes for the new toolings, the ships on both box covers are going the opposite directions from the box art on the old toolings (Yamato - new tooling - sailing to the left, Musashi - new tooling - sailing to the right; Yamato - old tooling - sailing to the right, Musashi - old tooling - sailing to the left)!

-David
