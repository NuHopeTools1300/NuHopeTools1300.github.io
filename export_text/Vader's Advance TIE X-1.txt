Here's the kit from Chris Kelley.

Took some extra steps to avoid future warpage.

[IMAGE: 000_0002.jpg]

[IMAGE: 000_0001.jpg]

The outer wing panels were completely remade around a core of 3/32 aluminum sheet.

[IMAGE: CopyofDSCN9231.jpg]

[IMAGE: CopyofDSCN9229.jpg]

A hinged lid was added which opened up some more detailing opportunities.

[IMAGE: DSCN9259.jpg]

[IMAGE: DSCN9258.jpg]

[IMAGE: DSCN9270.jpg]

[IMAGE: DSCN9270.jpg]

Wherever there was a 'pass through' in the main body the cast in details were rebuilt.

[IMAGE: DSCN9266.jpg]

[IMAGE: DSCN9267.jpg]

The mount for the PanaVise is recessed to look a lil cleaner.

[IMAGE: DSCN9265.jpg]

"Glass" was added to cutout or fabricated channels.

[IMAGE: DSCN9255.jpg]
