Sorry it took so long to get this done guys, I know I promised this a while back before my trip, and Ive been back for a while. One of the reasons is that I really hate turning the Falcon upside down as it is VERY heavy and Im always afraid of breaking something in the process. I hope I got everything this time. 

The pictures aer not as good as the pictures I took of the top of the Falcon, I know. That is because I was using a much better camera then, but it was borrowed and I dont have it anymore. Also the day today is gloomy and that day it was sunny so the lighting was much better. 

I tried to take all the pictres just as I did the first batch, although it may not be visible on this one, with the measurements taken from points without plating. This way you can use the measurements to make the ships skin itself and then add plating afterwards.
