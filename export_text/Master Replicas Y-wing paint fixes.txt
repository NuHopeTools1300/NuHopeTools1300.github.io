The MR version was almost destroyed in transit. It came to me for a rebuild and repaint to match the MR as much as possible. The colours were very hard to replicate due to the overlaying dark weathering and other semi translucent layers plus time in the light. In the end i rebuilt the nose and fixed the other issues. I Used Tamia grey primer plus a little TS32 (the tie colour) and some other colours (cant remember sorry) to get the best blend i could. once it was shot with the base, i blended it into the rest of the body to make the whole thing more seamless, and added the dreaded (read that as crappy) black weathering !!!!. If you really want to keep the MR colour scheme, don't worry to much about a super accurate match, just get the best you can and blend areas and panels all over the ship to make it less obvious where the repairs/repaints have been done.
