After 2.5 years, "The Lady" is nearing completion. Only a few things left to do, the adf pods are finished but haven't been attached yet,the cockpit goes in this week and the door handles. It will have working landing gear by remote control as well as working cockpit lights, strobe lights,spotlights. The gentleman who built this for me worked on the original shooting miniature for the show back in the 80's. I'll show more photos when she's done.
