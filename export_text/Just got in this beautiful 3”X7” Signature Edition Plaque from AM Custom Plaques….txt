About just over a week ago AM Custom Plaques was having a Labour Day holiday sale. Couldn’t find anything on sale except for this plaque that wasn’t part of the sale;

STAR WARS RETURN OF THE JEDI

GAMORREAN GUARD - 3X7 Autographed plaque signed by actor Stephen Costantino.

I really wanted it badly so I placed my order. I just loved it when I realized It would go perfectly like hand and glove with my Attakus Gamorrean Guard statue.

If you need a plaque for your Studio Scale Model, I strongly recommend AM Custom Plaques. Been ordering over 2 years from them with no issues.

Ariel is the best and trustworthy when it comes to signed plaques unlike others in the business that are arrogant and extremely difficult to deal with.

Thanks again Ariel.
