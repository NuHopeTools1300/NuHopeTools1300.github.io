Thank you Wildunc1. I got a "production" X-Wing, and I notice the underside is not as dark as we can see on your pix (specially the "black" panels). It seems it is a little more "battle used" too. I see there is more rust on your prototype than on the production model, rust that is clearly visible on the original studio model and that gives an better look. I personaly added that rust onto my model.

Concerning the "nose" it is not evident, but it seems it is correctly aligned on the proto than on the production, a fact that was heavily discussed on forums. On the production model the nose goes "up". It is not so important but it is a mess that eFX would have clearly seen and corrected.
