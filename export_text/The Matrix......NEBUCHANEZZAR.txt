I thought I might post this here. I am doing a rebuild, reconstruction of the  studio scale miniature the Nebuchanezzar from the movie The Matrix. This model is 36 inches long.  You can see by the damage I have a lot of work to do. The hull is fiberglass with alot of the hull detailing done with paper.... This ship was originally done by Guillermo in Argentina. This ship is fully lit and I will be spending alot of time redoing the fiber optics. all the antenae are lit as well as every plasma engine and cockpit. As you can see the back end is the worst as far as damage. I will try to post better pics as soon as I can. these were done with a webcam. Should be fun....
