Well, they were made by the same people from the same model shop with the same art directors, so similarities are inevitable. Sometimes the designs of previous models are used as jumping off points for new designs. It keeps the designs looking "in-universe".

  When I made the Jedi fighter maquette, I noticed that the drawings showed the overall wedge shape as being similar to the Star Destroyer's. So I made the proportions and angles of the wedge shapes of the fighter match the Star Destroyer. It made sense because the Republic was to become the Empire. 

I also was curious to see if anyone would notice.
