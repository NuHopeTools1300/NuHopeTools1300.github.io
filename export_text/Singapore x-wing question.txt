Yeah, definitely the MPC/AMT - you can tell by the weird pinched-up shape of the lower fuselage, just below the cockpit.  If it's touring as part of an ILM exhibit, I would assume it's one of the ROTJ background models, but that's only an assumption.  I can tell you that the paint does not match any of the 2-foot ANH models, or the ROTJ 4-footer.
