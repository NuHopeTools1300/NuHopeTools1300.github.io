I have both. The Atomic City is finished and under glass and the Custom Replicas is (regrettably) still in the box waiting for time and space (and warm weather) to begin. 

both are mighty fine kits. the chief difference is that the body of the AC is joined left and right and the CR is joined top and bottom. If pressed I would say the CR is a crisper and smoother resin casting. 

another big difference is in the presentation. The CR comes with a much more elaborate stand and dry transfer decals where the AC has water slide decals and a simpler base.

then there is the price difference.
