The holy grail. My all time favourite vehicle in the Star Wars universe. 

Thanks to the truly gifted Guy Cowen and Felix Beyer this masterpiece has been produced. I honestly cannot get over how well these pieces are. The vacuum pulls are brilliant and the armature is absolutely stunning. They have truly outdone themselves!

This build will take me a good while as I still don’t have all the kit pieces for it but it will be well worth it in the end. 

Being an avid manual turner/miller, I am very happy with the quality of the machine work involved with the armature. This thing looks cool just sitting on my desk!
