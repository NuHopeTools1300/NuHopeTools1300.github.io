I had never built anything of Star Trek subject matter before so it was a first.Well the deal fell through and I ended up with a 36x25x10 monster in a plywood case I made for shipping.The templates for the hulls feather patterns were a night mare alone. :angry It was one of those projects that raised my own personal bar for modeling a little higher 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
