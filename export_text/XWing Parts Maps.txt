There are a few things about the missing box to go on. It has 2 (diamond) shapes along the inner facing edge. I don't believe that they are the true shapes. I think that repeated casting have deformed them and that they may have been ribs or rivets originally. The second clue is the half circle/hole in the top of this part. The hole allowed ILM to run wiring for the light on the hero X-wings. ILM could have drilled it into the part or it could be on the original piece. Time will. I've been looking for this piece 4-ever.
