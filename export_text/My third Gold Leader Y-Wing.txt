I shaved them down length-wise, cleaned a few nubs inside, lasercut spacers with a slot for the wing (Dave Jones still had the pattern and let me trace it so I got a fool proof angle), and I 3D printed the nosecones, engines, and back vane assemblies as mostly one piece. Then I plated them with .020 styrene (maybe .030?)... the engines were repatterned because the masters were sloppy and not quite right, parts-wise. So I drew the plant ons and grew those too...
