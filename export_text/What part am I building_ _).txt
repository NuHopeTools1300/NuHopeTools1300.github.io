This is a kit by the very talented Mr Mike Salzo. Only a handfull were made. My build thread here:

http://www.therpf.com/f11/assault-hoth-132150/

It is based on the 4foot filming model. The ERTL is pretty inaccurate compared to the real model so just by enlarging it the proportions will be way off. The four foot models also differed from the smaller miniatures. There were 3 of the larger models built. One pyro version, one crashed version and another for closeups/flyby's etc. Each one had different features. The large cylinders on your underbelly need to be round not flattened as on the Ertl. Nice work though. Keep the pix coming.
