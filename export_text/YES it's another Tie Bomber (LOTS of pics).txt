So thanks to Bigturc and Telepathic for figuring out the last little bits of unknown parts on the TB. I wasn't satisfied knowing that some of the parts on my bird were wrong, so I purchased the one kit I didn't already have and grabbed the parts from the kits that I did have. I also discovered some better reference and was able to see some areas on the wing extensions that I had gotten wrong. So I scraped off the parts that were wrong along with the detail on the wing extensions. Reattached the correct parts and detail and re-primed everything. I also noticed that the original pylon covers on the wings were held on with really small set screws, so I added those as well. Last but not least, I got my hands on a small (non screen used) TIE fighter built by an ILM employee with the correct blue paint and was able to have it color matched. So with the correct color in hand I re-sprayed my now "totally finished" bomber. I really like the new color, however in the photos I took it ended up looking a lot more grey than blue. In person it looks a lot bluer. Also if you look at my cut away bomber that color looks a lot better in the pictures and it's the same paint. So on with the pictures.
