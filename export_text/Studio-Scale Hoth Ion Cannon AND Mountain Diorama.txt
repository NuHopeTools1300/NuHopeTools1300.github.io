Winter side-project:  Studio-Scale [Hoth] Ion Cannon... AND mountain diorama, for my personal collection (and possibly Wonderfest?).  Got most of the mountain section roughed-out and to fit on a 3'x2' base.  Most of the nooks and crannies are there.  Next:  onto paint, weathering, and snow.  Also need to fabricate the custom plexi-wrap and rear sky.  Built-in a wiring channel and keeping the ball loose, in case I ever get ambitious enough in my old-age to animate a ball-section.  More soon, after some R&R.  Fun to do a diorama, again... been a while.

Hey Scott!  Indeed that is your taun... keeps me company next to me by my desk every day... ;-)  I made the backdrop by cutting and bending (with a simple heat gun) a large piece of .220 acrylic from h depot (since having this done elsewhere plus shipping was quoted insane right now).  Then just simply painted it on a table in my garage with off-the-shelf spray cans... (in an unseasonably cold, damp garage, might I add, lol).

I had my own custom mix I did about 10 years ago for my Davidson taun (yes, I have both... hehe)...  was experimenting with another model railroading one that I have been wanting to try... need to figure out this week what I am going to go with.  Had recently looked at AK, too.  Some of the smoother snow parts I may leave as is, as they came out pretty nicely as-is... we'll see soon.
