G'day Sean,

Well mate the first Viper was my second time at trying weathering so that sorta tell's ya i've been playing it by ear so ta speak.

I tried some on my RM viper for the first time before i tried it on the SS one.

So all the weathering techniques have been new to me.

I prefer to use acrylics for my painting although both Vipers where done in enamels.

All i did was apply an acrylic wash and then the rest is all pastels.

The paint chip's effects on first Viper i scratched the paint off after spraying and letting dry for a bit where as the second i used salt for the chip effect after reading about it not that long ago.

Thanks again everyone for the more than kind words.

Oh and again,for letting me in to play with you awesome guy's as well.
