I've had a lot of questions about how the 'unveiling' of the 66" Enterprise went at the StarFest Con in Denver last weekend.

I've posted a page here:

http://squaremodels.com/starfest2006.htm

George Takacs (SaintG from Timeslip Creations), Ryan Comer (Belleauwood, resinhead) and Steve Neisen (SWPropmaker of Nice'n Models) were all on hand to give me a TON of help to unload and load the big beast. We were also fortunate enough to have George Takei (Sulu) come into the room, and we had the room locked so we could have some private time with him. He told us some great stories, and took some photo poses with the Big Enterprise.  Thanks to everyone who came out to see the ship, and to the guys above for their help..   We'll be bringing her out to Wonderfest, so try and get out there.

ALSO, on the website, I updated the VIPER MKII model kit with more pics of the weathering, and also put up a new page for the BSG Emblem Plaques/Model Bases, our first product offering from SquareModels.

See the home page for links to those pages: http://squaremodels.com

Enjoy the updates.

-Sean
