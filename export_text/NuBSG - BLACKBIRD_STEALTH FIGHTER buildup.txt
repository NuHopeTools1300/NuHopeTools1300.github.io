After working on this for a while, she's finally done! 

Full BuildUp review :http://squaremodels.com/stealth.htm

A couple pics below.

[IMAGE: bb20a.jpg]

[IMAGE: ckpt3a.jpg]

This was such an incredibly nice kit to build up. I didn't like the design at first, but after having this kit in hand and putting it together, I actually like it a lot. I've added some new details, like photo-etch seatbelts, a throttle and joystick, and some extra greeblies in the engine area. 

Overall, it came out very nicely. Sitting by itself, or along with the other 1/24 scale kits, it's just gorgeous.

Kip Hart (Wolvster) deserves major kudo's for creating such a beautiful ship from scratch!!! Thanks for the opportunity to build this baby Kip!!

-Sean
