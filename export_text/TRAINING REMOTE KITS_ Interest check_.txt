To go with the release of Frank's new kit... you'll have a 'building your remote to match the original' build-up thread I'll be doing.

As soon as my panther arrives in the mail and I've got all my stuff together I'll make a new thread.

FYI Training remote colors are:

Freight Car Brown...

hardly red at all.

Grime... 

a nice off white which seems to match the real remote pre-washed/weathered.

These are old FLOQUIL brand railroad paints, and I've read thats what the original model makers used on many of the studio builds. Not neccessarily this brand but the brown is definitely a match. Further verification on brown instead of red was matched to the original studio scale falcon... training remote color is not the red seen on panel details but the brown instead.

Stay tuned I guess... you'll at least get some great photographic reference.
