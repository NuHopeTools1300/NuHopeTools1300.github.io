This is an idealized but pretty dang accurate replica of the fleet ship at the end of ESB that appears as a blob under the Rebel Transport. The real model is pretty slapped together (and has a Vosper crew man glued to the side that I just could not bring myself to add, lol)... so I took a few liberties, puttying some areas and tweaking a few details for the sake of keeping it neat and tidy. I assume it flies in this position, but appears in the film flying in a completely different direction to how the engine is located, lol. Who cares, right? It was a blob on screen.

My references on the Fleet Ships are scant and few and they are very private - sorry in advance that I cannot share.  

I'm mostly done - I will add some finishing touches, give it a nice paint job, and debut it at Wonderfest at the end of the month! More pics then!
