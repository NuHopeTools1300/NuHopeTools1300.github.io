Hi guys,

Here are a couple of pics of an 18"ish solid resin casting of the Predator drop pod from AVP.

The original studio model was made here in the UK and was sent to Prague for shooting, where it fell into yet more silicone.

It is a four piece kit: the main pod and 3 air brakes.

Apologies for the cack photos, I'll get some better ones soon, once the 3rd air brake has been cast and the thing is in primer so you can properly see the textures.

Edit: BTW, the original paint job was achieved by laying down a silver basecoat, the weathering with Zebo (graphite grate polish) and then buffed.

Drop me a PM if you would like more info on this new addition to my collection ;-)
