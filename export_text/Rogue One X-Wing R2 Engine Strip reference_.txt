The Rogue 1 ship was based off the ANH X-wings.  I would bet a significant amount of money that the R2 Strip in that movie looked almost identical to the ones in ANH.  That being said I "think" the blue squadron X-wings were CGI in that movie so probably any reference from the ANH Special editions would be darn close.  the pic above is from the full sized prop built specifically for that premier - and its not all that accurate to what is on screen.
