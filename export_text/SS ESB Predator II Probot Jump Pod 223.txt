Got to work on the 'directional flaps'. The trickiest part was trying to figure out where the 8rad support sits and its angle to hold 223 (B19) flap. I decided to put these parts together separate from the pod so when it came time to put them on, I could make each one match the other. Not sure if it was like this on the original, but I put the flak38 part upside down to give a better gluing surface to the 8rad, as well as allow the plastruct piece to slide in. Fits pretty good, and I like how it looks. Now I just need to do this three more times.

Re: SS ESB Predator II Probot Jump Pod

The plastruct parts that I used are the LF-416 Lamp shade with the LF-116 Incandescent placed in it. The Elliptical Domes are VHE-48. As for the little green part that I put in/on top of the flak38 part is plastruct LF-3 Incandescent with shade.

The flat tan part is from the Tamiya Panzer Ausf. M/N. You'll need two of them.
