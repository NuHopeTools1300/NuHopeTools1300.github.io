Post #43  (forum post #2802759)
Author    : RickHamel
Date      : Mar 15, 2013 at 6:56 PM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

I bought an anti static gun--hooks to air and a 5000 volt source to produce ions to cancel out the inherent charge built up in fiberglass and plastic parts--keeps all the goobers in the air from sucking into your fresh paint--also a pic of the Futaba touch screen 14 channel radio that controls this machine---when you turn it on it loads Windows-houses dual processors-I'm not kidding! Amazing technology-unlike the old systems on 72mh this is 2.4 giga hertz and the transmissions are all digitally encoded! With the size of the craft and speed a good radio link is a necessity
