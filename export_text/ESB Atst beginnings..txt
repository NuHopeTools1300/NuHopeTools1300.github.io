Yes its another ESB Atst in the works and the previous atst threads have been incredibly helpful!!!  Kudos to those whom came before me and allowed me to leach of their work:angel! 

So I am getting in on this waaay late compaired to some of the others on the board.  I still need to track down a handfull or kits (AMT C600, Vosper, Entex Porsche, Brabham, Messerschmitt).  If i do not find a resin copy, I will probably scratch the entex part or find something similar as that one is beyond my reach financially.

Legs:

I wanted my atst posable so I built one leg from layered sheets of plastic and sanded smooth for good measure so that it was solid and strong enough to hold itself upright.  After realizing how difficult building one single leg was, i decided to mold it in 4 parts and poured a nice resin copy for an instant set of atst legs!!!  Hopefully the resin allows some flex and do not break when i add the adjustment screws.  If not i guess i can make some more...

Any comments or help avoiding pitfalls would be appreciated.
