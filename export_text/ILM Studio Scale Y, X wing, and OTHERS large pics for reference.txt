The Kindle edition (of "The Making of Star Wars") doesn't have pages, but refers instead to "locations." There is an entire section titled "Star Destroyer" (just after "VistaVision Strikes Back" and just before "Y-Wing) that has several great photos and one drawing by Steve Gawley. It starts at location 2656 of 9536.

For the Sandcrawler, the best photos are in the section titled "The Fox Preview" (just after "Escape from the Death Star" and just before "Color Relations"). It starts at location 7000 of 9536.
