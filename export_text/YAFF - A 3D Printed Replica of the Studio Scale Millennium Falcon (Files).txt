As many of you are aware I am going through my mid-life crisis by Detailed Modelling and Printing the Studio Scale Falcon.

Given the amount of assistance and general goodwill from within the Community - I am putting the files out for no charge to anyone who wants to give this a go as well. - I started this new thread to mainly just focus on the printing side of things, and for me to post updates as models are brought online.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT NOTE

To stop people just ripping off these files - and to make it easier for me to notify users of changes, updates etc, I am stopping all general public access sharing soon. 

They are still free for anyone who wants to print them, you will just need to PM me your Email Address for access, and I will add you to my Falcon Mailing list, as well as send you an invite to the files. 

So to pre-empt the switch over, feel free to flick me requests for access now.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is still a long way off completion, but we do have quite a few printed cockpits already out in the wild - and a lot more parts coming online every few weeks.

The documentation is still a bit of a WIP, so flick me a message if there are any questions - documents will update over time.
