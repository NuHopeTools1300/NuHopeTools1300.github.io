Post #2  (forum post #1883744)
Author    : REL
Date      : Sep 12, 2011 at 2:01 PM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

According to Rick Sternbach the studio model was 5 feet long, 1/226 scale.

Voyager is 344 meters long, so at 3 feet your model would be 1/376 scale.
