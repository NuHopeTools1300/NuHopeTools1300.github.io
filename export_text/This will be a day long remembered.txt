Amazingly enough, for the ANH Ties try Wal Marts house brand colorplace grey primer as a base and for the darker details try Krylons equiptment Grey primer.

  The Solar panels with real coolshade will be fine in the factory color, it has a glossy, semi gloss and flat reflection naturally when left that way, as per the originals.  As for the cast wings, semigloss is the BEST comprimise IMHO.
