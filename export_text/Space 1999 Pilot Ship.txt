Finally finished my acurate Studio Scale 1/24th Pilot ship.

From the Space 1999 second season 2-parter "Bringers of Wonder".  A quirky little thing that really doesn't make any sense (in the episode) 
		
		
	

	

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/1f603-png.895371/]

:-D

Very easy build and a fairly straight forward paint job.  A few bumps along the way, but happy to have a 'quick kit build' for once!

Original built by Martin Bower, kit pattern by Jim Small, Engines from Mike Reader, kit resurrected by Richard Lamer, very clean, and hollow, castings by George Takacs (Timeslip), decals (and now full kits) from Alex Jackson (Century Castings).
