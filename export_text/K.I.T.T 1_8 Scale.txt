Hey guys, 

This is a project i have wanted to take on for a long long time. Jack Sessums built models of K.I.T.T which were used in the Knight Rider production the donor kit is the monogram 1/8 Iroc Camaro. The kit is exceptionally rare in the UK so I took the chance on an already built up one. Shown with my Kenner Knight Rider voice car (Hoff signed) for scale.

There's many examples of miniatures used in the show.  One such example is the climatic mid-air showdown of KITT and KARR in "KITT vs. KARR" (the shot of them in mid-Turbo Boost, colliding and KARR exploding is all miniature, as was destroyed overpass seen in the scene prior to it and in the background of it).  Another scene I can think of is the shot of KITT getting plowed into by Goliath in "Goliath", and even more extreme stunts, such as KITT's jump from the parking structure into the apartment building (and back) in "KITT The Cat."  In fact, most of the even more extreme Turbo Boost shots and stunts were performed using model miniatures.  There's quite a lot of them in the show throughout the four seasons.
