Some early version Sherman tanks had 5 spoked wheels, but at 1/35 they would probably be at the same size as the M5 Stuart. I don't think that particular Sherman was made in a larger scale. You may be able to get custom replacement road wheels from an RC tank manufacturer. 1/16 may work.  But if you can find a 3D designed road wheel STL you could enlarge it to the size you need.
