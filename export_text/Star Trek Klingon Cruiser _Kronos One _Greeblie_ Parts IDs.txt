This one has me curious. It was placed on K'T'Inga sometime during the making of Star Trek: The Motion Picture (1979). The part is wedge-shaped with a pin sticking out one side. As seen here, it's about 1/4-inch long from front to back. There are many copies of this part all over the model so I'm guessing there were multiples of it in the donor kit.
