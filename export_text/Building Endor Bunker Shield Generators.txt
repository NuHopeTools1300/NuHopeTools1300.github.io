Quick update...

Scribed some lines (first time), added the little styrene bits, and bondo to smooth out the top (Still needs sanding). I did manage to drop the top section when moving it around and had to re-glue the Saturn parts back on. 

I tried gluing the 9 Dixie cups together, but the plastic is so flex that it would just break the glue. I tried Super glue and hot glue, no dice on either. So I ended up using Bondo to stick them together. Makes it a lot heavier but it seems to have done the trick, for now anyways. 

Wanted to see what it looked like primed =)
