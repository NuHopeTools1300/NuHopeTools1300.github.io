The top railing is 2mm thick on the studio models.. the hoses can be made by wrapping an extremely thin guage wire (we're talking a half a millimeter or less) around wire that's 3mm. Bass guitar strings can also be used, but I'm not sure of the source of the springs.. just as long as they're 3mm. I have some good views of the back side I can send but I'd have to clear it with Tox first for any side views as they're his property. I have some of my own though. Send me a pm
