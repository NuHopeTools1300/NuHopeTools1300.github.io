Extreme weight?  I found it to be pretty light, if a little nose heavy.  But it "almost" balances out when all the engines and such are attached.  Its heavier than styrene surely, but its not a crazy 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]

Here's what I did...  sorry no pics...

I drilled a 3/8 hole in the bottom - pic your favorite spot.  glued a nut on the inside with JB weld  yo uprobably want to use a wide flat washer as well to spread out the area.  Got a rod that was 3/8 and cut threads on it that matched the nut using a tap and die set. and then just screwed it in.
