Hi, Not sure what you mean by the "comb " parts, but pretty much all the pale grey parts on the bomb and cockpit module are from the 1/72nd Hasagawa Leopold Rail Gun.

The other parts on the bomb module:- Dark green ribbed parts are from the Tamiya 1/35th M3 Lee tank kit No. 35039, and the dark grey square parts with the tiny indentations and small cutout, are from the Tamiya 1/35th 88mm GunFlak36/37 kit No.35017
