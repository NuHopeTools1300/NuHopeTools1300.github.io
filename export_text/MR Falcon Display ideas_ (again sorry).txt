I used the "Better Homes" black 3 shelf bookcases from Walmart.  I put 2 together assembled as 1.  It looks great and fits the Falcon base perfectly.  I plan on adding a glass door to keep my son out and displaying my SS X-wing and Tie on the shelves below with LED spotlights on them.  Stupid Walmart doesn't have them on their website anymore but I just bought them in store around Thanksgiving.  They were about $60 each and $6 back if you didn't want the "Better Homes" subscription.  I will try to get some pics up soon.  I looked all over and this is the best option I came up with...
