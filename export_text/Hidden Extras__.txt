The fantail of the 6' Enterprise D model integrated the word 'ugly' amongst the panels.  This 'feature' was not replicated on the 4' model and was likely erradicated when the 6' was made over for "Generations."

Since the 6' model was only used for the first 2 1/2 seasons, I doubt there is a shot of it.

Ironically, the drawings that were included in the instructions of the ERTL model kit did replicate the letter 'G' from the word 'ugly'.  So I guessed they did base those things on the real model.
