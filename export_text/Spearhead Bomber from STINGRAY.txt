Spearhead Bomber from STINGRAY.  Quick and simple Studio Scale build, basically just need an Aurora B-58 (the bigger one).  You could of course use any B-58 kit. Added some small extra details/decals as it looked a little empty, but kept them in the 'style'. Wasn't sure exactly how it was weathered but it seemed simple from the screen caps, so I stayed with that. BTW if anyone makes one, add nose weight!!  I didn't
