OK to be clear..the base files for this model belong to Guy Cowan. The Scans I have, that he generously shared with me to help me build my 1/6 replica took him years of research and a considerable amount of money to acquire. I want to openly apologise to him, unreservedly, for any misunderstanding or flak he may have received because of this thread or any confusion I may have caused. My intention was always to liaise with Guy as the model progressed to seek his approval on my further use of the files and subsequent re-modelling and detailing...to be honest I wasn't even sure I was up to the task of modelling this, and wanted to at least work it up into a reasonable facsimile.

The last thing I wanted was to upset him...I know he has been treated very badly before, by other members of the prop community, and I am determined not to add to his woes...I consider him a friend and a mentor to the maker community and am in talks with him regarding the progress of this project

I am guilty of betraying his trust and possibly got a bit carried away with the subject matter and for that I am truly sorry
