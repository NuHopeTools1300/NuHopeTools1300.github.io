Been a while anything new? 

Also, Frank and Moe, would you guys consider also doing the new "Super Viper" that was shown in the previous episode? I call the MK VII seen in "Maelstrom" a Super Viper  because differences between it and the previous MkVII CG model are like the differences between a Legacy F/A-18c Hornet and the F/A-18e Super Hornet.

If anyone hasn't seen it or didn't notice the Maelsrtom "Super Viper" had new/redisigned intakes....the intakes next to the canopy are now more angular and pronounced and they taper back along the engine housing in a more angular shape. There are now new intakes below the wings that appear to connect to the reshaped upper intakes. The wing LERX are now shaped much like the Super Hornets, they now have a nice curve and don't go as far forward. Lastly, the whole nose is completely reshaped, it's more like  a MK II's now...wider and more angular and less pointy like the MK VII's usually have.

Hi ya Frank, the previous page has some pictures of the New MK VII. The differences may require a whole new build, but you guys are the experts.

The differences that I can see are:

1. A whole new nose....it's wider and more angular

2. New lower intakes

3. The side intakes next to the canopy are more pronounced and angular and seem to have a different shape as they taper back over the engines.

4. The wing LERX's are shorter and more rounded

5. The wing guns now sit on their "sides" compaired to the original MK VII's

6. Different paneling. In the finale when Apollo breaks off to investigate the "bogey" you can get a good look at the new paneling....more angles like what an F-22 has.

7. Not sure but the canopy "seems" to be bigger and more rounded....but I'm not 100% sure about that.
