Yah... what HE said.

The only thing that the Unobtainium and MR Enterprises have in common...is ME. :lol

They are completely different designs, using totally different reference. The Unob E is a "best guess". The MR E is created from original patterns and dims pulled directly off the 11 footer.

Sorry about the Unobtainium model. I got out looong before that trainwreck started. But I definitely saw it comin' down the track... 

Unfortunately, the deformation and sagging and stickiness and decal popping will never stop. Ever.

I owned one for a while, but sold it off before it got any worse. One of my customers actually wanted to own one, despite all of the obvious problems. I was happy to oblige.
