On a Studio Scale Galactica there are about 3000 kit parts. I think at this point about 99% of the parts have been successfully ID'd by Sean Corsini, Sean Sides, Jim Creveling, Charles and other dedicated builders. I helped track down about 3-4 myself during our builds over at Custom Replicas, and passed along the information (not much out of 3000!)

 There has always been a couple of parts, which to my knowledge, have eluded ID. Mostly because good photos simply don't exist, making IDing difficult. I happened to find a part i have been looking for about 5 years and wanted to share info.

Bottom of the Galactica, Neck Section (Pyramid) Corsini ID numbers 124,125,126,127 (turns out to be 2 parts not 4)

1/35 Italeri Hetzer Gun halves.
