Well...after doing 3 paint masters of the MR Falcon I got to do mine.

NOTE.........................

I made cockpit modifications as well as lighting and the stand. The paint job is exactly as the MR paint masters but understand the FACTORY paint job is a FACTORY paint job and looks AMAZING.... But it might differ from my own and the MR paint masters...Hope you all understand the difference.

I HIGHY ENDORSE THE MR FACTORY PAINT JOB....

As part of my payment...I was shipped a MR Falcon kit in sorts...Some of it was assembled but much of it was not...those who complain about the price have NO idea of what is involved in this bird...

I assembled it, added my OWN lights and customized the cockpit. I also made the base...it will have mirrors soon. I wanted the Falcon to fly high on the base

I custom sculpted the Chewie and Han figures as well as made the back wall with the lights. 

although the Falcon looks white...it has a slight yellow tint to it as does the Screen used bird...when photographed it looks white as does the screen used....we were trying to achieve that..
