I'm sure you'll agree it is a really exciting time right now for studio scale models. With all thats currently available or on the horizon, I've been 'forced' to get my act together and complete a bunch of projects that have been sitting around in various states, some for a few years now.

32" scratchbuilt Falcon. I've still got some final things to do - some little missing details and the lights, but she's almost done. The cockpit interior and dish are castings from elsewhere, F&M I believe. This has been great fun to build in my spare time at university. I'd guess its about 8 weeks work spread over two years. Grass not to scale.

Studio scale Probe Droid. 99% complete. The lighting has washed some of the weathering away. Awesome kit. Great fun to dirty down.
