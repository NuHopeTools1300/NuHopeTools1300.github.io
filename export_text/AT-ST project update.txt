Here is an update on my AT-ST project. I completed the underside of the head and connecting all the hoses. In a way it is a good thing that the reference picts don't show some things. It allows for one to do some creative kit bashing. For the panels I used the side skirt fenders from the Gepard. For the hoses I tried to recreate what I thought I saw in the ref picts. I don't have the Vosper parts for the legs yet but I think I am going to go ahead and paint and at some latter point if I get the parts it won't be a big deal to put them on.

Scott
