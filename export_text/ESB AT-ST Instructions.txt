Flint, it took me all of 1 minute to bend to shape with a pair of special tools I've had all my life, my hands and fingers.

All I did was estimate the length between the two bends, marked them with a pencil and just bent them to the desired angle. I then trimmed the piece to length, glue in place and WHALLA!

The styrene plastic is pretty thin and pliable so I wouldn't use any intense heat source as this may distort the plastic. Maybe hot water would help though.
