Hi guys,

There seems to be quite an interest by members who are currently building Tie Bombers, and those that wish to in the future.

I'm going to offer and see what the interest is in a basic parts set for people to build their own but have some of the basic parts machined for them.

The set would contain the central tube with a diameter of 19.1mm (cut to correct length and machined on the ends), the 2 body tubes (cut to correct length and machined on the ends and with the holes machined through for the central tube and also the grooves on both tubes machined in as well, the centre box that sits between the 2 body tubes (machined acrylic with the centre hole machined through and a mounting point hole-1/4" or 6.35mm in metric put in underneath to fit panavise thread mounts), and also the set would contain the internal wing hub parts (those pesky pieces made up of 4 round parts) also machined!.

Body tubes and central tube are Plastruct items that I will have on hand anyways and the other parts are acrylic.

This will give, I feel, builders a good start on their builds and take away some of the hard work in scratching this prop!.

I will as well offer any parts needed individually to members and don't feel you have to purchase an entire set!.

PM me if interested

Re: SS Tie Bomber Machined parts (pics added of assembly line)

Just a quick update guys!.

The bottom acrylic blocks that were made I have decided are not up to scratch and will be replaced with laminated styrene. Reason being that the acrylic sheet I was supplied with is crap with air bubbles present which means that when machined the edges are ragged and chipped. I myself wouldn't use them for a build and I don't expect you guys to either so hence the re-doing of these.

The centre blocks are perfect as these came from thick block sheet and the wing hubs are perfect too as these were turned rather than machined. Absolutely sharp tools were used too, but the acrylic  doesn't lend itself well to machining of smaller parts. Machined styrene comes out awesome!.

This will mean a delay in getting the parts out to you all, but I don't want to give you crap parts, so bear with me till I machine these new parts and I'll contact you all when ready to ship.

Sorry again for the delay, but hey these sets will not dissapoint.

Cheers

Hey guys,

For those that have or will be getting the set please note that I have put an 'F' on the centre block meaning 'front'. Both ends may look the same but underneath, the small block is placed slightly forward or back, I can't remember exactly which but your'e fine if the 'F' is on the front. This detail shows up in my ref so I went for it in my build and everything worked out ok with all parts fitting perfectly, as it will for you guys too.
