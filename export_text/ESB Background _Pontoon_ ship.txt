Hello All,

Here is my attempt at the ESB background ship that looks like it had the LR232 on the back.  I had a failed hull assembly from mine and decided to put it to good use.  Aside from the screenshots, I couldn't find any reference.  This is the best attempt with the lack of reference using donor parts from the same era as ESB.  It is built on a wood frame.  The pontoons are plastic cocktail cups joined with 3" PVC.  The completed model is about 24" long.  

Thanks!
