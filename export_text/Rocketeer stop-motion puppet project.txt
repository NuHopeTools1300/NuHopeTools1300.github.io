Here is one I don't think I have seen done before that I decided to do, that is a recreation of the ILM puppet made for the Rocketeer. In doing research for this the only helpful info I found was in Cinifex #48 that gives a bit of info about the making of the puppet. Most importantly that it is 18 inches tall. Also on Youtube I found a behind the scenes clip that described how the cloths were made. I do have a couple of pictures of the puppet and one with the armature that was real nice. But if any one out there has any pictures of the puppet or any production picts of the puppet being made if you could post them that would be great. I already got started by making the armature and getting the clay on.
