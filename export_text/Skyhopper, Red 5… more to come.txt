(Wanted to make sure this was show-able before I show it, as it’s seismic). Okay, so did you know that the missing shells from the Hero Luke Red 5 X-Wing miniature still existed as intact halves when they were, for whatever reason, separated from the wings/armature/cockpit? I have seen photos and those are what I used as a guide to scribe panel lines and add the chip panels. 

Would it blow your mind to learn that castings were made at some point? Or that uhhhh they worked their way to some deeply earnest guy in Baltimore? The best thing is how close I got with this replica build just from pics. Comparing it to a casting off the real deal is indescribably gratifying. Got a couple small things wrong, but not too shabby! More importantly is how it once again confirms the hull dimensions that Josh Maruska and André Bustanoby concluded were “right”.

Wow, this is nuts. Bottom mount is a post film replacement and the bottom’s nose seems longer but it would slightly socket into the nose mount plug thing.
