Hello everyone,

Here's my build up of the awesome Salzo kit.  This is such a cool design from the original show episode "The Long Patrol", and after some of the recent posts about this model, I had to get mine together. It's one of those rare SS kits that go together very well, and very quickly. Turned this one out over 2 weekends, and has joined the rest of the BSG SS fleet.  I dirtied it up to resemble the filming miniature using some oils and pastels.  Only scratch addition was the engine inserts. I closed up the cockpit since I didn't have an interior (plus I think the black looks cool).

For a bit of history on this kit (and Mike's gorgeous build up),  you can check this out.

http://www.therpf.com/f10/tos-bsg-studio-scale-antique-viper-94363/

Thanks,

-Sean
