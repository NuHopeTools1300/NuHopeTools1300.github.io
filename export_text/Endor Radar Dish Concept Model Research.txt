I concur with Jedi Dade on the top picture.  If you look around and find some pics of the parts from the old "Visible V8" model kit, you will see those parts.  Specifically, the valves and the spark plug connectors.  I had this kit and remember them. IIRC, ILM used this kit for a lot of details, including the half pistons for detail on the front of Hans blaster.

As for second pic, not too sure.  The circle piece looks like some sort of "twist off" locking connector of sorts.  You can see the tabs and slots on the edge where it would mate up with the other half.  I do however have a drawer full of the pushpins if you need them.
