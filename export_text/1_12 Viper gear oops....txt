So,

Spent the weekend scratch building a landing gear for another TOS 1/12 (HUGE) Viper.   I had been playing with the scale and printing them out.  Accidently grabbed the smaller prints (about 80% to scale), and merrily built all three gear.  Then, before painting, I put them up to the other built big Viper to check scale, and.... OOOOOPS, I saw it was wayyyyy too small!!   So, I guess these will just go on the side as a set of gear with no Viper, and I'm restarting the build process to the correct scale.....  

The good news is, I learned a lot building them, so I have some new ideas that will make this second round look better  (Including real rubber wheels, which are some nice grommets that fit the bill nicely!)

Back to the bench...

-Sean
