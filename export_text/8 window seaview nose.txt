Here is a pic of me holding a Fiberglass reproduction of the Eight Foot Seaview's nose. It is a direct copy of the  actual Eight footer's. However it was returned back from two window to four window configuration. The lines on the Manta fins were just that..Lines. No functionality at all. Just a relief to suggest something else...I also have Pics with the the Launching Flying Sub as well.

Note..the miniature was very crude, and would not pass today's rivit counting modeler...it was made to look good on screen. Period.

...To make the TV Four Window version,They originally took a fiberglass casting off of the old nose and added the new sonar domes etc. Yes, there is a seam between the decks. That whole top are area was removable to gain access to the interior nose lights.
