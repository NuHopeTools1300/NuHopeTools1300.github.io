Okay, top box - glue the top and sides together as shown. Leave off the back. Wasili recommends you not glue the base "frame" at this point but I didn't know that at the time, but I think it's okay? As you can see, I shored the seams up from the inside with Apoxie Sculpt, Probably not necessary, but I am a paranoid fella. You can practically sit on it now!

[IMAGE: L1130645.jpg]

[IMAGE: L1130646.jpg]

[IMAGE: L1130647.jpg]

[IMAGE: L1130648.jpg]

The next day, glue in the side gun well strips as shown - there will be only one way they fit, as both sides are not the same dimensions. So dry fit and pay attention! Again, I shored it up with Apoxie Sculpt. Let this all dry/cure over night before the next step....

[IMAGE: L1130649.jpg]

[IMAGE: L1130650.jpg]

[IMAGE: L1130651.jpg]

However,  it seems most of the post have become argumentative at best and I feel like it is alienating the people who are here for the more "fun" aspects of the hobby.   I understand the drives to be constantly improving the way that we do things and feedback is an important part of that,  but i feel like it has just become overall mean spirited in many places here.   Good people leave or are silent when a place becomes to hostile.

I really do want all my customers to be happy,  i know that is an unrealistic expectation.  I am not Apple,  like so many cottage industries i am a 1 man operation.  R&D,  operations,  manufacturing,  shipping,  accounting, just absolutely everything.    I have worked very hard to avoid the pitfalls of the garage kit industry but i am not immune to mistakes and oversights.  Even Apple does not have a 100% customer satisfaction so there is no way that i can make everyone happy as much as i would like to.

I will continue to try to offer unique projects for those who are so inclined to purchase them.  If someone is disappointed or it does not meet their standards I can respect that.  I just ask that if you do not want something from me or anyone else here,  at least respect that some people do and are happy with that.
