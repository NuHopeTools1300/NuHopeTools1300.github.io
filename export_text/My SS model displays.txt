I used 1/2 galvinzed pipe, pipe fittings, and floor flange to display the ships in flight.

I wanted the movie posters and pictures of the ships in their real environments to go along with them.

 I think it gives them more meanig to the veiwers then just sticking them on a self and guessing what they are.
