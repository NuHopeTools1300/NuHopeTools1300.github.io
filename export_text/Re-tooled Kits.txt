The Smthsonian Saturn V is technically a minor retool. The engine nozzles have longer pegs and one of the two cans we know from the X Wing has extra tabs and very minor mold damage to some surface detail. 

The first two box art versions of the Airfix have the original. All of the subsequent Airfix releases were the slight retool. And only recently they got around to making their kit more accurate. 

Unfortunately for Studio Scalers, it makes our builds less accurate. Anyone ever buy a USAirfix boxed version? I have also read of the Airfix kit popping up in generic bags.
