Hey Jase,

Lorne Peterson reiterated last week that two paper paint buckets filled with foam and clad with styrene were the starting point for the Escape Pod. They did something similar to build out the "hammerhead" cockpit for the Blockade Runner when that ship was converted from the "Pirate Ship."

Now whether that was a 5-quart bucket or another size is up for debate. I don't think Peterson himself ever gave the dimensions of the bucket. I'm pretty confident Rolando ("Echoleader") said he used the 5-quart bucket when he built the pattern that Steve N used as the basis for his kit, but that was years ago.
