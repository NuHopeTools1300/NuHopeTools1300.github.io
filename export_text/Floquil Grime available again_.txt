*Kind of...

Floquil used to sell a three pack of weathering pens that included Grime, Earth, and Mud

[IMAGE: 256744-f2785b86275507287dad0be1d7a3b3ae.jpg]

Testors, the former manufacturers of Floquil, is still selling this same set, rebranded as "CREATEFX"

[IMAGE: 256743-b84a10065fbc6c0ee7ed5ba641f7ebaa.jpg]

I have bought the pens and tested it next to my Floquil grime and I can confirm that it is the same color.

The downside is that you have to buy all three markers together for around $10 and you only get 1/3 of an ounce of enamel paint in each pen.

But it's great to use as a color reference for mixing your own out of, say Tamiya Deck Tan and Flat White - which will get you quite close.

Anyway, they're at  Amazon, Michaels, Hobby Lobby, heck they're probably in your local hobby shop right now.
