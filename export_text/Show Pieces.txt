Hey joberg , I'm not sure what the question is, but investors will want to know "What's in it for me?" and "How fast will I get my ROE". It's all about the money and not the passion. I think your biggest issue is to sell this idea to Howard Johnson. Your work is magnificent, but the hotel will have to invest in a display for your piece in a central, very visible location, and figure if this will attract more visitors to the hotel.

A sci-fi museum would be a good idea, but we had one here in a small town and it failed. Possibly for it being in a small touristy town. I think you have to put those museums in high traffic locatios  as an add-on to an existing venue.

If I was an investor, I'd like a whole model, not just a fragment, and pictures from the movie back-lit behind the model.
