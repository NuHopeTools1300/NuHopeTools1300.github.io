Studio Scale really has nothing to do with scale. It's more just the name of the hobby. Studio scale modeling is trying to match the filming models in size, parts used and to some degree techniques used. There's a lot of controversy and infighting on the subject, especially on how accurate the model needs to be when it comes to shape and hard to find kit bashed parts. But that's just my opinion.
