Thank you! It’s a great entry level build and I learned a lot from it. 

eBay was my go to for kits. I recommend being patient because there are some deals to be had. Check daily for the kits you need. I prefer listings that have a “make offer” options. You’d be surprised what you can that way. Also if you see something you’re interested in, save it and wait. The seller might come to you with an offer. All these little tricks add up to savings when buying multiple kits. 

The other place I’d recommend is model show swap meets. I got two SCREAMING deals a couple of weeks ago on two vintage kits. Plus you get the opportunity to haggle. 

This path is a pricey one but I’ve found a satisfaction that I never had building off the shelf kits. Patience is the key. 

I look forward to following your build.
