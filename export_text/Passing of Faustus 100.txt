I just wanted to take a moment and let everyone know that one of our own members Duane O'Brian (Faustus 100) passed away. Although I did not know him personally, I know many of you did. I told my friend and former RPF member Raymond Perez I would pass this along in his behalf.

"Faustus 100" passed away today. Most of the members there knew him from his 5-foot Falcon build that he was building throughout the years and interacted with other members building their Falcons. His name was Duane O'Brien and he passed away from complications from a stroke that he suffered about a year ago. I just got off the phone with his mother and she confirmed his passing. Duane would have been 58 on this June 22nd the same day as my own birthday and we both lived in Bronx New York we had so much in common. I told his mother of his passion for modeling and I told her I would let the guys on the modeling group know if they could say something and remember him and say a prayer for him. I would also appreciate it on behalf of his family thank you. Ray Perez former RPF Member.
