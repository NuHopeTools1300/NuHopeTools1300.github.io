Some of you have already seen the model and bought the 3D data. A kit is already in the works for those who don't want to or can't print it themselves.

The model is in Studio Scale 1:24.

The model was built by our team member Robert Cowley (Cowley Design), with help from Steve Neisen and John Stuart who provided many of the small greebles.

The render images were made by our render master Izan Ramos.

The award winning "Red Jammer" which was built and painted by Steve Eggers is also very impressive.

Enjoy looking at the pictures.

Greetings

Marcel
