I thought Vader was sporting skinny TIEs by 1980...

As for the studio scale miniature in the first movie, the model builders started with a standard TIE cockpit ball and started building new from the arms out. As an indication of scale, they included a copy of the standard issue ILM pilot, which was also used in the standard TIEs and X-Wings, and was a copy of the Airfix 1/24 Harrier pilot with some Airfix 1/24 Me-109 pilot arms grafted on.

In other words, the original studio scale miniature was intended to be approximately 1/24 scale. I don't know what scale the MPC kit was supposed to be, but I bet if you ask at the Starship Modeler forums someone there will know.
