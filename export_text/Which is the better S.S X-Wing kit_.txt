Author    : mslz22
Date      : Oct 31, 2008 at 4:39 AM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The biggest difference is the fuselage, which Moe did a fantastic job building, it is a little wider and a little deeper but just about the same length, and it has the canopy built into the fuselage.  The wings are also a bit larger, and slightly thicker, modeled after the larger "pyro" wing. The wing mounting blocks are also different, allowing the builder to mount the blocks flush like the pyro's or "floating" to better mimic the sissor mechanisim. There are a couple of other small differences.
