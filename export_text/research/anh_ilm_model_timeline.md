# ANH ILM Model-Specific Timeline Layer

## Scope

- Film scope: `Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope` only.
- Workshop scope: primarily the original `ILM` site in `Van Nuys`.
- Purpose: track `build`, `reference/export`, `miniature photography`, and `pyro/destruction` milestones by model family rather than only by overall shop chronology.

## Working Rule

- This is a companion layer to the master `anh_ilm_timeline`.
- A row is allowed to stay broad if the evidence only supports a `season`, `production phase`, or `program window`.
- I separate `reference export`, `finished filming miniature`, and `destruction stand-in` wherever the evidence suggests those are different milestones.

## Confidence Guide

- `High`: strongly supported by official, auction, or unusually clear period evidence.
- `Medium`: plausible and useful, but still partly reconstructive.
- `Low`: active working hypothesis only.

## Read

- `X-Wing` still has the cleanest build-to-reference-to-hero chain.
- `Y-Wing` now has a usable date structure, but the exact mapping between preserved survivors and filmed callsigns remains less stable.
- `TIE` dates are strongest at the test and pyro ends; the standard grey filming family still needs more exact stage dates.
- `Death Star`, `Blockade Runner`, `Escape Pod`, and `Landspeeder` are real and placeable, but many of their exact miniature-photography dates are still broader than we would like.
