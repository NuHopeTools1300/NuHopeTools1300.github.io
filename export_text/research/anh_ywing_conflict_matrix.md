# ANH Y-Wing Identity Claims Matrix

## Scope

- Film scope: `Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope` only.
- Physical scope: real `ILM` Y-wing miniatures and immediate ANH-era build states.
- Purpose: separate `physical object`, `collector/archive handle`, `screen callsign`, and `screen event`.

## Working Rule

- A `physical object` is the actual miniature or miniature family.
- An `identity label` can be a `screen callsign`, a `collector nickname`, or an `archive / exhibition handle`.
- A `screen event` such as "the first Y-wing Vader destroys" may involve a different physical model than the survivor usually associated with that role.
- This sheet is intentionally designed to stop several partial truths from collapsing into one imaginary super-Y-wing.

## Confidence Guide

- `High`: directly supported by unusually strong archive, auction, or cross-matched local evidence.
- `Medium`: plausible and useful, but still partly inferential.
- `Low`: active working hypothesis only.

## Read This Matrix Carefully

- `YWC-C07` and `YWC-C09` can both be partly true at once:
  - `Gold Leader / TIE Killer` can be the preserved survivor most associated with the first trench-run leader.
  - `Triangles` can still be the physical model that was actually blown up for that leader's death shot.
- `YWC-C08` may also be partly true without fully displacing `YWC-C07`:
  - the Alan Ladd Jr object may have been used in some close-up or formation material,
  - while another object or pyro stood in for destruction.
- The really slippery problem is not one claim in isolation. It is the temptation to assume that `screen callsign`, `close-up hero miniature`, `surviving archive object`, and `destroyed pyro stand-in` must all be the same object.

## Current Working Conclusions

- The strongest hard anchor remains `YWC-O2`: the preserved `Gold Leader / TIE Killer` survivor.
- `YWC-O3`, `YWC-O4`, and `YWC-O6` are where the taxonomy is still unstable.
- I think `YWC-O6` is strong enough to treat as a real candidate identity bucket for `Gold Two / Tiree`, even though I have not yet promoted it into the main fighter lineage table as a closed physical lineage.
- If forced to keep or merge candidates today, I would keep `YWC-O3`, `YWC-O4`, and `YWC-O6` separate until a photo-index pass proves otherwise.
- The cleanest present working split is:
  - `Gold Leader` -> `YWC-O2`
  - `Gold Two / Tiree` -> `YWC-O6`
  - `Triangles` -> `YWC-O5`
  - `Magic of Myth` / `MOM` -> `YWC-O4`
  - `Tiger Sprockets` -> `YWC-O3`
