# ANH Death Star Special Sections

## Scope

- Film scope: `Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope` only.
- Subject scope: Death Star miniature sections that are more specific than generic surface panels.
- This sheet is for the weird and named stuff: `turbolaser towers`, `crane`, `silo/tower`, `gantry`, trench light structures, and the still-open oddball candidates.

## Working Rule

- A `special section` is a Death Star miniature object or subassembly that appears to have a distinct job, geometry, or identity beyond the general surface-panel field.
- Some are securely evidenced physical objects.
- Some are only `candidate sections` pending better photos or archive corroboration.

## Confidence Guide

- `High`: directly supported by auction / official sources or unusually solid reconstruction.
- `Medium`: likely real and distinct, but still partly reconstructive.
- `Low`: plausible candidate only.

## Read

- The `turbolaser towers` are fully secure.
- The `crane`, `silo/towers`, and `gantry` are the next-best special-section targets and worth treating as real parts of the ANH miniature ecosystem.
- The `deflection tower`, `big radar dish`, and `torpedo target` area are still candidate rows, not closed IDs.
