First of all, I'm a huge fan of the show and I love the viper model.

If I had the cash, I'd definitely be purchasing, but there is one thing that bugs me a little about the QMx model.  The renderings were all based on the CGI models and not from the full-size models created for the show.  One thing that I always see when I look at the model is the rescue hatch release on the left side of the viper.  

On the full-size model, that's a chromed release hatch that looks like it is a recessed gas cap.  The QMx model has just a sticker in its place.  Since a lot of the action in the series has the actors walking up the ladder on that side, its a very visible item on the ship.  For me, I'd like that to be a small recessed latch.  I guess I'm asking too much as my impression is that these are to be the same quality as a filming miniature, so a ton of detail likely isn't necessary if you're using motion-control camera for flying sequences.  It still bugs me nonetheless.  

I also appreciate that the Starbuck viper has the DRADIS switch to the eye of Jupiter randomly.  That's a cool effect.  

As for the value of the ship, it's what people are willing to pay for it, and my guess is that they'll all sell out eventually.  I'd love to have one on my desk if it means anything to you.
