If you look at the other Project UFO Models, you will find a lot of these parts. But it is a tricky one for sure.

I think the "Box" in the middle of the front is just scratch built and the square part in the middle is styrene with a few added details. I wonder if that square can actually slide or move, as that is where the robot exits. They did that as a composite in the show, but wouldn't be surprised if there was a design brief that included that feature. Maybe it is removable and covers a deeper opening that was to be revealed? In the episode footage that square "door" is not centered. One reason I think it had the possibility to move/be moved.

Also of note the model continued to be revised after the "good" reference pics were taken. The "corners" on the wider end have added insets in the episode. So all we have are really lousy screen caps. Sigh.
