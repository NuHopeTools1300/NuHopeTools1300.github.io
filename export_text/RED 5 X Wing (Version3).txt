Re: Salzo Red 5 V3 build

I added the recessed step, well Moe started it an i finished it, but some of the bulk added to the canopy frame was so that they did not snap like twigs when getting pulled from the mold, i had nightmares about a big pile of useless fuselages with broken canopies. Also helps to survive shipping....it's a small trade off for having the canopy cast into the fuselage...

mike
