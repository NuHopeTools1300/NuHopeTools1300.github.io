Almost time to call this one finished (I still have a couple of things to noodle!) Red Jammer, the first ILM Y-wing, built in late 1975 and sent to England as reference for the full size set. Never seen on screen, that I know, until a couple of tiny cameos in Rogue One. Built to match the 'half finished' version. Many thanks to those that helped in various ways. Steve Neisen Mike Salzo for making the kit in the first place. Guy Gui Cowen for selling me his, Dave G, Jason Eaton, Lee Ralph, John Simmons for the help. On to the next two
