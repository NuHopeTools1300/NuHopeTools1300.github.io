Well, here's a quick build I'm almost done with. It's a 1/12 scale kit based on the Phil Tippet Maquette Bug for the original Starship Troopers movie. This kit comes courtesy of Steve Neisen. He did the master sculpting himself, and I gotta say, not only is the size of this thing impressive, but the details are fantastic. The legs all have reinforced rods in them (and I'm glad they did for this pose).

I've still got to put on the 'eyes' (I cut off the ones on the kit, want to put on my own shiny orbs) and the little 'antennas' out the rear sides, but I may not get to them for a bit and thought I'd share this. I still need to shoot a clear coat on this and then some dullcoat, and I plan to do a 'sandy' base for it as well down the road.

Questions on the Kit can be sent to: justabug@bresnan.net

-Sean
