Taking on the Mobius Cylon raider. My first attempt at doing lights in a model. I converted the cockpit , purchased the aftermarket cockpit with Cylon pilots. A bit of a pain to cut out the existing inner area and fit this in, but I think it worked. Had to add a few styrene strips to hide the gaps. Having an issue with the lights. I bought the light kit pre wired. The engine lights are NOT working. Found that out AFTER I epoxied them on the clear plate that comes with the kit. The headlights DO work though. Not sure what I'm going to do now. I have no experience with doing lighting work on these.
