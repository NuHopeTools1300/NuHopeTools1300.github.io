I was lucky enough to purchase one of Nice-N/Mike Salzo's Red Jammer kits recently.

I've decided to 'back date' it to it's original state when the original miniature was shipped to the UK to be used for building the Fullsize set piece in early 1976.

Using one of Dave G's engine armatures, an Airfix Saturn V, and Jason's greeble parts it was a simple conversion.

Thanks to the extremely clear pictures in the new Japanese Chronicles book, I'll be able to replicate the original paint job too.
