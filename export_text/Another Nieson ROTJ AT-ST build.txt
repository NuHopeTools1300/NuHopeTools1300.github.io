Hello,

1- there are 2 lips on the ILM models, so dont trim them

2- 1/8" is not 1cm, it's actually around 3mm, and it sounds good to me, I dont have my head close to me but it's something like 2 or 3 mm.

As for the warped head you can use a hairdryer or hot water but you will have to be very carefull. You can also cut a rib and place inside the head to get the proper shape.
