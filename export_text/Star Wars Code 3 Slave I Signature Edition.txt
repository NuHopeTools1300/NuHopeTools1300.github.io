ah- Code 3 pieces... They're fun for sure. 

Thanks for taking the time to take pictures up close and doing the youtube video. 

Just to let you know - the code 3 pieces aren't studio scale.   Studio scale x-wings are 1/24.  Code 3 is more like 1/35. 

The same applies for the rest of the ships too. A real SS slave 1 is like 2.5 feet long. 

see here for pics of a SS slave 1 hull:  

http://www.therpf.com/showthread.php?t=29541&highlight=slave&page=4

But being die cast and being detailed as they are they certainly are in a category of their own. Good find! :thumbsup

One more point of note.  There were several people who have worn the costume in an official capacity such as stunt players and suit models, but only a few had screen roles.  Most notably, Jeremy Bulloch (credited) and John Fass Morton (many of the Cloud City scenes).  The 1997 Special Edition brought Don Bies, Mark Austin, and Nelson Hall, but Bulloch and Morton were the go-to guys back in the day.
