I hope they are there both days!

RE: Blue 1 - yeah, it was re-weathered. They painted over the blues, added one more stripe for Red 2, painted the cockpit canopy, added a decal (or more likely, swapped out laser cannons with one of the original Hero-painted Pyros!!!), added an artoo, broke the canopy bars out (to insert a cockpit and pilot), grafted in thinner weird canopy bars, possibly retrofitted the thing for lighting (because the engine bodies look broken/reglued, and the surgical tubing looks to be aggressively punched into the wing areas), modified the armature (almost crudely at the wing roots), shot some darker grey coloration, some soot, drew weird extra blue/gray lines on the engines - it looked brighter/whiter as Blue 1, and the more familiar putty color as Red 2, at least ten years ago.
