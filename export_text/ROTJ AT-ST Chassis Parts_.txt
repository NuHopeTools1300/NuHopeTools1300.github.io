We're gonna disagree on this one Julien :lol. I'm sure the 312T4 is used twice (both shaved for the block on the bottom), the 312T is just used once (vents on either side).

I tried to orient the 312T blocks for a while, until it hit me that the original picture was the wrong way (flipped horizontally). When I flipped it, the 312T4 blocks lined up perfect, plus.. the squares on the 312T are more of a rectangle shape than the ones on the T4. The seam also matches up. I got all crazy from that one for a while.. the parts on the upper-back of the legs did turn out to be from the T4 too, so two kits works out right for that.
