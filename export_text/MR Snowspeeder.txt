Yes! Out of 4 models or 8 main guns, one was loose (with a little play) and 2 were broken away. One could be re-glued quite well. If you do not know it, you will not notice it. The worst one does need some filling. I will do that, before I repaint the model with red stripes.

I had the impression that when packing the models, it happens from time to time that the protecting paper gets under tension. The resulting force on the main gun can break it loose. According to a retailer, he had quite some complaints about that.

If your laser turrets sits in the correct position when the model is displayed, I would not mind at all.
