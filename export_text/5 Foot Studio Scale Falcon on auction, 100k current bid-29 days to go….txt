This does have a sense of irony. Much earlier—before stumbling upon this auction after visiting a Facebook group about the Millennium Falcon—I was chatting with my friend Murphy on Messenger about offering Sean Sides $100K for his model.

I even messaged Sean on one of his YouTube videos, asking if he would consider selling his 5-foot model and, if so, what price he would have in mind. I specifically mentioned that I was aware he had spent around $20K on kits and materials alone.

Then I come across this—like a slap in the face.

The opening bid is $100K, plus a $25K auction commission, not to mention shipping, taxes to Canada, and insurance. We’re talking about $160K just to get started. We’ll see where the auction ends.

$100K all-in, I can justify. Anything more, I just can’t.

Lovely artwork.
