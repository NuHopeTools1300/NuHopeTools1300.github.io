I am using a Canon EOS Rebel XTi for the photography. It seems to be working out quite well with the detail shots. I really like it! 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
 I usually have the F-stop number set at 29. The shutter stays open for a good 10-15 seconds for me that way to capture the details and depth of field. The lighting is just standard photo flood lamps, but with 500 watt Eiko Supreme photo flood bulbs. The bulbs are colored "blue", thus shining on the models and washing them out a bit as if they were in the film itself. 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]

The screen I bought from EEFX.com. At a very reasonable price for what you get.
