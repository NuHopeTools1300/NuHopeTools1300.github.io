Hey Guys, this is one of those kits I have done 5 or 10 minutes here on there since I got it from Steve at WF back in May....now with 2 kids running me into the ground I have been trying to force myself to work on something for myself at the end of the day after everyone else is asleep, this is what is current on that "after hours" bench....Steve also asked me to post this up hoping it may help anyone else currently working on one...

So far the tricky part has been doing the seam line fill work on the rails that go around the edge of the wings, tricky because if you are not careful you could get filler on the detail parts, I was careful and taped up the detail stuff while i did filler work.

Other than that I have wired up my new favorite LED's, they are 4 chip superflux superbrights from oznium, they act a bit more like regular bulbs rather than most LED's that have that pinpoint thing going on.

I rigged up a mount for the pipe with some scrap and when added to the acrylic armature stuff that Steve includes it will be nice and sturdy when all put together, any warping you see in that body will all get pulled together nicely when the time is right.

I also made myself a lens for the back, which will be molded and cast in clear to help diffuse the light from the LED's...

Hope this helps anyone else out there building, more to come but slowly...

mike
