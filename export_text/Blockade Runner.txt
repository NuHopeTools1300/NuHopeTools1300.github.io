I know that the Landspeeder miniature is mostly pink, it may even be the same shade as the small Blockade Runner model highlights. 

My guess is Joe Johnston either used an off-the-shelf color from another manufacturer, or he just made his own mixture from the Railroad Colors they had on hand, Who knows, maybe a few drops of Reefer White added to some Boxcar Red. The Landspeeder was big enough that he probably used a spray gun to lay down the two main colors, the darker of which also looks an awful lot like Boxcar Red to me.

Either way, if they built the Landspeeder before the small Blockade Runner, there was some ready-to-use pink lying around the model shop when the time came to paint it.
