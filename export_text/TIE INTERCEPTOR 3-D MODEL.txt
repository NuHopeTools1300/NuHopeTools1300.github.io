Well what I mean by brute force is that u have to be really strict with the software...it wants to screw up and get messy. Everything must be checked and constrained to an axis or datum point and almost nothing eyeballed or estimated. A clean, 'hygienic' environment is what u try to maintain so that any anomalies that develop in the workflow can be seen and eliminated.

Here's the download link; https://imperialprinthub.etsy.com/listing/4454750726
