Just finished this one guys. thought id give you all a peek.

the wet transfer decals were a no go. disolved onto nothing but cracks and smears when i put them in the water so i ended painting the stripes. 

good thing though cause i feel the paint looks much better that the decals would have.

one bad thing about it is the resin is starting to leach in a few places. Very disappointing to say the least.

This osne is a studio scale model. very big and very nice. was fun to paint this one.
