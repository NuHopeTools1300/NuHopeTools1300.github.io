When taking these photos, and knowing the issue with the TIE fighters color, I experimented with the "Blue Lighting" to see if I could get them to show as the same colors seen in ANH. Not surprising, the farther away the lighting, the more "blue" the TIE's retained. When I applied the "Direct" "Blue Lighting", the TIES washed out and looked like the more "grey" color from ANH. At my limited skill level, I will never really be able to capture the look the pros did,.. but I understand it a bit more now!
