You could get a pin vise from your local model shop and a small drill bit, and drill a small hole in the top of the cockpit and the bottom of the gun, not too deep to go right through though... insert plastic rod of the same diameter as the drill bit, and you'll have a rotatable y wing gun...easy peasy,,

As for the marks on the turret, they could be turned into blast marks with some dark grey almost black streaks of paint...

Or,,the grey on the turret might be difficult to match perfectly, so you could buy a rattle can of a similar grey, mask off the damaged panels and spray em. There's a few differing shades of grey on most Star Wars models to give some contrast to the panels on them. If you mask up a few more and repaint them too, the new ones will blend in...

Does this help ..?

Cheers

Phil
