Hi everyone !!

With all the seeming interest lately in the new "V" TV series, and guys showing

off their classic miniseries models, I thought I'd post some of my work here.

A few of you may know I've been working on the original "V" miniseries shuttle

for some time now. I had a casting of one of the larger studio models and decided

to fabricate some accurate landing gear to go with it. I also designed a set of decals, since the studio casting didn't come with any.

[IMAGE: Gear5.jpg]

I've also finished off a scratch-built passenger module for the model, which will lock into the front and back model halves with metal rods.

http://www.smallartworks.ca/pix/Vparts1.jpg

http://www.smallartworks.ca/pix/Vparts2.jpg

http://www.smallartworks.ca/pix/Vparts3.jpg

These were made using measurements taken from the actual studio model, supplied to me by the owner of an original. The parts are very accurate. I desihgned them to be able to be made into any version seen in the show, from the three-segmented "squad vehicle" to the strech limo troop carrier version.

I've got a number of other pics of the shuttle on my site, and hope you

enjoy having a look at them.

http://www.smallartworks.ca/Gallery/Skyfighter/Skyfighter.html

Any comments or thoughts on this model ?

Jim
