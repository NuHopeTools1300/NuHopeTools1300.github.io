As I've recently discovered, the detailed panel that goes behind the cockpit canopy frame (I call it the "modesty panel") looks like this only in models built for Return of the Jedi. It appears they were cast as one piece for those models. In ANH, it was done as three separate pieces where the sides angled back. You can see this accurately recreated in the new AMT 1/32 TIE Fighter plastic kit.
