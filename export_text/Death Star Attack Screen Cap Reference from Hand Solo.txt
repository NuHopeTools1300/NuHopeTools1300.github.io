This could really go under Costuming and General Modeling forums as well as this one,.. but this reference provided by Hand Solo  (Bob) shows some extremely interesting things of the Studio Models and Pyros involved in this great scene.

Bob uploaded these to me to show reference of some items that are commissioned from me at this time,.. and I was impressed with the work and detail he pulled from the scenes. 

X-wings, Y-wings, ... anything that was involved in the attack on the Death Star from Ep IV, as captured from the Special Edition DVD. True,.. some ships may be digital,.. but some aren't as well,.. and they are shown in detail too. May things surprised me as they were never noticed from the speed at which the scenes happened. Frame by frame sheds new light on many items.

So,.. here are 816 hi-res bitmap images starting in the Ep IV pilot briefing room and ending with the destruction of the Deathstar. 

The file started at 2.10 GB of data, but compressed to 733 MB in the zip file here: 

http://www.kuhnglobal.net/RED2/Bob-DeathSt...-Screencaps.zip 

I uploaded the massive zip file to another Kuhn Global server at about 6 Mbps and it took 19 minutes 20 seconds to do so. So,.. you do the math according to your download connection speed as to how fast you will pull this file.  

REFERENCE FOR ALL TO USE AND ENJOY FROM BOB (HAND SOLO)..
