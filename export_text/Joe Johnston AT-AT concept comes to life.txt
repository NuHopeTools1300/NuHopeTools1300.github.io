HI folks. Heres something i've wanted to do for a while now. Its the mega influential Joe Johnston concept AT-AT from way back in 1976.

At 1/16 scale, Im pretty sure this would have been the size if ILM had produced a miniature of this.

Ive loved this design since i first saw it and its influence is easy to see in the Sandcrawler as well as the AT-AT. Is about 27 inches long and built on a simple toy base which had roughly the same track design as the Joe's sketch. I will be making another one built onto a Pershing which is definitely the intended donor for this unused bit of hardware.

It's made from acrylic sheet, and kitbashed with anything i thought was applicable. I did try to keep most of the greeblies dated to that era, so there are quite a few shared with the screen used AT-AT's. 

I hope you guys like it. Happy modelling everyone.
