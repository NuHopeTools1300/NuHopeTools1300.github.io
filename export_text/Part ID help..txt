It looks to me like a LAU-7 missile launcher with the body of an AIM-9 Sidewinder minus the front and back fins. The real launcher was about 110 inches, so that divided by 4.5 inches puts it at about 1/24 scale. The LAU-7 was used on the F-18 Hornet, AH-1W Super Cobra and the OV-10 Bronco.

Hope that narrows it down. With the soft detailing, I can't see it being a modern model. Most likely from Revell, Lindberg, Airfix or Monogram. Perhaps another member can narrow this down further.
