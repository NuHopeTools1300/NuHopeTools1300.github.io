While EL is nice and even, it does not last. It gets dim over time. LEDs are still your best bet in terms of ease of installation, cost, and longevity. The trick is diffusing them to get an even glow without hot spots. You might consider using LED strip. It's like a tape with lots of LEDs stuck to it.
