I just looked up the Tamiya Sherman. It also looks as if the wheel configuration has changed from the Sherman to the Sandcrawler. The road wheels on the track assemblies have a much higher clearance and a more uniform trapezoidal shape on the Sandcrawler. 1/36 would be pretty good with down scaling some key parts

the 1/16 Sherman 1/16 would scale down to 1/32 (I found 1/35 parts, but will have to look for 1/32 parts)

the 1/24 Harrier stuff would scale down to 1/48. There seems to be kits of that as well

1/32 stuff scales down to 1/64 (an established scale for many things)
