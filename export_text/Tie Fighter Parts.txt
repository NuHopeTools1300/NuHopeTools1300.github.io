Joewhite has created a comprehensive parts map. It’s on studioscalemodellers.com. I believe you still have to request membership, since as a guest you can’t see the contents of the reference material section (and maybe other areas). You can see a snippet of the parts map on post 15 of the TIE Advanced build log here.

The wings are very hard to build correctly due to the dependency on Koolshade which is the ribbed material in the panels. There is some still around, but it’s hard to find and I’ve never seen an alternative look close to the original. See this thread for more detail.
