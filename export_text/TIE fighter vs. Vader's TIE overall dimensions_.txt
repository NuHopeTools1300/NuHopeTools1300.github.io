Both models used the same pilot figure, which was a combination of the pilot body from the 1/24 Airfix Harrier kit, the arms from the 1/24 Airfix Messerschmitt Bf 109 kit, and a head that was either a custom sculpt or came from some currently undiscovered 1/24 kit, possibly of a dragster or motorcycle. So the short answer, as Martyn said, is 1/24 for the pilots.
