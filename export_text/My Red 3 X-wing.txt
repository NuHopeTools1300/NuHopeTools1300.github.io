Keep in mind this is not the standard Red 3.  

The "canon" Red 3 in 1/24 scale is the one seen in the Art of Star Wars book, but this does not represent that one.  This model relects the larger scale Red 3 (with a different weathering pattern) that is seen on the MoM museum tour (hence large numbers of ref pics). 

Ironically, it apparently never saw screen time.  It was built for a seen in ROTJ (or was it ESB?) that didn't make it to the final print.

Thanks guys...

This was a blast to do. I dont get to do much for myself, always doing comission work...NOT COMPLAINING...

To answer some questions...

I just painted the pilot abnd added the visor, otherwise him looking like Luke is the skill of the sculptor.

The base is 1 inch MDF board and the rod is 3/4 copper pipe with an elbow soldered to another copper pipe. All painted flat black.

Mainly Floquil paints were used, most of the weathering is airbrush and oil washes. Very little pastels.

Dean
