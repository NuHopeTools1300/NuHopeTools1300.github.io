Bro'.....make your own. Go get a set of those brilliant death star tiles from Tony Agustin over at the SM shop.

Make silicon molds and duplicate them as often as you want. Don't have to mention that you are duplicating only for you and your project...right? Okay....these tiles are great in detail. You can of course place them Icons style...each one next to the other. Or you can leave spaces like the original death star sections.
