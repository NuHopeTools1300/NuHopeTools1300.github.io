So, monday I sat down and finally ran my Fibre optics. I've been putting this off for about 4 months. I'm using the Hyperdyne lighting kit, but replaced the blue and green LED's for a Yellow and a blinking red. I havn't hooked up the blinker in these pics, but the others are. I was hoping the yellow would be lighter. There's supposed to be 56 holes for FO's. I think I got 50 done. After that many I kinda felt loopy. The problem I have now is in the center of the console, there's supposed to be a white square lit. I'm going to have 'fun' running the large cylinder I have for it through the fron of it with all the FO in the way.
