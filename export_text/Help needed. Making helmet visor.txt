You might not have a large enough piece for the proper reaction for the resin to cure, or the tint may be causing problems. Try casting a clear piece without tint to see if this still happens. If it doesn't, you may still be able to get a translucent paint to put over top for the same effect. Or you could try making another mould with a larger piece that you can trim away.
