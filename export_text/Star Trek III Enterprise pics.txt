I don't know if that neck pylon segment is Trek II/ Trek III vintage. It is certainly painted in Enterprise A markings. The "Enterprise" marking on the photon launcher (which was never on the hero studio model) identifies it to me that this particular piece was used in Trek VI (likely used for the closeup when it fires a photon at Chang's cloaked Bird of Prey). I know a large pylon was built for Trek II as they used melted wax in it to simulate the Reliant's phaser fire carving it up like a hot knife through butter, but I thought that segment was potentially larger.

An oversized saucer was also built for Trek VI and used for pyrotechnic mayhem.
