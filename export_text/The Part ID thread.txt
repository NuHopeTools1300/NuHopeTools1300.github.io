────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

A lot of Airfix military kits were made to the same scale as OO model railways which is 1/76th scale but the bulk of their aircraft range were 1/72nd scale. The short answer is that manufaturers often fudge the scale indicated on the box to make the kits fit their marketing purposes.
