Here is a commission I have been working on . It is from the film Sinbad and the Eye of the Tiger. I have included picts of the clay model thus far and the reference material I have been using. In the process of sculpting his I have been trying to mentally strip away the fur to get at what the body underneath looks like. One book I have has 2 pictures of a tiger with with some hair on it. I don't know if it was used as the master model or just for study.(see pict below) so I have been using that to help with my sculpt.  It may not look anatomical to a real tiger but again my attempt is to recreate Harryhausen's tiger. What I would like to know is if any of you out there have any other good reference matreial for this project. Also does any one know what Ray used for the fur on this one.

Thanks
