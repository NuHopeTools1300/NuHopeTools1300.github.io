I received my RED 3 today from Richard (REL)! It has been a long haul with two different birds being built. First was the replica of the 4-5 foot RED 3 made for ROTJ that never saw screen time. Second, and the one you see here, is an exact replica of the ANH, two foot screen used model that currently resides in the Lucasfilm Archives! It is THIS one we decided should be in the collection. And it is the first X-wing to be painted as such!
