Dean,

Sorry you were a little dissatisfied with the casting- this thing was a bear to get made.  I had intended to do the casting in a pressure pot, but I was unable to access one that was big enough!

However, you certainly made it look amazing!

As far as base color goes, I recommend: 

   Polly Scale Model Railroad Colors

  D&RGW ORANGE  F414254 (1oz bottle) for the base color

  SAND F414302(1 oz bottle) for highlights

  (available at most hobby shops, particularly ones that carry model railroading supplies)

Mark

Yea, the 'salmon' color of the original Monogram kit was actually a VERY good match for the base color.  There is a lot of 'weathering' of lighter colors on top of that, but overall a very good match. 

Its not an easy paint job to get to look right- its very easy to get the whole thing too dark and too orange-y as you weather it down, but Dean really captured it!
