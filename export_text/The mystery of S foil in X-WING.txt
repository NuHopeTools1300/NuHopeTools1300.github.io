Those two panels that you see are only covering up the view of the shaft. They were temporary and stuck in place when the wings were open to cover it. You can tell that they are just stuck in there (with tape?) because they aren’t parallel to the inner wing surface.

So the pic with the uncovered shaft was probably a mistake. Kind of like leaving the droids out of their sockets in the Y wing miniatures in some shots.

I bet the ILM guys never expected people like us to dissect the filming models so much. 

They intended for us to view them on a big screen in a theater, with the models appearing to fly by quickly, not frame by frame on VHS (or digital) media to ‘catch’ the inconsistencies and mistakes. Missing or swapped droids, ‘wrong’ models in a shot, or even duplicates of the same model in a composite shot....they thought they could get away with it!

I sometimes think that the Lucasfilm, and ILM guys probably get a good laugh out of us, and the lengths (extremes) we go to duplicate their work!
