Hi everyone. Heres a little vid showing the completion of a really cool Red Dwarf background prop from Season 8. Shown in every opening sequence, so a nice well known (in a subliminal way) model from the Modelling skills of John Wellar, one of the crew together with Bill Pearson and Steve Howarth, who was kind enough to send me a couple of his personal images for reference. Thank you Steve, without those it would have been considerably trickier.

So i hope you enjoy this archeological trip, it's been great fun finding the parts and trying to see where they went.

Thanks folks,I hope you enjoy the vid,  happy modelling
