Hello All,

I wanted to share some photos of my Viper Mark 4B. I named it the B to differentiate it from the Michael Miles version released years ago. 

My friend Nik made the 3D model based on my reference and parts breakdown. The parts were then 3D printed. Here they are in the raw before prepwork. There still are some changes to be done. The parts were made hollow so I have the option of lighting it.
