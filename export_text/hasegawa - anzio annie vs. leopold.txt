Exact same kits - only change is the name on the box.  Beware, there is another Morser/Thor kit, which is the Munitionspanzer version.  Parts in this kit differ from parts in the regular Morser/Thor.  Later versions of the Munitionspanzer also differ from earlier versions.  The box will clearly state if it is a Munitionspanzer kit on the box.

No, just a few different sprues between the "rail" morser and the tracked "ammopanzer" "morser". 

  Typically, you will need some bit from both for most SS projects.

  You can NEVER have enough Leopold/Anzio annies and Morser Karls . . . especially if you can get them for ~$30.

  The "Old" are the dark gray plastic, and the "new" are a light gray. I've too found the old dark gray ones to be more brittle.
