If it helps, every single AT-AT in the archives is a different shade of grey when you compare them against each other. You can put on a blindfold, throw a dart at a grey color chart, and you're good. Bear in mind, the magic happens in the weathering - you can use Tamiya primer as a base color and be good to go, honest.
