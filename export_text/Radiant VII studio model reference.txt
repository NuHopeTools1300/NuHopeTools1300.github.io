Hi,

the recent posts of pictures of the studio model of the Radiant VII from Star Wars Episode I don't get out of my head, so I started to do some research.

There are statements for the length of this model which reach from 1.6m to 2.4m. Then I got the Blu Ray Star Wars box and discovered some short clips

of the pyro model being transported by two men. With pictures taken of my TV (have no Blu Ray player on the computer), I now tried to determine the

length of the model by comparing it to the height of the workers. I used statistical values for the height of male US citizens from Wikipedia.

You can see the result in the picture here:

[IMAGE: 92185-12df0e28b8718959cd840af4c123e3e0.jpg]

(image credits: Star Wars Blu Ray box, bonus disc 1)

With the man dressed in blue as reference, the length is 206.56cm, with the man in white as reference, it is 196.26cm long (both without antennas). 

I guess assuming the real size to be 2m (or 6.67ft) would be justified if no better data surface...

Someone here has better information?

Cheers,
