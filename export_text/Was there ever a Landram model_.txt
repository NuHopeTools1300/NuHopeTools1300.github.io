Okay...

I just got off the phone with the transportation department at Universal Studios.

The guy who I talked to admitted that he didnt work there when they were doing Galactica (but that was almost 30 years ago now so no big surprise there).  What he said was that he recalls the landram being there when he first started, but that it was "scrapped a few years back".

What exactly THAT means is anybody's guess.  I didn't have a chance to press him for more details. He didn't seem too interested in  answering questions. Probably union.

I'll keep researching. I sent an email to the Universal Transportation Department head and asked about the Landram and those cycles they used on Galactica 1980 (Land Probes).
