It was my birthday last week and so I decided to make myself a present, No surprise to the folks that know me, it was to be a Sky1. Good thing about this is I had access to the Original 2 footer owned by Phill Rea. I took loads of measurments and tracings and headed off to start. There is another one out there but thats just a fusalage moulded with the wings being seperate. Now I like my birds with wings moulded in. Harder to do but less chance of Splitting joins, What with the engines hanging. I intend to take a moulding from the main body. This is where I am with it.

And theres the nose! Note both parts are a tad oversize to compensate for shrinkage out of the mould.

The ribbed section is made up of the back end matching the front of the fuz and the circle at the front, Spaced at the required distance and then the tubes are set in at the right places then filler applied to the inside then sanded down.
