Eric

  I've been working on the TIE Variants for the last 12 years and have been over the Vader TIE a few hundred times.  To my knowledge the piece is not a kit part, or one that is a least known.  On one hand the edging looks too perfect not to be a molded styrene part, yet on some of the photos you can see what looks like burr marks on the sides and other imperfections.  I'm personally certain that the piece was scratch built.  

  I'm not sure about the Airfix 1/24th P-51 Mustang, would love to know what piece(s) you are referring to.

Look forward to seeing your build.

Dan
