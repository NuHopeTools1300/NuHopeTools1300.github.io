I use this stuff. Last I called Castrol, they had sold the stuff to another company...new stuff was simply labled Super Clean.

They didn't have the Castrol stuff anywhere in town the last time I went out for one so I settled on a generic brand. Don't know the name off the top of my head but it did work as good if not better than the Castrol stuff. 

Keep in mind it also strips putty (at least the Bondo spot putty I use). Can't remember if it affects stuff like Aves, though I would think not. Automotive paint? It eats the Plastikote primer I use. A 1-2 day dip and a toothbrush is all you really need. And water to wash it off of course. Hope this helps! Peace!
