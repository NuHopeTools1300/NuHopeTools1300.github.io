The black stand was just for the standard E. Signature editions got the gold trim.

Things to look for....

Gap between impluse drive and saucer

shabbily drilled holes for the three lights on the secondary hull top

slightly sagging saucer out of alignment with centerline of warp engines (I think most had this)

poorly masked landing lights below hanger bay

unstraight red stripes on top of secondary hull

too dark heavily drawn "grid" lines on saucer bottom or top

Uneven clear coat, "blotching" on saucer in the right lighting this stands out

blotching on inside of warp domes, this may happen much later

Decals used where the printed markings got screwed up

poorly drawn gridlines or evidence of erasure and corrections

Gaps where engine pylons meet the secondary hull. 

That is just off the top of my head,

I am sure I am forgetting some.

Detailed closeup pics appreciated of course, very curious if they finally got the QC issues solved.
