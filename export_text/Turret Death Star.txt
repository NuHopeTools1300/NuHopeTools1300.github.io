Hello everyone here is my project under construction, it is the Death Star turret.

I try to reproduce it using a maximum of original part when it's possible for me otherwise it's personal creation.

In some figures it measures 70 cm high with a base of 33 cm side.

It will be motorized (guns and turrets) with lightup effects for the guns and also the sound, for that I use the arduino for the movement of the turret and the pololu for the movement of the guns and the adafruit for the sound effects. The arduino and the servos are in programming settings (sequencing, movements, speed)

Here is the WIP in photos and videos.
