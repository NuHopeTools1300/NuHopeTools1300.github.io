I started this project in around 1994/5. Back then it was a one off build that I had radio controlled (Side flaps, wheel covers, and the beetle back. along with lights) I was Asked by a friend to turn it into a kit, built up the main parts with a view to adding all the detail in stages. I started the process and promptly lost intrest. About 4/5 years ago I went back to it and got it to the point you see. I gave it and all my reference to a mate who was gona do the artwork for the decals. Its taken this long to get it back  (July this year) Anyway I will probably finish it off next year.

The photos are of the kit build up (In Gray) the pic of it in white was the master model that was RC'ed.
