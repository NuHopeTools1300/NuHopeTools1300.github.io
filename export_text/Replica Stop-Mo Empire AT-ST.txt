Finally completed the chickenwalker from Empire Strikes Back this weekend. Some have asked me about the accuracy of this replica. Well, there's really no way to know, as the filming model no longer exists. And vintage photo-ref, if it still exists, has never surfaced as far as I'm aware. This model is the result of a group effort, and more information can be found here, for those who are interested: http://imaginager.blogspot.com/2008_09_01_archive.html

As seen in the SW Technical Journal, this model has a distinctive blast mark and "Viking" emblem on its left side. These details are not evident in the 2 scenes that made the film, so I've shot my pics as a kind of "before and after". 

Enjoy. BTW, see post #44 for the pre-weathered pics.
