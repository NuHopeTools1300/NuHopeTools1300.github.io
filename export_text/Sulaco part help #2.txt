U channel part with bumped out interior walls and those two round injection moulding marks. (Might be from the model train world)

Below the backwards Flakpanzer Gepard part.

The part is 1 inch (27mm long) and the Sulaco was made in England in 1984 using mostly model train parts. Appearing over a dozen times, its the most used part which makes me think it comes packaged as a bunch. Model train building kits tend to have many repeating simple parts like this.

I think it is a Plastruct part. Sold by EMA in England. This part is all over the Outland Refinery made around 81. Other Plastruct structural parts are also in the images you shared. You can see another on from the catalog pic I included. It's possible these parts have changed over time, but I know many of them are the same as I have ordered a number of them recently for Outland and Space 1999 builds. So it's it good bet it is this part from the catalog.
