Thanks guys. I am honored to be your opinion. I am not the master, it has it! Everyone here in the rpf inspired me do my thing. Germany is also considered in my work, but here the guys have really feeling!

I proceeded with the painting as follows: Primer in light gray and wethert with Mixol black and brown (Mixol is a concentrate for varnishes and other colors), I have it diluted with water and applied with rags once. with an airbrush, I have put accent on dark areas.Notes / details I have done with a brush. Then I sprayed all over with clear varnish to protect to.
