I decided to scribe some panel lines to designate the

call sign for the ship, I wasn't thinking which one I wanted

to make at the time. But at 9mm wide I was able to fit 6

of them some ......so Gold 6. Then I checked to see if there

was a Gold 6..... turns out Gold 6 was the call sign for Hol

Okand, he flew for the Alliance in the early years so it's

possible he flew one before they started leaving off the panels.

Flying under the callsign Gold Six, Okand flew with the Y-wings of Gold Squadron in the early years of the Alliance. Prior to the Battle of Yavin, Okand played a role in disabling the deflector shields of an Imperial outpost at Kashyyyk, where he flew as Jon Vander's wingman, using his ion cannons to bring down the shields.

Here he is but in a X-wing it said he died in the battle of Yavin.
