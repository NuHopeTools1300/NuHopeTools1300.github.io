Hi RPF buddies :lol

In this forum, I already have another post regarding ANH ISD.

But I'd like to update periodically progress of my ISD using this post.

For my SW museum, I 'd like to make the Imeprial Star Destroyer mark-I.

If someone asks me "what is the most impressive scene in the movie you've seen recently"?

I will say it without hestitation, Leia's pod escape from the Star Destroyer.

First, I estimated the length of 3’ ISD in order to make the frame as follow.

The length and wide are 910mm X 460mm based on Lucas museum book which is published from Japan.I think that the length in the book is wrong. 

Several months ago, "Ringa" said the length of ISD is about 4feet.

i.e.) 4feet = 4 x 304.8mm (= 1feet) = 1219.2mm

I tried to prove above story using Tamiya 1/35 8rad.

Used part of 8rad in ISD is exactly 152mm as 1st pic and 2nd pic.

Also, the length of ISD is similar to 8 x used part of 8rad.i.e.) 8 x 152mm = 1216mm

From above result, I found the length of ISD is nearly 4feet. (refer to 3rd pic)

Secondly, I measured the length and proportion of ISD using Excel spread sheet because I've never been used 3D tools such as CAD.(refer to 4th pic)

The basic frame is made from wood and the method is the same process by ILM modeler.

It's a tremendous...Actually, I feel MR AT-AT beyond ISD frame is too small. 

From now on, I will border with acrylic framework the sides of ISD.

In the 5th pic, I used a measure to the tune of 500mm in order to compare to ISD.
