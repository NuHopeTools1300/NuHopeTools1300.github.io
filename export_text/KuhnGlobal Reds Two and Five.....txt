In late January, I started a Mike Salzo Hero X-wing for Kurt. I've been commissioned to build Reds Two and Five ( Wedge and Luke ).

Right now, as the photos show, I've finished the engine cans and the R2 strip and am working on the main fuselage. The engine cans were weathered using pastels and a 50/50 water/Craft Barrel Flat Black wash applied with a brush and then blown with a straw. The speckling comes from the use of an old toothbrush and 

The afterburners have actual red cellophane gels added behind the heat sink detail just as the studio models did.

I removed the thicker material in the engine cans with the Dremel so that the turkey feathers looked appropriately thin.
