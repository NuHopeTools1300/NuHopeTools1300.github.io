Just wanted to share this Viper that I'm working on. It's nearly finished. Just have a few touchups to do and finish off the bottom of the fuselage with new T track. It needed a LOT of cleanup work. I also had to grind down the bottom rails and going to add new ones from plastruct. The bottom of the fuselage was so uneven, had to add styrene to even it out. I think the results are pretty good for what this kit was. It's the studio scale kit that Monsters in Motion offers. Most likely an old Icons viper recast from what I can see. Going to add a name plate and acrylic case to it after it's completely done.
