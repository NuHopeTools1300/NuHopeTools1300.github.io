Some more info: The first 2 and 4 foot pulls were painted by a guy by the nickname of Dragon (if memory serves). He finished the 2 footer an put it on the conference table next to the original.  He was the only person who could tell which one was the original, his painting was that dead on.  There were many shades of green and silver. Like most of the SS replicas, the first few were hand painted by a small group of talented people. Later the assembly line of god knows who came in. So in a sense if you got a really low number X-wing, ID4 attack ship etc., you got a superior paint job.
