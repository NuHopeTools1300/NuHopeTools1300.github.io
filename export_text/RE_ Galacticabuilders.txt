Sure, anything studio scale-ish, BG related and made out of physical materials (not ones and zeros) is welcome IMO. CGI has been discussed recently, but more as a construction tool for blueprinting. There are plenty of other forums for CGI discussion. We just like big-ass starship models.

I think it's at around 250 members now, with very few contributors. This has always seemed very strange to me- someone must be building something out there?

The group focus is really for people who enjoy building physical BG replicas, although if you wish to contribute in showing your work in a smaller scale, you are most welcome. I built a 4.5 foot Galactica years ago before I got really serious about the subject, but have yet to see a half-scale version. I'd love to do one myself, but the bench is full for the next few years.

See you there.
