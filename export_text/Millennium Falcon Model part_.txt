that is the rear "engine deck" off of a panther tank.  The same panel is used all over the place on the ILM models.

If you are detailing a ERTL falcon - look for a 1/144 tank.  I used it on mine and it was perfect.

if you're going a ESB 32' falcon its a 1/72 tank, and the 5 footer used a 1/35
