The Nichimo Tiger 2 and the Jagdtiger decks are 99% identical, the only difference is the size of the centre hole in one of the vents below the door (closest to rear), which is cut from the part on the model. The Jagdtiger also supplies the travelling rest for the barrel as a SS donor (picture here; http://www.therpf.com/attachment.php?attachmentid=583659&d=1454048210) and 3-4 other parts on the Y.

Yes, turns out the GREEN background Otaki Yamato is also a boxing that contains the original molding of the kit.  Just be sure to avoid the Yellow background boxing and the Silver background boxing.  Set your Ebay search for "OT1-28 Otaki Yamato 1/600", and either be awake between 1:30 AM and 2:30 AM when Ebay sends out their alerts, or do searches on International Sellers at least once a night/morning every day for two years and you too will get lucky.  (I am under the impression that International Sellers do not "show up" in Saved Searches, which is how I found mine in Germany of all places.  The guy who had it wrote the price he paid for it in 1977 on the inside box top, and it was less than 12 Deutschemarks.
