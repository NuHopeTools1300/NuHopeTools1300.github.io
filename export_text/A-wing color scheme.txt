The color I had picked out for the "red" was a red primer, one of the automotive brands, I can't remember the exact brand but it was pretty close to chronicles color wise.  I'll see if I can dig it out maybe next week? That projects been in a box in the back of the shop for far too long . . . too many projects and not enough time.  Look for a red automotive primer at your local autozone, wallyworld, etc, and that should be a good start point for you.  Not sure what was originally used, but I LOVE my rattlebombs and I've gotten pretty good at matching colors, and IIRC it was pretty spot on.

  I know for the white I was going to use Tamiya Insignia White since its actually a VERY light grey color to help tone down the white a bit prior to weathering.  Plain white was too stark on the test shot.

  Hope that helps
