Of course, being a geek, I have a perfect justification for the GAL 356 on all subsequent  shuttle shots...

That designation only refers to the ship the shuttle came from. Perhaps it was like a "cattle brand" for the battlestar so they could identify shuttles that belonged to them.

Or maybe the number referrs to a shuttle manufacturing lot and there were plenty of them... all for the Galactica out of lot #356.  I don't think that there was ever a mention of specifically what the numbers on the side of the shuttle stood for.

That having been said, it is absolutely more reasonable to think that each shuttle would have it's own operating number and that the number would be the most prominent marking. So, I totally understand where you're coming from.  But the proof is in the putting* and we cannot doubt the continuing evidence that many shuttles bear the GAL356 label.
