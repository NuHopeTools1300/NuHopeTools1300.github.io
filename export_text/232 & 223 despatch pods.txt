I present you now my 223 pod. It's REL's kit, mastered by Ralphee.

All the greeblies casting I got with my kit have an issue, they were soft as chewing gum. Richard, as a professional, kindly proposed me to replace them but I refused, I had already in mind to use original kitparts, that was some time ago. And the pod slept on the shelf for many months. 

Now it's time to finish this one as I'm also working on the 232. 

Im using Rich's rotocast hull (I had some problem with it as one side was thin like paper and it crushed... I did my best to fix that ! The hull was in 6 pieces after the disaster. :cry)

[IMAGE: 223_pod3.jpg]

[IMAGE: 223_pod1.jpg]

[IMAGE: 223_pod2.jpg]

[IMAGE: 223_pod4.jpg]

[IMAGE: 223_pod5.jpg]

Im not as crazy as Jason, I cut a 312B plate and cast it. 

Of course, when I placed mu plastruct order, as always I forgot something... this time that's the tiny dishes which goes on top of the "T-shaped" 232 parts. 

I used a chieftain part to detail the base of the truster. 

I dont know if there ever was a cap built for the nose of this baby. So I dont know what to put as the flat nose is a bit annoying to watch.
