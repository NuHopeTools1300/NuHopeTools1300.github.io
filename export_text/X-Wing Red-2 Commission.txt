Hi guys, thanks.

JMChladek said:

			Was the tan shade really that yellow on Red 2 (like the Red 4 coloring)? If it is, I LIKE it!
		

Click to expand...

 You are right JMChaldek. After a lot of thinking and looking at pics we decided to paint the Red 2 commission as seen on the  Ken Rice set of pics. 

[IMAGE: red2kenrice03.jpg]

We realized the Ken Rice pics showed a more yellow color than the Blue 1 pics but then again the blue one pics show the bird before the final coats of weathering were applied. 

 As we started painting the bird we saw that the panels seen very yellow in some of the Ken Rice pics made the X-Wing look so cool that we decided to give the commission that tone of weathering, a tendency to yellow just as Red 6 has that tendency to Red Brick marks and panels.

 And as always happens with pics of the ILM X-Wing's the strength and color of the weathering and panels varies a lot from pic to pic depending on the lightning of the shot, so we decided to try to paint our version in way it had it's own identity and that it looked as cool as we could.

What do you guys think?
