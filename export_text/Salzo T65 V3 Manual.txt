Well, it's here.

And I kinda wish it wasn't. :angry

The castings are, in a word.. I can't think of a word for it.

It has got to be a recast - there is no way that this kit would gain such popularity and value if the quality (or lack thereof) of the castings I received are any indication.

FYI - I didn't expect a kit that I would just shake the box and the pieces would fit together, nor am I a novice at resin kits - but this has to be up there with the worst I have ever seen (and I have seen some shockers).

For a brief summary - the guns are bent, the brass tubing protrudes out the back of the guns, the upper fuselage is warped, the lower fuselage is covered in pinholes, the cockpit is a disaster, the engines have small chips of resin missing... These are actually the GOOD points!

To say that this is going to take a lot of time and effort is a huge understatement - especially considering this was a grail kit for me.

I will try to post pics later today - if I can summon up the enthusiasm to take it back out of it's box.
