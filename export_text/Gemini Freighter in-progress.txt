Hello there,

Decided to start another Original Series Battlestar Galactica Studio Scale project. Figured I'd try to tackle this little beauty.

All 16 containers are being built from scratch, using the same kits the studio did (Reefer, Moving Van, Container, Post Van).  I've even been matching them up on the sides to the rivets. It's been a lot of fun, can't wait to get them painted and weathered.

Started on the head section as well.  Most everything is fitting exactly as it should to the studio miniature.  I'm still adding some greeblies here and there, so these are not the complete parts yet.  All in all, it's coming together pretty well.  Substituting some greeblies where I can't ID the parts where needed, just to speed this thing along.

The last pic is of the studio miniature as a comparison.

More to come...

-Sean

Here's another update. Making progress on the engine section. I had to use a couple sub's for original parts, but most of them are correct to the filming miniature. I'm not planning to light the engines, will use a reflective tape similar to what they did. I still have to add the styrene strips on the inner engines that cover the seam from the railgun parts, and have a few more parts (like radiators) to add to the engine section.

The last two pics show the updated head section with the crane parts installed, and the part of the harrier that was cut out to make the main head body.
