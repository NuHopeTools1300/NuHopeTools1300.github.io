Was just reading this article over at The Digital Bits:

The Digital Bits - The 2011 Star Wars Saga Blu-rays - First Insights

The article is a preview held over the weekend for some members of the media for the pending release of the entire saga on Blu-Ray.

One of the people attending the media event on behalf of Lucasfilm was Dennis Muren.  There is an extras disk in the set that includes dozens of what are referred to as "Model Turnarounds" - that allow you to see filming miniatures from a variety of angles.

A comment attributed to Dennis in the article states "fans who like to build replica models of Star Wars vehicles will really appreciate the high level of detail you get to see"

Have a read.
