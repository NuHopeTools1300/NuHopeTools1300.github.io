Definitely looks like the right kit to me! I found a nicer image of the turret and it shows the indent along the bottom rim of the lid as well as the locations in which the rectangular slots should be, based on the details that are glued in place all around it. So should be the Tamiya 1/35 T-62A? The misleading part is that the box art of the T-62A is incorrect as it doesn't show the indented line around the bottom of the turret, but any pictures of the built kit that show up in a search point to it being the correct one.

[IMAGE: 262279-3186694bde471bcda3e9d6dd818276ad.jpg]

Edit 1: I also get the feeling a plain ol' ball has been placed inside of the Vader helmet to bridge the empty space between the bottom of the turret and the helmet beneath it. But I'm sure most here came to that conclusion fairly quickly 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]

Edit 2: Realized the dead giveaway that it's correct is because of the two columns in the tiny holes are dead on as well. Only difference is the welding droid does not include the plate with the bolts shown inside the red circle on the right below.

[IMAGE: 262284-aed4b794cafa4ba844952bbd97fdc074.jpg]
