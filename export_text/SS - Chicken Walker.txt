Thanks guys. Kudos to Guy for the great plaque. Also, the pose was a biooootch given that the styrene rod below the foot broke twice... had to reinforce it with brass rod. 

4 kids at the age of 6 and under has impeded my model building pace. This one has been almost finished for quite some time. Being a daddy and a model builder is extremely tough. I had a window of about 15 minutes to get a couple photos, so sorry for "bedsheet" background.

The base is an old tombstone from a new bond deal I was involved with (a tombstone is an acrylic "trophy" to commemerate a financial deal usually... I'm a financial analyst... so it is a rare occassion when my job and pastime collide). By the way, drilling through the acrylic was NOT easy.
