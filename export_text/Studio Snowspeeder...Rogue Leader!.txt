Started out on this last weekend, pretty much spent the weekend taking dims and measures.

It is the Gort kit, but i was pretty bummed about the quality of the hull, hence me taking dims etc.....and rebuilding the whole thing in styrene....my head hurts already LOL.

Itll match Nicks pattern proportions pretty much spot on, but im making a few tweaks to the hulls to make it a bit more specific to Rogue Leader.

Ive placed a few of Nicks resin castings upon the hull, and so far, so good, everything seems to fit well...but im still not sure how much of the kit ill use, there are a few areas that need a bit more accuracy refinement....well see.

This model will be radio controlled.....nooo, it wont fly,are you MAD :lol....but the upper and lower flaps will move via micro servo's......in time that is.....as my head...yeah, its still hurting taking dims from the hulls, but im not throwing all this work, and RC equipment, at poor hulls casts....whats the point?

bests......lee
