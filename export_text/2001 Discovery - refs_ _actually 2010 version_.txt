Ok....Bumping this as new info has come to light on this particular model. check this.

Large scale 1984 movie prop miniature/model of the USS Discovery spacecraft. Built by award-winning model maker Don Pennington (Titanic, Star Trek, Ghostbusters, Cocoon, The Right Stuff, True Lies, The Abyss, etc.) from the motion picture 2010: The Year We Make Contact. At 9-1/2 feet long, this model is big, though a smaller (3-1/2 ft.) and a larger version (12-1/2 ft.) were eventually used in the movie -- this model was not fully completed at the time.After the film release, this model was painted and detailed by Pennington like the 2001 Discovery (which was destroyed at the request of Stanley Kubrick). The 2001 finish is far more attractive than the orange dust-covered 2010 scheme.This model has been in storage for over 25 years, appears to be complete in every painstaking detail and is in great condition. It is packed in a custom, re-usable crate. The loaded crate is 10-ft long x 18-in. x 18-in. and weighs 175lbs. The model has 3 recessed hardpoints and hangers with stainless aircraft cables to hang from tall ceilings. It is made of urethane resin castings, photo-etched brass parts and other materials on a steel and wood frame. The triple antenna is detachable and can be set in any position. The forward sphere is hollow with clear cockpit windows and a removable dome access panel (allowing interior customization). The maker's mark appears in a couple of places on the model.The attached photos of the Discovery "in space" were taken by me, with a black sheet and a simple spotlight. This model comes directly from Donald Pennington, Inc. It was stored in conditioned space, in Los Angeles. It took a five-person crew several months to complete, at a cost many times higher than its current reserve price. This artifact comes with some documentation: drawings and photographs taken during the construction.This is your best (and probably only) chance to own one of the most recognizable spacecraft in motion picture history -- a vehicle fully designed by aerospace engineers and built by special effects craftsmen.

Vintage USS Discovery 2001 2010 Space Odyssey Movie Prop Model 114in. Long | eBay

His shop built two of them, one for the casino and one for Don and his hung in his shop until he moved to Arizona in 2011 (or was it 2010...).  I used to work for Don, but not on this project.  Anyone promoting this as being associated with 2010 is very much misinformed.
