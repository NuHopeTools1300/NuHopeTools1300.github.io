I've always loved the A-Wing but never felt like I could build the studio scale one - just too damn big and curvy. However, perfect storm of John Simmons build of the prototype, mixed with a ROTJ theme build for Wonderfest this year, plus seeing new BTS photos of the SSD trench - a sudden urge to build hit me. My previous builds have sometimes taken years to complete. With the WF deadline looming, I ended up pushing this one out in four months. I'll admit its rushed in some spots (specifically the paint job) - but it was totally worth it.
