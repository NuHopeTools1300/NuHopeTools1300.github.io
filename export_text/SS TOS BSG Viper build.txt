It's a mag light reflector, search for mag light 108 000 038 for these specific ones, which fit the smaller SS stuff like vipers etc pretty well. You need to open up the bulb area a little to accept a 5MM superbright LED.  

On the viper afterburner part on this kit there is a center mark on the inside of the plate, I used a 3/4 inch drill bit to open it up.  That way the reflector sits flush on the plate with a small lip to glue up the reflector to the afterburner part.  Over this a clear cast detail plate will help diffuse the light more evenly.

thanks

mike

Post #10  (forum post #1234183)
Author    : mslz22

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

For the fusion line, about a day or two.  It really depends on the paint, most of the time i try to judge the tackiness of it, too tacky and it will not work.  Most of the time I try to use flat paints, the only  kits I have done with semi gloss or gloss has been the viper, tie fighter, and x wing, and that was because a good color match was readily available in a spray can that only came in semi gloss.  I have started going to autopaint stores to get good matched paints in flat, the nozzles they give you are as good as a spraygun for painting the basecoat, and they dry superfast.

thanks

mike
