Check the Facebook groups for discussions regarding MYC ships accuracy. I believe there were a couple RPF members involved in the discussions as well. If you want nice ships with limited editions (unlicensed though) they might be a good choice. If you want better accuracy then you might want to look elsewhere. One thing that you'll notice immediately is the details which are not as crisp and sharp as they should be

I will only mention the MYC 1/4 Stormtrooper and TIE Pilot statues myself, as I've seen both in person and were disappointing regarding accuracy. The Stormie doesn't look much better than the 1/6 older Gentle Giant Stormtrooper statue, with narrow chest armor and other issues, while the TIE Pilot is kinda fat and has a tiny helmet. Again, the 1/4 size statues are impressive, but a decently trained eye will notice the accuracy issues right away.
