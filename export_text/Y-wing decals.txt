────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Lee, if you check out the Falcon's Decal Survey files hosted on my local modeling club's website, there is a spreadsheet that lists (as much as is known) the sources for the decals.  I'm certain the AMT Watkins trailer is cited there as I recognize the use of the black hood stripes, the manufacturer's black & silver plates (lower right corner), the tax stickers plate next to them, plus the Fruehauf and Cummins stickers.  I believe the blue & white Florida license plate was also used (trimmed) on something I'd looked at recently (TIE fighter perhaps) so it was definitely a well-used decal sheet.  Thanks for the nice scan of the sheet, btw!

Oh, the website again is:

http://hamptonroadsscalemodelers.com/

Follow the 'New' link or check under the Tools>Reference & Reviews tab for the relevant articles.

Regards, Robert
