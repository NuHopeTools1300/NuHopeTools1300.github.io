You would probably want to mold and cast.

Unless you already have a good 3d scanner and printer on hand, molding will give far better results for much cheaper 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
 In fact, for simple shapes like these, it would be much faster and cleaner to model them in 3d from scratch, using the source parts for measurements than trying to scan them using photogrammetry. But then you'd still need a nice resin printer to get smooth parts. So yeah, molding all the way, will only cost you about $100 to get enough good silicon and resin to mold all the parts you need.

If you want to keep the kit pristine, I guess you could try to mold and cast the whole sprues
