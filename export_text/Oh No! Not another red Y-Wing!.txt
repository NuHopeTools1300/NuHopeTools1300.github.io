Re: Oh No! Not another red Y wing!

Hey Paul, this is a better Floquil paint chart-

http://www.testors.com/category/133505?n=0

Some of the colors are a bit off but most are pretty close.

The mods are mostly parts removed, the replacement parts are from the airfix bf 109, a bunch of them come from that kit then the centurion parts on the wing.

The correct cannons on top of the canopy are from the 109 kit too and are the machine guns that are in the nose cone of the aircraft, the ones in the wings fit the gold y-wings.

Also the top laser cannons had two little rockets on the sides of them.

The "tubing" is green insulated single strand copper wire.

One of the most important mod was the installation of the partial r2 strip on the lower rear fuselage and then the S-5 part on the right hand nacelle.

Al, thanks bud! Im working on this and trying to finish the resource colector and some BSG stuff.

Julien, it is Floquil  south pacific armour sand. when photographing it, it looks more yellow than the reefer yellow, which is probably the correct color on the red leader.
