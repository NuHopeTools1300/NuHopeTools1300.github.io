Thanks Matt and Kurt!

If anyone is interested in one of these, please contact me at mbradley3@cfl.rr.com.  I also have some much cheaper kits made up of mis-cast pieces that will require a little more than usual clean up, but are entirely workable.

Just to add to the theme of this thread, Here is my own build I threw together out of some of the first (imperfect) castings out of the mold:

http://flickr.com/photos/10867551@N0...7605197661465/

(last 5 pics on the album)

 I built and painted it in a day to display at another convention in Tampa where Gil Gerard, Erin Gray, and Felix Silla were appearing (Gil and Erin asked me to bring the models, so how could I refuse!). Even with the quickie treatment I was pleased with the way it came out- the later castings with some actual careful assembly and painting should come out even better as we are seeing with Matt's build!

Modelers, Miniatures, and Magic 

This is the name of the gallery, online in the next few weeks, and in person in 2012.

The site (when it comes up) will be www.modelermagic.com (.net, .org, etc)

This site will be for professional modelers and collectors to show their works in any way they wish to... even with tutorials if they choose. Each will have their own section to do what they will with. It will be a one stop shop with reference materials, and contact information for both the artists, and the makers of any kits. The galleries will show everything from the items creation to the pieces final display with the collector (if they choose to be involved with the site).

It is NOT a board, though it will be laid out like one for ease of use. There will be no commentary from anyone other than the person in charge of their own area. There will be a live chat area (that anyone an participate in) where scheduled live chats will occur with specific artists, kit makers, collectors, and possibly major industry names.

In short, it is a promotional tool I am providing to the professional hobby to help along the continuing imaginations and aspirations of those who will eventually become professional artists, or even those who just wish to enjoy the hobby.
