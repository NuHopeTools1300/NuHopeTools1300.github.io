Years ago I restored one of those. The wings were horribly warped. I replaced them with a new set of wings and repainted the model. I tried to use the same ‘flavor’ as the original paint job, but less weathering.

The original model was very heavily ‘weathered’ with a mist of black spray paint.

I gave it as a gift to a very close friend of mine.
