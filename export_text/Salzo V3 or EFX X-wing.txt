Depends on whether or not you want studio accuracy vs an idealized/implied version. 

The Salzo V3 is as close as you're going to get for studio filming accuracy . But you have to ask him to add the specific parts. He made two different cockpits, different pilot figures etc. 

 From what I understand an accurate mounting point system is in the development stage from another party. Can we say opening wings and 6 points of mounting point greatness! The hull is hollow after all. 

The EFX is idealized in that the cockpit opens (balk), the cockpit is a replica of the set cockpit, pilot looks like a real Luke etc. R2 and cockpit has lights.

 Maybe they can add sound effects etc... ("The force is strong with this one", "Use the force Luke". 

Hull wise it looks pretty close. The paint job off the factory floor will stink, that's a given on all the MR ships.
