Description:

A miniature ship component from the production of Richard Marquand’s Star Wars - Episode VI: Return of the Jedi. The piece was made for the production to be used as a component in dressing one of the ships in the film, but remains unidentified as which it was made for.

Made from resin, the piece is made to depict a ship's thruster control jets. The piece has three jets aligned horizontally and includes multiple features detailing to depict the tail end of a ship. Painted grey, the piece has a metallic finish, and while some paint has chipped from age, the piece remains in very good overall condition.
