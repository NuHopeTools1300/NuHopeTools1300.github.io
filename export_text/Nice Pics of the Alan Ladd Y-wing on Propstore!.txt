This Ladd/Propstore Y is Tiger Sprockets (named for the mount cover on the top - look at the LEM Saturn part and instead of an F1 part, it has the tank wheel). 

Gold Leader is Tie Killer. Now resides in private hands.

Triangles is a pyro, and probably was a stand in for Gold Leader? Detailing is almost identical - even the piping. Profiles sold it's shards a few years back, I believe.

The ILM tour ANH Y is the "Red Stripe Artoo" 

There's also Red Jammer (not filmed but used for the Elstree set construction) and the Bill George fan-made-but-used-in-ROTJ Y with the R5 head, also at the archives.

The archives also have a small handful of crudely cast and painted "stunt" Ys with riveted brass strips instead of Ts - dunno if they were pyros or what. They all have permanent mounts so they're like Ywings on sticks.
