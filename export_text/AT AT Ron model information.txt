I was around here when they were offered and considered getting one but never did. The original modelmaker’s first name was Ron hence the name of the kit.  I believe these came out prior to the MR offering and was the first SS AT-AT. It def looked the part although with regard to actual accuracy, I believe there were some issues with it. 

From memory, it was an original sculpt with some found model kit parts used for reference and for some reason, my memory banks are telling me the legs were pose-able. Can’t remember for sure. As far as the offering size….it was small. I want to say it was a $500 kit which was expensive for the time, so I don’t think there was a huge demand. 

As I always say with many of my posts, others would surely know more and I am only giving you quips from a memory that isnt the best.

I got rid of all of my MR items and had the original MR AT-AT with the mirrored base. It was a beautiful piece and the only studio scale item I have ever had. 

It’s been some time now but the initial offering with the mirrored base was fetching more money than the second offering that had the Hoth diorama. I’m not sure if that holds today but surely you can find out. 

Lastly, when I sold my last MR item, I listed it on the MR Facebook collectors group. I only mention because you may be able to find someone selling one cheaper than eBay. 

As far as accuracy, nothing comes close to the things the SS members do/have done here.
