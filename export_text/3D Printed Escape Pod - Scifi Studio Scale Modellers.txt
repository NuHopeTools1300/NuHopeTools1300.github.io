Yes, it's me again 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
 this time I want to show you our Studio Scale Size version of the Escape Pod. If anyone asks what scale this is... well, that's hard to say but we estimate it to be 1:10. The main body is made entirely out of one print. The complete kit consists of 17 parts. 

This modified version was again built from our team member Robert Cowley.

The painted pod in the following pictures is the V1 of the pod from us, and unpainted are the V2, as soon as I have permission from the customer I will show you more pictures of a finished painted V2 pod.

This replica of a Escape Pod (V1) from us was painted by Lee Stringer with of course Archive-X Paint
