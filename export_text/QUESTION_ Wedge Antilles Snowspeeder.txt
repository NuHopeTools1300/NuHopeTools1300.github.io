I recently heard second-hand that Steve Gawley said the originals were indeed RED and that that decidedly orange colored speeder seen on tour, that we all assume was Wedge's Speeder, was actually made POST FILMING for promotions/tours (a tell being how it mounts to the stand). I will be asking some ILMers about this next month - I'll let you keow what turns up, if anything at all. 

I can tell you one thing - no one will remember which color was used if it was paint.

But I know they used Letrafilm on the screen used grey speeder. It would likely follow that they used the red Letrafilm on the Wedge Speeder. I have some - it's all designed to match Pantone colors. So you can easily get a sample, via Pantone, to match paint to. I would NEVER use Letrafilm on a model for longevity purposes.
