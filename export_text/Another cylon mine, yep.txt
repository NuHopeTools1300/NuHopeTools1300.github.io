I wanted another way to mount this thing, I found some small knurled 3mm nuts, I embedded 1 in the model. Then made a brass stand with another silver soldered in place. This will look better for display purposes. I probably will not detail the back side, the old axiom states "If that side of the model never gets filmed, then it stays unfinished. Plus there isn't any photo ref of the backside. Those leopold braces are so delicate. they broke twice already.
