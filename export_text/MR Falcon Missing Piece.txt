You may have heard some people complaining about a "missing" piece on one of the front mandibles of Falcons they have received. While it's not impossible that some parts might have been damaged (there are a lot of small fragile parts on this piece), MR need to figure out the scope of the problem, if in fact it is a problem.

The starboard (right) cockpit side mandible should have two small detailed parts as shown below.

[IMAGE: StarboardFRONTMandible1copy.jpg]

The port (left) radar dish side mandible should have one part at the top corner but NO part on the lower corner as shown below.

[IMAGE: PortFRONTmandiblecopy.jpg]

There is evidence to support that the Falcon did indeed have an extra detail part on the lower left mandible, but it broke off long ago.

MR are charged with replicating the model as exactly as we can, as it exists in the archives today (excluding obvious damage and wear and tear). If clear evidence is available where we can point directly to a part that's missing and show EXACTLY what it was, then we can add it back on. This was NOT the case on the front mandible. The part was missing and we had no CLEAR pics proving what kit part had been used. Rather than make it up we left it off.

The Falcon travels the world on display and this, along with age, takes a toll on the model.

One detailed part on the underside had been replace with an unpainted gray plastic duplicate. Dean when painting his master, copied the pics of the model and painted the part gray. Common sense told us that it should have been body coloured and we had a clear older pic of the underside that supported this, so we got the factory to ignore the gray part on the master and paint it body coloured.

A huge amount of research went into this but some choices had to be made with Lucasfilm.

If people THINK a part is missing please get them to check this thread. If it's on the PORT (LEFT) mandible then there should only be ONE detailed part. If it's on the STARBOARD (RIGHT) mandible then there should be TWO detailed pieces.

Thanks for looking.

Barry

Dave,

When ILM completed the 32" Falcon, each of the arms had 2 pieces. When MR began development of their replica, the same ILM 32" Falcon had 2 pieces on the right arm, but only 1 piece on the left arm. One of the pieces went missing between construction and the time MR started creating their replica.

All of those who ordered the MR replica should get all 3 of the original 4 detail pieces that were on the mandible arms of the ILM filming model. That's the condition the model was in when MR got to it.

Evidently, someone reported not having all of the mandible arm detail pieces. But, I don't know if it was ever confirmed that the person was actually missing any of the 3 included mandible arm detail pieces. 

If you have 3 pieces, you're good.
