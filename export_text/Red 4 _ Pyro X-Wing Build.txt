The shrinkage is pretty much proportional, isn't it? I've had a good look at hollow and solid castings side by side, can't spot much difference; admittedly I'm not using verniers. Just that I'd hate for anyone to misunderstand and word to go around that Frank's solid castings are some kind of POS or anything, you know? Solid resin does shrink a bit, that's all.
