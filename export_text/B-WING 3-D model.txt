As some of you may know, at the moment, I am between workshops...the tenancy expired on my boatyard spot and I am moving out of London. So (probably) no more death defying adventures on the high seas, trying to land improbably large replicas of classic props. 

So I am working my way thru Star Wars ships that I either haven't modelled, just fancy modelling, have been asked to model or may want to build some time in the future...just to stay sharp and involved. 

First of those, and inspired by Thibkaji's wonderfully detailed build thread, as the title says, is the Studio Scale B-Wing...a real oddball craft but, as always, quite rewarding once you dig into the guts of the build.

Anyway on with the pics; first off is the Cockpit/Canopy section.
