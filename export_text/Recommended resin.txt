They now make a pre-mixed version of the go-to mix everyone has been doing forever for detailed models:  in the past it was Tech Fast Navy Grey + Tenacious black.  Now you can get "tough navy gray", which is much cheaper than buying the tenacious separately. it's the same mix. Works really well.  Does not sag even a little (assuming you're not way overloading it structurally).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Tenacious and the “tough” mixes like tough navy are a little less brittle on fine detail parts in my past experience of trying out making mixes. Generally tenacious = a little more give, blu = more durable except for fine details, because of the lack of give.  The little bit of give isn’t a bad thing.
