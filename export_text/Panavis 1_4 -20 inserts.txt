Guy, just go to Lowes or Home Depot and look in the nuts and bolts section..........threaded inserts should be hanging up somewhere.  You can usually get packs of 4 of these things for a couple bucks.  The longer ones are best as you can anchor them inside better.   One other trick I have used is to get a 1/4" bolt and begin threading at the back of the insert and anchor the entire bolt and insert for extra stability.   I used a longer threaded rod with the same threads and extended from bow to stern on my Xwing.   Worked like a charm.  By doing this,  you can support the weight of the whole model and not just at that insert location.   These resin kits are just too heavy to trust such a small connecting point.
