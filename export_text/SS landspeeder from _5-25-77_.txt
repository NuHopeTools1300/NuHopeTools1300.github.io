Hi everyone,

I'm still here.

The last couple weeks have been absolutely mental. The initial deal to move into a large new workshop fell through as the owner considered me to be the golden goose or something. So i managed to strike a deal with a fellow modelmaker to work out of his shop, which is in the middle of nowhere unfortunately. This is just being set up, with my tools spread out over several locations. But most important was the fact that i was working on 2 architectural models over the past weeks for one client, with no room for delays. The deadline simply had to be met. And i did..yesterday...

It meant working up to 20 hours a day, including 3 all-nighters to meet the divided deliveries of all the components of what is for me basically the biggest architectural model i ever built. As the workshop is really in the middle of nowhere (it's in the woods), it meant that i would just go home to sleep a couple hours.

Right...with that out of the way (i opened a bottle of champagne to celebrate this, and i don't even drink alcohol)...it is time to get back to business.

The Landspeeder models have taken far too long. Progress was slow, and came grinding to a halt when i was doing this architectural model. I'm in the proces of moving all the pieces, molds and materials to the new workshop, and should have everything settled there early next week. Then i can make a good assesment of what needs to be done to complete this once and for all.

Everything (all kits and builtups) will be sent out on the 18th of May, with (photo)updates on friday the 4th, and friday the 11th of May. If your kit or built-up is not send out by the 18th, i will refund you your advance payment.

I do sincerely apologize about the lack of communication, that was unintentional. And no, i'm not ripping you off, even though the silence will have given you that thought, for which i am really sorry...

On a brighter note...Tonight i will be off to London (was actually supposed to go a couple days ago, but work preventd that), and will be back on sundaymorning...as tomorrow i'll be helping out a little bit with setting up the new Star Wars prop exhibit in London.

I hope all of you are very fine, and very well. Once again my sincere apologies.

All the very best,

Wasili
