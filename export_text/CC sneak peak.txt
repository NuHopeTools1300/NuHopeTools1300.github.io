Well, I just received a kit and let me first say that the castings are immaculate and very crisp! This thing is incredibly cast, George is a master! I think I only found two minor bubbles on the whole kit. 

The hulls are masterfully rotocast, light yet sturdy. The cockpit area on both pods will need to be dremeled out in order to insert the cockpit tubs. That should be an easy process. The engine slot will also need to be dremeled out to slide in the length of the engine support. Epoxy from within should secure the pods to the engine cross member length.

The engine cross member has two solid metal rods set in the length of the casting for support and no way will that ever sag.

The figures also look incredible.

This is going to be one fun project to build and paint and is a superior resin kit especially when compared to some sub par resin kits out on the market.
