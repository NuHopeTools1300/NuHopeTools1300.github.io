all of these are good suggestions. mcmaster-carr carries a product called extension springs in lengths of 20-36". i used their smallest size (1/8") which was perfect for a project i was doing.

bead-fx.com carries some interesting-looking coiled wire, although i'm not sure of the diameters. here's a link to a shot: http://www.beadfx.com/images/h-fwsh.jpg

unfortunately i forget the name, but there's also a coiled type of wire made in india that comes in really small diameters. i was searching for it one night, but the name's escaping me at the moment. edit: sadi wire

and of course jewelers have a special winding tool they use to make coiled wire for jewelry. the great thing about that is you could make your own custom sizes.
