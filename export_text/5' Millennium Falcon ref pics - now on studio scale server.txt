Omes, yeah, I lucked out.  I'm six foot so it wasn't hard to slide the cam across the top of the case. I had to polish it a bit first though, kinda greasy. Getting underside pics was harder, the autofocus on this 2001 model cam was confused by perspex and the flash worked when it wanted rather than when I wanted. I took a couple of pics with a ruler in the hope that'd help with scaling for the model. Really wanted to take more but the crowds made it impossible. Unfortunately in the chaos I oriented the camera vertically and cropped the edges of the case, which would have been useful. I meant to take measurements of the case itself, but again, in the chaos...oh well, model's on an angled mount anyway.
