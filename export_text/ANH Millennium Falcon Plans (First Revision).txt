The following might be of some help for extrapolating measurements and proportional comparison. 

I "scanned" the Entex Wankel kit parts on a flatbed scanner at 600 dpi. While a flatbed scanner has some slight parallax shift along the minor axis, I find photography introduces too much parallax error for accurate measurements.

For what I'm currently doing with my overall '5 ft' MF measurement survey, I'm only interested in the overall part "footprint" - the cross-section that is lying flat on the scanner glass.

I come up with an overall part length of 8.132 inches.
