There are few colour high-rez shots of the ANH model. There are the black and white ones, apparently taken by Dennis Muren in 1978, that were taken square-on. Many of these are excellent, though the ones at funny angles have problems with depth of field. Some of these were republished in the Japanese Chronicles book, which is great.

There are also tons of digital photos taken by enthusiasts at exhibitions. These are extremely high-rez - they're just of the post-ESB five foot Falcon which has experienced some damage over the years, in addition to the ESB modifications.

Between the two you can get a pretty decent view of what the ship looked like in 1977. Just not perfect.
