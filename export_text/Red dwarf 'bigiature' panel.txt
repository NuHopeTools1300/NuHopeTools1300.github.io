So i appreciate work has stalled a bit on this while i gather kits and identify parts etc, so i decided to make a larger test piece to use a few of the spare parts from the north cormorant kits i have and also to try a few more weathering and painting techniques. 

I used the previous test piece in this new panel

[IMAGE: EB1246B9-F8C1-4E15-99BD-9BB5C5E3E866.jpg]

Got the first layer of red primer on

[IMAGE: D326D813-FAFD-4856-B162-6272497A67A0.jpg]

Masked and painted the white area where the red dwarf name will go, i decided that this will be a cut away panel and will only show part of the name, its going to hang on my wall so kinda wanted it to be a thing that people have to work out what it is. 

[IMAGE: 4406E57D-77BA-4454-BDC3-F083D4B3CB44.jpg]

I put part of the text on, i think this size of text fits in with the airfix astronauts i have 

[IMAGE: 4406E57D-77BA-4454-BDC3-F083D4B3CB44.jpg]

I skipped a step in the pictures because I got into it and for got to take any but i weathered by masking off areas and dusting with black powder paint then fixing it with tamiya flat clear spray, a quick rub with wire wool and then mask different areas and repeat, i only did this twice and believe I have it right. It feels the right colour to me , any more weathering and it may get too dark, I may still do a bit more with the steel wool in some areas 

[IMAGE: 104BCAA3-29D4-4CE0-96B8-B537FCD609B9.jpg]

But wait, someone is missing . I did a little hand painting on the area at the bottom of the 'R' and also kept it masked so it looks freshly painted. I sourced an airfix 1:72 astronaut kit which has some really good figures in it at just the right scale for this job . 

Ill post some close ups of the figure when I get them but i painted it in matte black first , then silver , then flat clear, i also ended up doing a black wash on him to give the suit that grungy look. 

[IMAGE: 565BB130-569B-431C-944A-71AD683FC61F.jpg]

All in all very happy with how it turned out, and I've kept the project moving along . I do feel that I may be shelving the main project until more parts are identified . But i do have enough spare parts to build quite a few of these in the mean time ;-)

here's a final pic with all the paint dried hanging on the wall
