While I'm working on getting all of the above components gathered, I wanted to put a couple questions out to the folks who have already worked on similar builds. I noticed there was some discussion of which tank kits to use for the tread detail parts for the aft end between the impulse drives and the leading edge of the nacelle strut details just forward of the nacelle pylons on the main hull. My question is this:

Just want to make sure I'm grabbing the right ones.

Alright, got word from the shop that the top and bottom plates for the main hull have been cut. I now have 5 of 6 donor kits in hand, but one was a bust as the front half of the part from the 1/35 Crusher Joe Powered Suit kit was broken off and missing, on BOTH parts. So had to source another.

Waiting on its replacement plus the sixth donor kit to come in, then I should have everything I need to get started. Minus the super-rare Bandai Deformed Hero Series Xabungle, of course. Had one (thanks to ProfKSergeev) once upon a time, but I've been looking all throughout the house and can't seem to find the few parts I'd already stowed away years ago for this project. Just my luck, lol.

In other news, I think I may have identified another detail part that I don't believe I saw discussed elsewhere. It's the small extra detail sitting at the front of the part from the 1/100 Valkyrie kit that goes in the cut-out at the rear of the TMP smoothie saucer:

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/parts_map_b2n-jpg.1829171/]

The stock part from the Valkyrie kit doesn't have anything there; it's just flat. The prop department had to have added that detail there. Comparing the above pic to the donor kits I have, I believe the added detail is the small ship (Part #5) from the smallest of three sprues from the 1/2600 Guramu-Zan kit.

Thought about adding that little bit of info to the NCC-7100 Stargazer thread, given all the discussion therein about parts identification for this model, but as that hasn't been active for 7 years figured that would be a serious necro-post so decided against that  
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
 Figured it would be just as beneficial posting about it in my own WiP thread, so here we are.
