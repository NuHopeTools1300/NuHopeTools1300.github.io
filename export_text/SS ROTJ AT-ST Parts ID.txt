Click to expand...

Break, actually that's not because of the weight but of the design of the parts which plays with the maleability of the alloy, which is not the case of the plexi. They broke where the parts should "pinch" the joint rod, in some case that broke where I threaded the plexi because the thickness of the plexi was thin on the legs (5 or 6mm) and the screws were a bit more than 3mm in a diameter, that doesnt leave a lot of meat for strengh...
