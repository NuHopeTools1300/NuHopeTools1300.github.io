Hi guys been awhile since I have posted. A little update on my ongoing projects. Still working hard on my snowspeeder but have gotten caught up in this endless loop of building and rebuilding parts in an attempt to get it perfect. It has been tedious but it is still coming along. Just wanted to share a project with you that I have just about finished. I decided to replicate the prop so the cross tube will be visible through the tie's window frame. The building of the model is finished and the pics show it with a couple of primer coats on. I just have to add the lighting, I have the LEDs ready to go. I also have to glue on the flak seats to the bomb tube and the stuka parts but I'll put these on after colour coats. I was hoping to get some advice on paint colour. The reference shows it to be standard Tie Fighter colour, do you think Tamiya haze gray would be best or should it be bluer? I also have a request, I need a couple of parts from the Tamiya Vosper, I am looking for A5 or A6 and a D26.

Thanks again and let me know what you think.

Lee,

  Speaking of springs (leaf springs), the Titan 90 was also used on the chicken, remember? Either way, though, all the 1/25 trucks have similar parts that just so happen to have a center hole diameter of .75" (19.05mm). 

Similarly, a while back I estimated the largest ring at 1.37" (34.8mm) in diameter after placing the Leopold and Karl parts on the inner wing for reference. Oddly enough, the Freuhauf flatbed has a part (below) which just happens to measure 1.375"! Not a complete match on other dimensions but at least it's an indicator that there are parts out there that are very close.
