Post #8  (forum post #2885171)
Author    : moffeaton
Date      : May 5, 2013 at 4:08 PM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

They are silkscreened with a clear carrier - essentially what sytrene model kits have! Not ALPS or laser printed.

The only caveat is that I'm limited to seven screens/colors, so some of the subtle shading differences get "homogenized", which has apparently cheesed some people off. I try my best to strike a balance with what looks "best" across the board (for example, if there's more "bright yellow" to "dull yellow" on the sheet, the dull is going to be bright. Sucks for a few decals here and there, but eeeeeh we live in an imperfect world filled with imperfect people).
