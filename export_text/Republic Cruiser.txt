Sure,     the only thing is my Drawings are done in Cad and i just usually draw out the basic structure ( top and side)  and then i break the ship down into individual parts that will be made as assemblies  and do separate drawings  for each part.  LIke for the Salon pod  and engine.  docking ports and antenna array.  and i also  design the internal frame as a separate drawing.  Once that is done i will build each part and then start to detail the parts with the reference pics.  then start to fit all the parts to the main structure.   I find its alot easier to keep the drawings simple unless theres some details that need to be spaced and fit,  Like cut outs and other symmetric details.
