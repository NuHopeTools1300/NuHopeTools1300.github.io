I just ordered one today and it cost $311.62 by Fedex International Priority courier.

I believe the other option when I checked out was Fedex International Economy courier at  around a $850, which doesn't sound very economical to me.

I am guessing the International Priority is Ground shipping and the International Economy is air shipping. They give you the option of one or the other when you check out.

Hope that helps.
