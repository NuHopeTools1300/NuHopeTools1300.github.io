Not to be redundant, but yes Jesse's Kit is by far the best.

I've had both his kit and the Kit offered by Monsters in Motion.

I am afraid I can not recommend the Monsters in Motion kit at all.

While I have had good dealings with them in the past. 

The Viper kit that I got (indirectly) was cast from molds that had already gone "south" In addition to that, the details that were added are not very well done and inaccurate.  

Though this kit does shares a common "ancestor" with Jesse's it is much further removed from the original Filming miniature

The Launch tube,and sled that comes with MIM's kit however was much better in quality. I believe they are willing to sell the Base/Launch-tube components separately should you wish to have a "background" for your Viper display.

Jesses is the Viper "Guru" and makes a most excellent kit!

I may be able to post some old pictures for comparrison
