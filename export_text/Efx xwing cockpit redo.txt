I have to have an EFX XWING Missing a cockpit.

(Dont ask why)

So I molded one, to complete it...but then thought"Wouldnt it be cool to CHANGE it?

THis is what I have so far.

[IMAGE: 1227002255.jpg]

Holes can be drilled into the top, if  you wanted to LIGHT that area, but I was going after the Screen and gauges.

Ill get  JT Graphics to make a cool transparent decal.

Its probably more accurate to the VIDEO Game version of the XWING, but I like the look. I also like the gauges as to me, they pay homage to the studio scale filmed model in a strange sorta way. 

Im also thinking of redoing the canopy.  If you shave the front cockpit floor panel down (see pic) , the seat can be lowered, and Lukes head does hit the glass.

So the Canopy can be made smaller.  I think its slightly oversized.

When I get that far I will update ya.
