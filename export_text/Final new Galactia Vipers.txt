IT'S HERE. IT'S HERE. IT'S HERE............

DAAAAMMMMMMMMMMM... All I have to say is that regardless of what science fiction subject, you HAVE to get this kit, if you are holding off to think about this, don't - buy it now - this is the BEST mastered garage kit I have ever seen.

The casting is great, the details are great, the assembly is well thought out and well executed, the clear parts are superb, the decals are sharp the base is rich looking, what can I say????

I LOVE this kit.  :love 

It is worth every penny and was well worth the wait.  
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
 

Out of the box, the first thing I examined was the body, little did I know what kind of treat I was in-store for. The body is hollow and is held together with alignment pins, too cool. After this, I was so excited to open the two parts bags to examine everything. Moe went to town on this thing, I was looking at the landing gear legs and the seat components, dam. this thing is TOO COOL.

Originally I know Frank was going to wait on the instructions, buy man, these things are fantastic too, full color and well printed. 

As Frank mentions in his letter with the kit, it is not flawless, I am googly eyed, but can still see, their are a few, and let me emphasize "few" bubble, such as in the ends of alignment pins - which won't show, and I had a small one on one of the gun mounts that can "easily" be fixed. This is one garage kit that can easily be built right out of the box without doing the typical repair / restore work.

Thank you Morten, Mark, Jeff, and Frank, you guys really outdid yourselves.  :lol
