Post #19  (forum post #416498)
Author    : REL
Date      : Sep 16, 2007 at 12:23 PM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

I know it shows up as browns in the pictures, but it's actually golds, bronzes and greenish silver. It really shines in person.

The whole thing was painted with Metallic Sahara Gold mixed with a little black to darken it, then I laid the aztec masks down.

[IMAGE: kkd7_03.jpg]

Then I sprayed the surface with Metallic Solid Bronze mixed with silver and white for the lighter aztecs. Then the entire surface was sprayed with a mix of silver, white and transparent green.
