I believe it's not just because of the fin but also because of the placement of the pylon attachment socket on the inner pieces. The Constellation nacelle pylons go right out what would normally be the "bottom" of the nacelles (albeit not centered) instead of at an angle like on the Constitution. The bulge surrounding the mounting point, evident on inner halves, is also not there.

A skilled modeler could adapt inner pieces to match, sure. But it's avoidable labor if you just use the outer halves.
