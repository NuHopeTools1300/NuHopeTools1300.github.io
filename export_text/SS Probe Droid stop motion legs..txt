Author    : ralphee
Date      : Jan 13, 2012 at 11:15 PM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Thanks Stu, its mainly the joint fabrication that hurts you. This set uses a dowel to hold the three joint rings, instead of a pinch bolt, which is overkill IMO anyway.

Using the dowel method, you have to trim/grind just enough meat off the inside of your ABS ring, to give the right tension over the inner dowel.

Then, keep in mind, those rings want to bind when they run together when assembled, so yeah, the joints are tedious.

Its cool when you nail a couple, and your able to just jump into building a full arm.

If i had a bag of 200 centre drilled plexi discs i could just bolt together and make joints, i could build these arm sets all day long......then again :lol.
