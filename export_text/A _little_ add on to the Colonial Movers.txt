Click to expand...

Nothing cuts in this process. Cutting is subtractive, while 3D printing is additive. It deposits plastic on an xy plane one z-layer at a time. There are other processes though, which are slightly different, but I don't think any of them cut. That would be what a CNC mill would do, out of a solid, pre-existing piece.
