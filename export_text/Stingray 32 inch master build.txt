Click to expand...

Nope just an open pour mould, I think the type of resin you use is important, As for being Tacky, unless your casting into a hard mould then thats almost allways going to be the case.  Its not a simple shape to do so a softer shore is a must. I have found that after a few days they lose the tackyness but Out of the mould they look like drink glasses :lol Somebody told me that if you put it in the oven at a low temp it looses tackyness alltogether :confused
