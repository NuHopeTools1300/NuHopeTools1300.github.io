This past fall I researched and scratch built this studio scale Martian electronic eye that appears in the farmhouse sequence in George Pal's 1953 THE WAR OF THE WORLDS. It lights up and has sound as well. It was included in a booth at our Comic-Con where we had Ann Robinson the actress who played Sylvia Van Buren in the original film.
