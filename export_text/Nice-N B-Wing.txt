Well, when it came to starting the painting, Guy was away in Japan or somewhere,....I couldn't wait till he came back, so I ordered Tamiya AS20,....I'd used Archive X on a DeAgo Falcon & it was beautifully smooth & a dream to airbrush,....so using the AS20 rattle can was a horrible experience,...splattery, thick & very slow to dry.....I then mixed my own colour for the blue/grey areas with Vallejo acrylics.....still couldn't get it right,...too dark,....but pretty quickly I got the proper stuff from Guy......then things began to get better for me

Yes Graham,...I drilled out the joints using a 6mm drill,....pretty scary,....but luckily no fractures,....I bought some 6mm copper tube & a 4mm brass rod that fits inside the tube....to tighten, I bought 3mm allen grub screws, to go through the copper & grab the brass

So 3 grub screws on each wing.....I'll take some more pics soon
