I realise this is an ancient thread and that you all may know this already kits parts used ( just my best guess so far) 

Airfix

oo/ho Girderbridge main Trusses and smaller support for Mole body

possibly oo/ho Footbridge

Revell

Everything is Go! Atlas Rocket  Atlas Rocket halves on Truss

1/72 X-15 decals

Aurora 

1/76 Convair Hustler. Wing leading edge on Mole Body above retraction tracks

                                   Engine Halves on Chassis fenders with undercarriage parts

                                   External payload nose either end of retraction tracks on side of Mole body

1/48 F5 Freedom Fighter. Decals

Faller HO Cafe/Garage.     Fence - detail on Mole Body

                                           Inner roof rear  of Mole body

The elephant in the corner of the room is the Victory Vickers Vigor tractor toy.

anyone attempted a parts map?

I'm still looking for sources for the silver coves, the round silver  side ports on the mole body and the two 'nozzels' at the front of age chassis
