FIRST PIC

Yellow - Two pieces. The bottom one I don't know. On top of it is a piece cut out of the Hasegawa 1:72 Sherman bogie wheel part.

Red - Bandai 1:48 Tiger 1 (see attachment), plus Bandai 1:24 Shiden part (hard to make out in the pic, but it's near the center of the sprue).

Purple - Don't know.

Green - Part of it is the side of a scratchbuilt box. On the side of that box is a piece from the Bandai 1:48 Panther G engine (other side of the engine on the other side of this box). Behind that you have a piece from the big Bandai 1:24 Panther (get your wallet ready).

Blue - Don't know.

Orange - Don't know.

SECOND PIC

Blue - From one of the Airfix 1:24 planes - Messerschmitt, Hurricane, Harrier, or Mustang. Can't remember which one.

FOURTH PIC

Purple - Hasegawa 1:72 Gasoline Tank Truck (I didn't find this one, but it was posted publicly a while back).
