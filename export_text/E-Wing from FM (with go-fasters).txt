Hi all,

The kit is expertly molded by Frank Cerney, and mastered by Morten Moeslund aka Moe. This is my first post with pics, I hope it goes right...

Just thought I'd post this E-Wing I'm doing. It has some added intakes and a rear vertical wing system that reflects the slant of the nose. I'm trying to make it seem more sleek and not cut off in the rear like the usual x-wing.

I also reworked the cannons to have some cooling fins and some rod-like additions like the x-wing cannons. I put them on the wing tips to separate them from the engines more than in the original kit configuration.

Any feedback is welcome. I'm leaning towards a red color scheme with gray accents.
