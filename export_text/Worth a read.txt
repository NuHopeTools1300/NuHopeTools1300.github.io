I realize everyone is upset with me, and I'm not sure where to begin with this, only to say I way over extended myself and fell into the same trap that a lot of garage kit makers do, thinking I could make something in a week or so, and not coming through, then not charging enough, then the money is gone, and then I have to take on new stuff to pay for the old. It was especially worse in my case since that was all I was doing for a living, so when it came to the electric being turned off, or taking on a new commission, I just did it. So I'm in a position now where I'm years behind on people's projects. 

As for Max's post. Most of what Max sent me was unusable very grainy 3d printed material that was extremely low in resolution, a lot of you know this from when these projects started.

This was the majority of what was sent to me by Max, I just couldn't do anything with it. So at most I used it as a reference to scratchbuild one, and then not even then since they were 3d printed from a mesh he would take off other websites. 

http://the-grey-ghost.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/31kkting2.jpg 

Those that were there when we were developing the Slave know this. 

I basically had to rebuild everything, the few things I did use turned out ok, like the Slave body, but originally what was sent to me was total garbage, again, as some of you know that were there and saw the finished result. So, as a result of the things he sent me, I had to put hours and hours of work into, eventually in 90% of the cases just starting over on it when I could have scratch built it easier. Everything Max sent me I could have made myself, I have that ability, but I was trying to help him out and get him some money so he could pay for those expensive machines he bought, since his wife was complaining about how much he spent on them. I considered him a friend, so I didn't tell him his work wasn't up to molding standards. He would usually go on places like Sci Fi Meshes, download a mesh, then print it out and send it to me to make into some kind of kit. Well it didn't take long to realize it didn't work, so we would be on the phone for hours trying to come up with another plan, then another, then another, until eventually I was so far behind with these projects I couldn't see daylight. 

The Defiant was ok, but the body shape was all wrong so I had to rebuild that, which wasn't Max's fault as the mesh was wrong and we didn't realize it until it was already made. That's another mesh that was downloaded from Sci Fi Meshes and was wrong.

Mustang, that kit you sent me completely warped out of shape because the resin was bad, you knew this, so I don't know why you're attacking me. I will post pictures of your shuttle to show people what it looks like. And I never said I didn't get them, they were delivered to my neighbor's house, also you sent me castings made with heavily tinted clear resin that's still weeping to this day, I noticed when you started selling them in the Junkyard you switched to regular resin. The castings were worthless. 

Sovereignreplicas.com is not down, I had my domain with ATT/Yahoo, they split and Yahoo cancelled all the accounts that didn't sign the new user agreement, well I didn't get that email, or it was lost in the spam folder, so last month I go to check my email and the site was gone. I have now got it back, but there's still a problem with the email. If you want to email me you can at rda2012 at gmail.com 

I haven't read the whole thread yet, I'm going to do that now, but all I can say is my absence was due mostly to try and work and get these projects that I've owed for years caught up the best I can. I haven't bailed on any of you and I will eventually come through, I'm very sorry for taking so long.

edit:

Max didn't just send me stuff to make into kits unsolicited, I asked him to, so I take responsibility for that, hoping something he sent me could be used, he did do a lot of work at his own expense, my mistake was not being honest with him to spare his feelings as I didn't have the heart to tell him his stuff was bad. If I could go back in time I would have just told him I can't do anything with this material for the most part and went on, that's my failing that I have a lot of trouble saying no to people. So I can understand why he's mad.

Caveat: I'm a newbie here, I have no relationships with anyone either on RPF, or on this particular thread.  I'm pretty much new to modelling, after a very long hiatus (25 years).  

Well, I came across this thread, cos I was curious about the 1:650 scale Enterprise E - I was hoping to one day own one of those suckers.  Well, Googling yesterday, I found Commander Max's blog post yesterday and read it with interest.  Prior to this, I did not know REL was connected to Sovereign Replicas.  I can say that a few months back, I had some interesting conversations about Sovereign Replicas with another person via EMail and I was warned to stay away - this particular person was not Max, but did tell me that they lost nearly 60k USD correcting problems with Enterprise D kits sourced from Sovereign Replicas, that were clearly in production via Sovereign Replicas, despite REL's post to the contrary.  This particular person was extremely vehement in his dislike of Sovereign Replicas I might add - I will keep this person's name out of this though.  This person had not gotten any compensation back from Sovereign Replicas and according to them, was out of pocket by aforementioned 60K USD.

Now, since I work in IT, I was curious to see the failure notice when trying to send an EMail to rel@sovereignreplicas.com.  So, I've done some research and shall share that with the community, along with my notes...

Firstly, a WHOIS lookup on the sovereignreplicas.com domain name:

Macintosh:~ melkor$ whois sovereignreplicas.com

A few notes here...DNS hosting is with Yahoo.  The domain registrar is also Melbourne IT, which is Australian based.  I'm immediately suspicious as to why a US based individual would register a domain with an Australian domain registrar, especially one that is not cheap (background, I work for an ISP as a tech, my background is with domains, DNS and hosting).  US domain registrars would be far cheaper than Melbourne IT.  I also note that the registrant for the domain is Dana Long, and that privacy contacts have been enabled for EMail contacts.  A PO Box is present and a phone number is published.

I then proceeded to do a "dig" (DNS lookup on UNIX like systems, replaces the RFC deprecated nslookup command) for "MX" records (Mail Xchanger records):
