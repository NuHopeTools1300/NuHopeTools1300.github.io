I had such great memories of this show when I was a kid.  After watching the DVDs now, it is pretty campy, but the effects were quite incredible for TV back then.

So, I've had this studio scale kit for a couple of years, and finally busted it out and had some fun with it.  Unfortunately, the overall kit was in pretty poor shape, I think it was one of the last ones out of the molds.  Rather than working the entire Sat V halves, I decided to scratch build my own section. So the main body and rear engine is the original kit. 

I grabbed a SatV kit and added new 'docking ports', adding the C channel detail on the ports that appeared to be there on the filming miniature.  I also put my own domes on there, as well as the connecting T channel, and the parts near the vertical stabilizer. The cylinder is also scratched as well as a few other details.  the side 'wings' on the fuselage and this section were also scratch built from styrene.

The kit did not come with waterslide decals, so I drew those up and printed them out, they worked out pretty good.  I think there was a version of the kit with a clear engine so you could light it, but mine didn't have that, so I painted an engine glow effect. Lastly, there was one episode where the number 3 was shown, and I felt it gave it much more character and added it.    I'm glad to have this kit, and add it to the studio scale collection. The last photo is one of the original model.

Enjoy

-Sean
