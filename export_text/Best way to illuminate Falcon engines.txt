I'd say go for battery powered LED's because a) they

give off the right blueish white light you need for

the falcon, b) they are cool and won't melt your

model, and c) the batteries will last a long time.

I would suggest putting the LED's in an array behind some

kind of diffusing material otherwise you will just see a bunch of lights rather than the collective glow of the engine.

It all depends on what you're comfortable with.

If you want a look that's as close as possible to how it looks on film, use a cold cathode tube. However, these take a lot of energy to run, and being glass you have to worry about breakage.

If you want absolute reliability and lifespan, go with an array of LEDs with a diffuser in front of them. I believe VoodooFX (amongst a couple others) sell these if you are unable to put it together youself.
