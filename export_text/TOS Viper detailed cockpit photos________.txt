Most of the "pilot in cockpit" shots were done on this little "forced perspective" Viper, which sort of resembles a kiddie ride. In these scenes it's being used as a "simulator" to train new pilots.

[IMAGE: BSG-FPset.jpg]

[IMAGE: BSG-FPviper2.jpg]

Note how the canopy and "engines" all tip back together... this was never intended to be seen on screen, obviously.

[IMAGE: BSG-FPvipercockpit.jpg]
