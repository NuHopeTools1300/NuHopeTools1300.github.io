────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

If it is built using a brass frame and aluminum parts it should be in around 35 lbs. The are a few resin based cages and spine assemblies out there that come in about 20 lbs. The resin may sag over time though so constant base support would likely be required. Brass is SS accurate but the cost and work is much higher. This rear engine assembly alone weighs in at around 8 lbs. Not mine but similar. Mine is still in pieces!

[IMAGE: eaglerearenginesolderrear.jpg]

[/IMG]

Once again. A really nice addition to any collection. How are you going to display it? Coffee table style. The acrylic case alone is enough to hurt the wallet!
