Actually, the show was called "Walter Cronkite's Universe". It aired for a couple seasons. Walter did the show as part of his post CBS Evening News contract as he wanted to do more then just straight news. One of the other programs had him on the set of Tron as they composited an all new sequence just for him to demonstrate what they were doing with the high contrast positive and negative compositing. After he passed away last year, CBS news aired that clip again as part of their news memorial.
