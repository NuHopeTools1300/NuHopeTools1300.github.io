WOW!! :$

So many responses!! Geez, thanks everyone, most kind!!!

Rob/Jim,

The 'cockpit' turned out to be a total scratchbuild. I couldn't get the very front most lip right. Looking top down, it looks almost dead on, but front on, it doesn't have that nice 'spoonish' shape.  After scrutinizing the black and white pics though, I was more and more convinced it was an 'enclosed' cockpit, not the clear 'spoon' in the restorations.

It's not perfect, but it works for me.

Gene, yeah, Ryan is right, I've used our Pool table for over 3 years now as my 'workbench'. I think my wife is DEFINITELY nearing the end of her patience. I promised to clean up for a couple weeks at least, so she knows what that means!!   Yup, another ship is coming, but at least we'll get a few games of pool in before it disappears for a while!!

Thanks again for the comments!!!!
