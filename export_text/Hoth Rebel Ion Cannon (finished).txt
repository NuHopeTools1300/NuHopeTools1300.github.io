Thanks for so much positive reactions 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]

To answer the questions,

1.The paintjob and weathering:

   The Basecolour is a mix of Tamiya flat white and grey followed by a mix of 

   some Vallejo Model Air Colours that are airbrushed very thinned to give it a

   little dirty-brown used look.

   Last step was the dark streakes, i made it with black pastel chalk and  

   two fine brushes.

   Then a coat of Testors flat clear.

2.The Base:

   is made of Styrodur and gypsum.
