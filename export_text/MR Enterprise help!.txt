Cycle the power multiple times, see if that wont jar it loose,

Try vibrating that nacelle motor externally. Pad the nacelle with paper towel or something so you do not scratch it and hold something that vibrates against it. Cycle the power concurrently.

An electric tooth brush would probably work as a vibrating device. No jokes guys! LOL

Maybe warming it up too, a hair dryer but only from a safe distance to slowly warm it up.
