So that's it...

The markings were done on the bigger pod just with tamiya tape over the base colour and dark grey over that, remove tape and hey presto !

The smaller one was a bit of a faff, airbrushed black through masks, then a sharpie for the thin lines...thanks to Lee for that idea...then just a misting of the base colour over all to tone it down and get rid of the sheen of the sharpie marker..
