If I understand it right, the Gold Leader will not be released as a kit, only as a display model from EFX. The other Y's (Triangles, Tiger Sprocket and MOM), however, are planned as a kit release through nicenmodeldesigns. According to their website the models were aimed to be ready by April 2015 but at the moment they seem to still be work-in-progress with no further information on their release or pricing.

I have the Nice-N Red Jammer Y-wing (still in progress), my experience on SS modeling is limited but I have nothing but good things to say about the kit. It's effin' beautiful!

There was another release of a studio sized Y-wing recently. The Galactic Resin Models Y-wing raised some controversy here in the RPF, I believe the overall size is about right but detailing leaves much to hope for and requires significant amount of work to get it to match the studio models. 

With a little patience, the junkyard might pay off as well, although I think unbuilt Red Jammers are very, very rare by now.
