Finished my AT-ST Studio Scale replica from Empire Strikes Back (not Return of the Jedi) 

It's a 'fun' build, but not for your wallet 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]

Many thanks to all that helped in one way or another, couldn't have done it without you! Niels, Marvin, Scott J, Guy, Quincy, Jason, Allan, Lee, Julien, Weili, and MakerLab Austria.

This is very much a material mix, much like the original. Main difference on mine are the laser-cut acrylic legs in place of the metal stop-motion armature. 

For this one I did very little research as it was already done by the hard work of others.
