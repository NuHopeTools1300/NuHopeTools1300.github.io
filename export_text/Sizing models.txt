A simple google search can usually provide the results you are looking for.  For instance, a search of "Slave 1 dimensions" would provide the following result from the Wookieepedia site:

Length:  21.5m     Width:  21.3m

21.5 x 39.37  = 846.455" or 70' 6.45" full size length

846.455 / 34 = 24.895" scale length

1/34 scale x 150% = 37.34" long at 1/22.66 scale
