Yeah, that's because it was never there on the actual filming miniature. Unfortunately, the owner had some of his models restored, and the restorations didn't quite live up to the original parts, this being one example. As you can see from the Flat Top under construction here, the nacelles were actually Leopold Railgun cannons, and the center structure was part of the big Centurion tank kit.  Kudo's to the owner for trying to recreate the original part, but unfortunately the new owner (if there is one) gets a less than accurate filming miniature.
