Just wanted to let people know that those who live in the Los Angeles area can see these models and more at the Skirball Cultural Center's Star Trek: Exploring New Worlds exhibit. They also have a large 1701-D that I believe was used in Star Trek Generations, as well as the Borg cube from First Contact. Most of the pieces are from the collection of Paul Allen.

I can't tell you how neat it was to actually see these incredible models in person, especially the K'T'inga. The whole exhibit was very well done, go if you can you will not be disappointed!!
