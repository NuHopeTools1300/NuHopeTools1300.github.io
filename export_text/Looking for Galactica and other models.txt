RKW, Chris Pappas is friends with Ed and he and Ed are working together on those projects. No need to worry about Chris and his site.

The Raider that Mike and Jesse are doing is going to be more accurate in that it will include a few parts not found at the time I did the Raider for Jim Key that Jesse bought and after the fact I found out a few other bits of info that helped figure out the body shape that I had to sculpt and I was able to get my hands on a pyro from the real molds that is helping to make things even better. I'm involved with it as well. Jesse and Mike have great kits! The Chris/Ed ones are build ups only and are off the hero's matched in color in everyway possible.
