The gentleman who worked on the original kit is a member here.  Am I getting the name right:  Anibuspro?  As he pointed out in another thread, that kit was built in a hurry and before all of the reference material came out.  So for what the original sculptors had to work from, it was for it's time an awesome kit, in my opinion.

On the other hand, they are a good deal of work to build.  I think the casting quality dropped off since MIM took over the production of the kit.

And as others have stated here, save yourself a world of hurt, avoid MIM and wait for Randy Cooper to finish his kit.

Just my .02 cents.
