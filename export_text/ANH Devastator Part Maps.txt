So far it is consistent with the info I have.

But I suggest you extend the green color of the big Akagi parts to the end. It's all the same piece with styrene strips glued on top.

Also, amongst the not-yet colored greeblies I can see a bunch of bits donated by Tamiya's 1/700 Scharnhorst:

- the dome with the little cannons, on top of the 8rad piece (part nº12)

- next to it, the set of four double-barrel cannons (part nº4)

- the two cranes (part nº31)

by the way, on Scalemates.com the Gneisenau appears as another version of the Scharnhorst, with some new parts. I don't have both kits so don't quote me on that but I'm sure many of the parts needed can be found on either one.

EDIT: I just checked my Scharhorst kit and I found a funny thing: the sprue where the cranes are located has the name "Gneisenau" carved on it! I wouldn't go so far as to say that you don't need the Gneisenau to build the Devastator, but as of today, I've found all the greeblies I need on the Scharnhorst.
