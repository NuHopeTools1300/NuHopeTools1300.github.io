Some scans on another site:

GLENCOE Lunar Lander

Parts 13 & 17 are the wing-tip blaster cannon of the Malmori fighter.  Specifically, the lower landing legs of the Moonship.

The Moonship girders were also used in the Valkyrie fighter on the top and ventral sides.  The Moonship Command Modules were used - 12 of them! - as the cargo pod sides on Cowboy's hauler.
