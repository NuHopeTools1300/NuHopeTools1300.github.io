If ya can't get Jesse's (Jestefarean) REAL casting of the Viper,.. then I suggest Monsters In Motion's kit which was slightly modified in the rear engine region and has a launch ramp. Find it here:

http://www.monstersinmotion.com/catalog/pr...products_id=687

It's $175...I have one and love it. Keep in mind that the REAL castings are owned by Jesse though is case you want the real thing. They are almost Identical aside from the engines region I spoke of. The MiM version has been slightly cleaned up from the original in that area. I can't be quoted on this, because I am not positive, but I had heard that it was Jim Key that cleaned up that area off the original castings that MiM aquired. Then there is the rumor of the ICON castings..blah blah blah... too many non facts. The only real fact I know is Jesse has the most acurrate and real stuff and MiM offers a great kit as well. There is even a 19"...or was it 22" verison in the UK offered that I hear wasn't done very well.

I hope you get what ya want.
