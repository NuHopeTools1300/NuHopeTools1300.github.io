Hi Ganesha7, I had the plans that Neil Ellis drew up back in 2008 I think. I sculpted green foam to create the bucks for the nose and upper hull. Main body was acrylic ribs to create main frame with aluminium tubing for internal supports, then skinned in styrene. Hope this makes some kind of sense.
