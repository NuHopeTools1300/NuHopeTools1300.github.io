Unless you're into counting the rivets on each individual donor kit part and making sure its totally the same (this is a serious statement) you'll be extremely happy with it.  Seriously there are some very minor differences, but unless you're an expert, and I mean REALLY expert (like Tim Ketzer and Co.) You'll e hard pressed to find inaccuracies.  Keep in mind that there were several AT-ATs made for the film and that they are all slightly different so any one picture might not be the one that MR copied, but even then you need to be an expert to tell most of the ILM originals from one another although they are different from one another...

Enjoy your purchase.  I enjoy mine.
