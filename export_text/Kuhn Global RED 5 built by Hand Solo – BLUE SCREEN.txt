Thanks again for the comps guys! 

As for RED 5 questions...

This RED 5 was built up by Bob (Hand Solo). I currently have him doing many build commissions, and I am sure he would be up for more even though his plate is full. 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
 

If you are looking for a great X-Wing guy, he is one of the best for "realism" and correctness of markings (insignia and weathering). 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
 He also achieved a very kewl "carbon scoring" weathering effect rather than the standard effect achieved with most paint ups. It created a very "real" feel to it as if it really could have been in service if it were full sized and real. 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
 I highly recommend his talents, though I feel a bit greedy at times and want him to use them only for the collection! :lol But that would be very wrong of me to do so...LOLOLOL. 

He also built up a FANTASTIC RED 2 for the collection (which will be posted soon with Blue Screen Backdrop).

Later today, you will see more of his work with the Battlestar Galactica TOS "CDR CAIN" Edition Viper I will be posting with Blue Screen Backdrop.

As a serious collector of items that I enjoy showing, there are only a few artists that I gravitate around. Bob (Hand Solo) is one of them. Richard (REL) is another (whos GREAT talents are well documented everywhere! 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
 ), and there is a possible third I am looking at adding to the mix soon. The very talented Matt (Max Zephyr). The beauty of this conglomeration of talent is that they all will be utilized in very different areas of the artform. From scratch building, to sculpting, to outside kit build-ups. I well respect them! 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]

So many of you artists are so FANTASTIC that it makes choosing VERY Difficult!
