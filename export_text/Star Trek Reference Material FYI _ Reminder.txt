The 'U.S.S.' and 'NCC-' section of the decals are the originals from the ST2 Reliant markings. It's why all the later appearances of the model have either a 4-digit registry number, or a 5-digit number with a lot of ones. They were never replaced, so the new numbers had to fit in the same space.
