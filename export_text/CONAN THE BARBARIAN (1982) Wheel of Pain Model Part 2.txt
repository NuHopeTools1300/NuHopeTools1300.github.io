In the first part I made the upper part of the wheel, now we go with the rotating main structure. I will try to make this rotate like in the movie.

So that it can rotate, I will make a metal structure with a main tube and a rotating one. This structure will serve as a skeleton to hold all the other resin pieces, arms, wooden logs, figures, etc.

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/0-jpg.1509003/]

In the next photos you can see two small axes in which the two stones that crushed the grain will be inserted and that will also rotate.

At first I made the stones of wood, after I covered them with sand to get roughness and then I made a mold. Finally I made two copies in polyurethane resin and painted them to pretend to be granite stones like in the movie.
