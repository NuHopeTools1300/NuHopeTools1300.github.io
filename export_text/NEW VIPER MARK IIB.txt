Ok.

I have a question. 

Many of you have bought the VIPER KIT.

Now the APOLLO Run,  is going to have LANDING GEAR.

SO my question is,  do you want the kit with a SEATED APOLLO, or...a standing one.    At First, BOTH would be the obvious answer...but then you will have one figure going to waste. Not to mention, Im trying to keep the price of the kit affordable.  This run will be professionally cast, so its gonna be NICE.

But its also expensive.

The Solution is to make one of the Figures optional, or an accessory.

We might also do a CDR ADAMA Figure, as the kit will have DECALS to make the 

HUSKER Viper, APOLLO VIPER and MAYBE one other...if you can think of a Pilots name.  We are also considering an ALPHABET Sheet with Name plates, so you can make your OWN Names.

The Kit also has:

Uniform wall thickness, so its LITE and ROOMIE for electronics.

CLEAR Instruments and EXHAUST FINs.

Detailed Gear,

New detailed Seat with survival back.

Lowered Floor so you dont have to cut your pilots legs off.

Locator pins on ALL MAJOR  PARTS.

Price: Not as cheap as the STARBUCK, but not over 300, thats all Im promissing.

Please post your thoughts here.

Its your opportunity to have your say.

Thanks...

I would say offer the figure seated standard, this way the builder is covered either way he chooses building  the landing gear up or down.

The standing figure can be an added option.

If you only had the standing figure in the kit and the builder decides to build the ship in flying mode, the standing figure is usless short of doing a conversion to make him seated that some people may not be able to do, or can't handle, due to lack of skill or knowledge to do the task.

I have done enough work with resin kis that this would be no problem and not a big deal, others may not find it so easy and could find it intimidating and limit their building options, thus loosing potentionl buyers.
