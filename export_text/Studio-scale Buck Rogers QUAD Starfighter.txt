You have been prolific of late, haven't you?  Good job.

The actual piece for the filming miniature was nothing more than a hollow shell (not sure if it was vac-formed or if it was assembled from flat sheets) that was placed on top of the main body.  (The narrow canopy was removed and a part of it was glued to the back of the quad.)

There was no relief, save for a couple of detail parts glued on.  All the panel lines were in fact drawn in pencil.  The windows were just painted - including the reflections.

So, if you want to do this - you sorta have to do what Mark did.  The windows do need to be painted black.  Otherwise, you have a LOT of Dremelling to do to make the four seat cockpit.

Neat way to 'pimp' your Starfighter/Thunderfighter.
