Hi all,

I normally stick to the prop forum but i have been lucky enough to get my hands on a Studio Scale 89 Batmobile. This isnt Howards kit but from the same source his original came from, therefore it needs Alot of work.

So far all i have done is blast it with some filler primer, next im going to go over it and fill in all the hundreds of air bubbles and cracks.

I really wanted to display this with the cockpit open with all the dash lit fiber optically and have the front and rear lights working also. I have therefor decided to scrap the interior it came with and scratch build one, im also chucking all the greebles that came with it and they are rubbish castings.

I going to have batman standing next to the car and have been searching for one the right scale but have failed to find one so i have started to sculpt my own.

I have never tried a build like this before so any help would be greatly appriecated.
