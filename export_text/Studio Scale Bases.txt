I finished the studio scale Icons style base for my TIE advanced, so now I have a custom configured base for each of my studio scale ANH fighters.  Next I am going to do one for my Y-Wing, which will be the biggest since I have been making these to be the length of the ships themselves (as you can see with the X-Wing).  I'll post that along with my Y when I finish it, I am still working on adding the lights to it like Jason is with his model.

I used the tiles from the Starship Modeler store along with my own pedestal and extra custom made tile to create templates that I molded and then was able to make into stronger one piece bases.

I got the plaques custom made by www.mpreps.com.

Hope you all enjoy the pics.
