OK guys dont laugh too hard!:lol  I thought i would make my own figures for the MR Falcon. Ive not really attempted to alter figures before so here we go. Han was fairly easy. I bought a set of Dragon 1/35 figures. They have seperate legs , arms heads etc. I noticed that one of the figures looked a little like Harrison Ford so i just trimmed the nose a little. I have carved all of the collar and detail parts off and made it look as though the coat was open a little. I then made his hair from Milliput. I know its not great but im sure it wil look good when its painted!
