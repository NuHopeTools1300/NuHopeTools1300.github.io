Hello: I re-painted my ICONS ID4 Attacker to give it a custom metal/organic look, just like the screen-used miniatures. The ICONS paint job was the base color & then I sprayed metal blue, olive drab, airbrushed dark grey & flat black, as gave a few washes of dark tan before I sealed it with a semi-gloss sealer. I liked the results I got... good luck with your paint job & make sure to share some pics.
