http://www.starwars.com/eu/lit/ref/news20071105.html

Well, I'm working on the Falcon right now.

These aren't blueprints that would help you build a model, but more illustrative and filled with tech specs for the ship.

I've got a ton of reference for the details, but my problem is getting the overall proportions right. I have some top views of different Falcon models, like the ANH top view in Chronicles, but they're distorted by the camera lens and perspective etc.

I'm not looking for a ton of measurements, maybe like a dozen of the top view, and less than that for the side and front.
