As far as I can tell, there were four scales all together. There may have been more, but they are unknown to me.

1. Full size, one to one.

2. 36". Same scale as the big cop car and taxi/proto you see above.

3. 11". Very common on ebay, and even on the RPF. i've got about four or five different styles of this size.

4. 3". Not sure if they were used in filming, but there were a large number of three inch cars made. Possibly as maquettes? I don't know. I have about five or six different styles.

The car pictured in the first pic above is indeed some kind of weird taxi lookalike. I have a couple of those, and it's still a mystery to me. it looks a WHOLE lot like the taxi, but the front grille is different. Maybe they modified this pull into the taxi?? Maybe it's just another 36" car they made?? I dunno. I have a pal who worked on the movie, I should run it by him.

By the way, if anyone wants one of those big taxi things, send me a PM.
