Nathan, cool to hear that you finally snagged an AT-AT. 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
  For the side guns you need to pic up Tamiyas 2 cm Flackvierling. You could also buy the big ass truck which has the same barrels but buing just the gun is going to be cheaper. The kit carries 4 barrels/recievers, you'll need them all. There's still a lot of scratch building involved but I'm sure you'll do more then fine.

Here's a link to the kit you need: http://www.hlj.com/product/TAM35091

Good luck.

Steve.
