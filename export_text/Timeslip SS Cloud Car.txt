I’ve been working on this on and off while waiting for coats of paint, Apoxie Sculpt, and putty to dry. I see a few other cloud car threads so I guess now is a good time as any to post this up.

Enlarged the gunport

[IMAGE: f0a6e7c9-0670-41ce-afd6-2804acfd2c6d_zps1c600d1e.jpg]

Attached the PODs with Apoxie Sculpt, wood, and screws. I ran a red marker down the middle scribe line and used a level to help me line them up.

[IMAGE: 10755af8-c944-4f51-8e2f-e287b2025610_zps0d5c2abc.jpg]

My original plan was to cut off the dashboard section of the cockpit, mold it, and run it in clear so I could light it. After doing all that (with permission), I realized that I didn’t exactly like the way that it was going to turn out, so I went ahead and scratched new dashboards. Depending on how the final fit of the pilots go, this may have been a waste of time. They pre-fit okay, but we shall see.

[IMAGE: c2eb71b3-d658-4067-a99b-d2e19bb41fb0_zps710711fe.jpg]

[IMAGE: aphoto1_zps4f0c2810.jpg]

[IMAGE: aphoto2_zps9e64739a.jpg]

Then I had to dig a small trench for the wires to go through.

[IMAGE: 3d033d27-0eff-448b-a9a2-640e40c48e8a_zps3b574d80.jpg]

This pretty much where she sits right now.
