Those are from the full size prop I think you'll find. I already have those. Having said that though there is a blueprint that shows edlunds notes showing the panel which is to be blown off. The full scale speeders all had this panel in tact. Even lukes model has the panel open on the larger  Pyro version but the full size prop in the snow had the panel intact. In the Making of ESB there are some great pix of the ILM guys with a styrene speeder being made and another with the model parts laid out on the floor. Great pix .
