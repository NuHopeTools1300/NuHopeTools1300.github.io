They are very good. And they are in the U.S. I have purchased many cases from them and only had one that arrived broken, which they replaced immediately. All of my cases have been at least as large as the one stated above... actually...mostly larger.

Regarding the MR Falcon.. the one problem I see and have been mulling over (but haven't asked them about yet) with getting a case for it is the large top space required. This company works mostly (from what I have received from them) with 1/8 inch acrylic (which is probably why their costs are so good). The Falcon base has a 1/4 inch lip. Also,.. due to the large top space and gravity,.. I believe you would really need to get the 1/4 inch thickness in order to prevent cracking from top-middle sagging. I am not certain they offer the 1/4 inch, but it is worth asking about.
