AMS at its worst!....yes I'm crazy :wacko

I noticed that those three round ports near the floor by Helo's station are not 6 cylinder engine gaskets after all...hehehe.

Hmmmm...cooling fan guards huh....then I got to thinking...they are lit up from inside and oh what the heck I just can't control myself!

First I thought there must be something out there in photo etch...like those cooling, blower fan guards used on the top of some diesel locomotives....N scale should do the trick, did some searching and found a set by a company called BLMA...turns out that they have an extensive list of photo etch goodies. The set I found was perfect and in N scale they are 8 mm in dia...the round cooling vents on the Rpator are 3mm...with a bit of triming it will be perfect! The design isn't spot on but at that size...who cares. No train shop near me had them in stock so I checked ebay...found a set with FREE shipping to boot! pretty durn nifty huh?
