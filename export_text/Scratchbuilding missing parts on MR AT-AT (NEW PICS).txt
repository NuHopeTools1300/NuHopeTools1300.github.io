I have just received an MR AT-AT which had the side head guns missing and the rear feet pistons missing.

It also has no display stand or acrylic cover, but i will put it in a real glass case anyway.

Here are the side guns so far:

Far left, the master i made from plastic tube, bits of lego and filler. Next one is a cast taken from a mold of the master. Next is another cast with the kitbashed german flak gun in place and a metal rod fitted through it which fits nicely into the side of the AT-AT's head.

[IMAGE: 1_35.jpg]

The top part of the gun is just resting on here and is about to be molded.

[IMAGE: 2_20.jpg]

Here are two new pistons which i cast from a mold taken from the original. The hole on the inside is where it fits to the peg on the AT-AT's leg. I'm going to replace all four pistons.

[IMAGE: 6_9.jpg]
