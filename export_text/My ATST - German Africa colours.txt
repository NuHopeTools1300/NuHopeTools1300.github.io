Here is a couple of pictures of my ATST. I wanted to create something different than a grey paint scheme, and thought about WW2 German Africa Corp. This is a possible paint scheme by the ATST pilots on Tatooine. The chipping is made with a sponge and tons of drybrushing is applied. My idea was that the pilot had painted the ATST themselves, and didn't bother to clean it first. Just like the German troops did with their tanks. They often was shipped directly from Europe to Africa and then painted there.

The base is made with real sand used in Reptile tanks, bought in a pet store. The sand is mixed with cement and white glue.
