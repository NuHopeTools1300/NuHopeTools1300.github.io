I have seen photos of one person's efforts to build a basestar and know of only one other attempt. There is so little reference available to try to recreate this subject. For some reason, this design does not seem to be very popular (but I have always liked it).

My site is fairly active on the topic of Galactica, then there is the Yahoo Galactica Builder's Group. Most folks who are building these replicas keep their discussions to private e-mail and there is not a lot of posting on the boards anymore. The primary reason for this is competition for kits and parts -- as more people get into building studio-scale replicas the parts/kits get harder and harder to find and more and more expensive. Because of the high cost and limited availability of reference material, very few projects ever get completed.

Links:

http://www.StarshipBuilder.com (no membership required to view posts)

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/galacticabuilders/  (requires free membership to view photos)
