Picked this up in the JY recently...it is an old school niesen solid body ywing.

My goal for the fall is to have it built and primered before winter. Painting will probably have to wait till next season.

At the moment I do not have a specific Y in mind, other than it will be gold leader.

The basic build does not seem overly complex...but it has a little bit more to it than the viper I did earlier in the summer.

I am once again unsure about lighting. I really don't know an ohm from an amper. Also, it is solid cored (body and wing canisters...so it doesnt beg to be lighted. Love to know if anyone has lit these old beasts.

Now, I am doing a bit of work to hollow out the body and the canisters...so it may be feasible...but unsure. Before I picked this up I was collecting bits and pieces and i hadnt seen any Y for a while (have someones castoffs including head and greebly plant ons...and another kind fellows leftover redjammer-***-goldleaders canisters and various parts). So plan another Y as well...but start with this.

So far I have sawed off the backplate and hollowed out the fuselage and one of the canisters. It makes a real difference in weight. Have drilled a hole partway through the fuselage for the rod (1/2 copper pipe). I need a longer drill bit so i can get the rod all the way to supporting the head.
