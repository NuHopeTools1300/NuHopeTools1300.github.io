A little 232-Pod useless trivia for y'all

2 years ago while working on a movie called 'Iron Sky' I modified some some existing CG models of German vehicles into Moon versions.  They were going to be used as dressing for the main German 'Black Sun' base.  Quickly it was discovered that they appeared as little more than a single pixel on screen!  I did end up using them in other shots but they were still small.  Later there was a need for the US ship to fire it's nuke missiles at the base.  Realizing that shot would show a dozen or more of these dropping out of the ship and launching towards the German base I hatched a cunning plan...
