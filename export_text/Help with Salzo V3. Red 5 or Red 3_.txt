Joe, there isn't all that much publicly available good quality reference material for ANY of the original Star Wars X-wings bar Red 2. The only other really well-documented one is the big Red 3 from Jedi.  Most of what is available can be found online, and plenty right here (check the various X-wing threads).

Another thing you can do is just follow the layouts of other people's builds. Guy Cowen's Red 3, just finished and right here on the RPF, is a stellar piece of work. There have also been some great Red 5 builds which are close to or even of reference quality.
