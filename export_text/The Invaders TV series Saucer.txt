Too bad the Aurora/Revell Kit was inaccurate. Number and spacing of energy ports around the top is wrong and there should be no grid pattern on the lower engine circle window (and it should be red/orange in color). The power domes under the craft should be a green/blue color. The color of the model should be a light gray and not silver metallic. The interior is based off the first season without the extra control room redressing done in the second season.
