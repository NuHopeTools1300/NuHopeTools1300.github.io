Oh, has anyone else had a problem with the piece connecting between the mandible and the saucer that I've circled in red being extremely crooked? Not only are they both not straight front to back, but they also lean outward top to bottom, just crooked all around. My last Falcon had this exact same problem(along with defective lighting) and being such a large piece it was very obvious, I thought surely I wouldn't receive another one with the same problem but it appears that I have. If I've received two Falcons this way then it must be pretty common, and I feel that such a large and prominent piece being put on so sloppily and crooked on such an expensive piece is unacceptable. Well, Dean's fantastic paint work makes up for the shortcomings, but that still makes me mad as hell, especially after all the trouble I had with the last one.
