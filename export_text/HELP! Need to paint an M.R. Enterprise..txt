The recommended color on the Polar Lights 1/350 TOS Enterprise box is Tamiya XF-12 JN Grey.  However JN Grey will be too green looking compared to the MR Enterprise's color.

You can try mixing Tamiya XF-12 JN Grey with some XF-19 Sky Grey.  The Sky Grey will tone the green tint way down and it'll look very much like the MR hull color.  

I did a mix of of 3 parts JN Grey, 1 parts Sky Grey and 4 parts Flat White for an AMT Enterprise but you can use less white or none at all.

Mike.
