I did this quick animation for Star Trek TNG's 30th anniversary. While this is CG, I referenced the actual physical models of the D closely and I did my very best to remain faithful to the way they were painted, and also how they were lit on the motion control stage. I wanted to capture that "look" you get from physical miniatures. Anyway, here's hoping it brings back some nostalgia.

[video=vimeo;211048371]https://vimeo.com/211048371[/video]

Vimeo compresses the detail in the video ever so slightly, so I have also supplied full res stills. Click each one to view full size.
