It's your choice of course but if that's the case I think a lot of modellers would prefer it to be black rather than blue if you're going to colour the resin. Of course it doesn't matter so much as it's a static display model but in terms of pieces that get handled such as replica guns etc. I definitely prefer black resin, in fact I've refrained from buying stuff that's done in white resin before.

There are those that claim pigmenting resin is messy. My answer to that would be that it's as messy as you make it. I cast in black resin all the time and if I'm careful with it it's a very neat process. I should add that I always wear gloves and work in an area that isn't affected if I do spill some of course.
