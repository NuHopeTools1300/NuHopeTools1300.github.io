It has working lights, drive motors and a scratchbuilt partial interior. I plan on stripping it and repainting with new correct decals that I have. It was missing the tripod for the radar dish. I found the original piece in the Airfix Lunar Modual and will install it in the restoration.

Author    : Chris Martin

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Trust me when I say the original studio model used the V braces from the LM legs. For a start off, there should be four struts, not three. There's also a Gemini part right in the middle of the roof on your laser tank cockpit that isn't there on the original and a couple of parts snuggled into the battleship part on the roof of the corridor under the gun that shouldn't be there either. There's a few erroneous marking too. Otherwise it's very good.
