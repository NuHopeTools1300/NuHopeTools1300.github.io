Something that I recently picked up that I thought worth sharing…

These are production-made decals for the Enterprise Refit miniature from Star Trek: The Motion Picture.

What’s particularly cool is this set of decals includes the early version of the Starfleet Insignia and stripes for the engineering hull.

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/img_4344-jpeg.1816524/]

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/img_4345-jpeg.1816525/]

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/img_4346-jpeg.1816526/]

The decal sheet is 27” x 20” and appears to be the same version that was sold in this Propstore auction in 2020:

			https://usm.propstoreauction.com/lot-details/index/catalog/267/lot/62746
		

I’m planning on eventually getting this  framed for my office.
