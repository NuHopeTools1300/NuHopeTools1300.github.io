Well, after a year hiatus on this project, I finally finished it.

This model is a replica of the filming miniature used in the Original Battlestar Galactica series, we see it in the first 'movie'. It is primarilay made out of 1/8" acrylic and is approximately 44" long by 7.5" wide. The weathering is mostly oils, with some pastel and airbrush finishing. Most of the kit parts match the original, but some scratch parts were made to fill in for those I didn't have.

It's quite amazing how it changes color under different lighting. Sorry about the poor pics, just didn't have any good light down there.

Hope you enjoy!

-Sean
