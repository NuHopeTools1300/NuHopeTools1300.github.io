The base is the hobby style balsa plywood.  Then it has 1/8 thick sheet styrene on top of it.  The raised "Z" section is made from balsa wood with a styrene skin.  Once all the styrene was put on I just started grabbing misc parts from different model kits.

The majority came from a Tamiya 1/35 Hanomag kit.  Took roughly two hours to do all said and done and I was very pleased with how it turned out.
