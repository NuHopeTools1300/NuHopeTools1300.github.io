Hey Ringa, If you mean the coming out of the time tunnel bit then this is what I did. I used a ring effect from Particle Illusion, and a lens flare (yeah I know 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]
 then I created a moving mask around the ship so it looked like it was coming through a layer (inspired by Kelsey Grammers ship coming through the time rift in TNG's Cause and Effect) I then illuminated the edges of the moving mask and added a zoom blur to it. The way I made the ship move towards and away from the camera was hilarious. I just tilted the tripod towards and away from the ship 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]

If you are referring to the "time tunnel" effect, I just lifted it from Doctor Who.

Hope this helps
