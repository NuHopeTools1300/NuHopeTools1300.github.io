Post #8  (forum post #1778207)
Author    : Boba Flint
Date      : Jul 21, 2011 at 3:15 PM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The base color was custom mix. I used a combination of Testors French Dark Blue Grey with some RAF Azure blu. At some point I used light grey in the mix as well.  I used strait light grey for the all the lighter spots and stippled it on with Micro Mark's #8 dry brush. Some of the weathering was done with a dark grey pastel and Tamiya's weathering set A and B.
