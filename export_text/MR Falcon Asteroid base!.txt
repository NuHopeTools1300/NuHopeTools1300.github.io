I need to put some fresh black card down whein i have painted the asteroid as the one thats on it is a bit tatty. Of coarse if it all looks a bit naff when i have done it i simply take the thing off. I noticed something on my MR base...........in order for it to be centered the best on the base,  I had to turn the falcon such that the left outside line of left mandible is parallel with that side of the base...in other words, it needs to fly towards that corner of the base somewhat....   Once you do this it will look like it is supposed to be that way.  When I leave the falcon pointed straight and parallel with both sides of the base......it looks terribly uneven.
