Here are the photos I took at WSMI in Wichita, KS.  They should be open for anyone to view and download.  Note:  there are 1400+ pics, 5+ gigs.  It took over 6 hours to upload them.  I plan on leaving them until I need additional space, ie probably no time soon.

https://skydrive.live.com/redir?resid=B36510BA2591A1A4!169

Some of them are not the best in quality. The combination of the glass cases and less than ideal lighting make it tough.  I was hoping to get  quality through quantity.  I decided to leave some of the poorer pics since it seems some people can look through muddy water and ID a part.

The only thing I want in return is that if you make a new discovery based on them you share it...at least with me.
