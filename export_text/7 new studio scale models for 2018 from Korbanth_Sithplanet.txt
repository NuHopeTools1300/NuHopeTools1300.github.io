Quick update on new Studio Scale models coming in 2018

With the help of Modelermagic owner Kurt Kuhn, I purchased the master/patterns for

Chris Kelly's 1/24 scale Vader Tie X1

Chris Kelly's 41 inch blockade Runner

Alfred Wongs 40 inch U-Wing (never been molded yet)

Alfred Wongs 1/24 scale B-wing  

Alfred Wongs 1/1000 Republic Star Destroyer - 36 inches long

Also being MASTERED right now is a 42 inch Wide Mega Star Destroyer - Supremacy, snokes capital ship and the First Order Dreadnaught 36 inch long  

(yeah, the Pizza Slice ship)

Patterns are verified from both Chris Kelly and Alfred Wong as being their original master patterns that were sold to a person who made them for a time, and now belong to me. The reason the person who sold them to me, was because this person has a long history of bad karma, selling less than top quality castings, ripping people off, and his reputation was so bad, he was having difficulty in selling them, so he went through a third party mediator over at ModelerMagic to find a buyer -  and Kurt Arranged to get the patterns so our team could take them over.

First up will be a 15 model run of Vaders X1 Tie and 15 model run of the B-wing model. Both models will be casted using a pressure pot for bubble-less model kits.

Second up will be the 42 inch wide Supremacy (Snoke's Mega star destroyer, and the First Order Dreadnaught models)

Third up - The 40 inch Uwing, and 41 inch Blockade Runner ships, and the 36 inch long 1/1000 Venator Republic Star Destroyer

Many have seen my "Korbanth" 38 inch Super Star Destroyer model, and I've added a few other models such as 1/24 scale T-65 Xwing and 1/24 scale T-70 Resistance Xwing which can be purchased through my site of www.korbanth.com

Thanks.
