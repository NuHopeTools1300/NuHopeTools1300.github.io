Studio scale replica of the shuttlecraft used in season 2 of Buck Rogers.  This is the ship used often by Buck, Wilma, etc. to go from the Searcher to planet surfaces.  It was featured heavily in episodes like 'The Satyr' and 'The Crystals'.

As with the original miniature, this was another re-use of my mold of the Buck Ranger 3 (see previous post).  For this one, the wings were shortened quite a bit,  'vertical stabilizers' were added to the wings, and the vertical tail was removed.  The rainbow stripe is a tie-in to the other 25th century Earth ship on the show, the Starfighter.

Also like the original studio model, there is no interior and the solid windows are just painted black.
