I have seen a couple of these obscure rebel fleet ships done. Most notably the (double pontoon) ship. I thought I would make the single pontoon ship. Not much to go on so here is what I have started out with so far. I think that the 1/24 Harrier engine is one of the coolest kit bashing  pieces so I wanted to make it a point to use that. From the pictures It doesn't look like there is a window in front but I decided to add that and have a detailed cockpit. for more interest. The pilot is from the 1/48 Bandai Jagd panther. I am trying to keep this in true ILM form and use kit used in other ESB models. Enjoy   Scott
