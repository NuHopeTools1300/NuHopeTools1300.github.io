Went back and corrected a lot of shapes- just wasn't happy with the way some things were coming out.  I took some 'weight' out of the middle- litterally cut the thing in half down the middle to do it.  I also reworked the wings- the geometry on this thing is a real headache!

Try to look past the putty, marker, and cut lines (lot of sanding to do soon!):

[IMAGE: 3892896274_b7c2544c3c.jpg]

[IMAGE: 3892897116_a9bb65d5bc.jpg]

[IMAGE: 3892109407_bf22c9c2d3.jpg]

[IMAGE: 3892899048_1f0dbcc205.jpg]

Again, still don't know how identical I'm coming to the original studio model, but at least now all the lines end up the right places.

I'm fast approaching the step of figuring out detailing under the tail and bottm-  there's pretty much NO reference out there, so I may have to 'wing it'.  I have and ID on exactly two parts from the Airfix Saturn V- other than that it will be pure speculation.  But at least this beastie will be big and look good next to the SS Starfighter.
