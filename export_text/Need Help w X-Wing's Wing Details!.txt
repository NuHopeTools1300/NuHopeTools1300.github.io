────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Although it was probably obliterated by an air bubble the part in the photo is missing a tiny bit of the Saturn V trimming that is present on some production castings. It's barely a cube that comes off of the side, and will overhang the circular opening of the tank jack. Dont cut it off if you're making a pattern.
