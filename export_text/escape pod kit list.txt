Most people aren't going to post an actual kit list, as a lot of us don't maintain them over time. I started one for Slave with great intentions, but then dropped the ball on keeping it updated. It's as much of a chore to build the lists as it is the model, LOL. Below is the state of that list when I last updated it. Many of these kits are used extensively on a number of ESB subjects. 

If you have a particular question about a part ID from any subject, just post a pic of the part you're looking for. Things have changed a little around here, and there are a few people who will beat a path to your door with the ID if they know it. Some of them may even start checking kits for you. Because it's fun, which is what a "hobby" is supposed to be! 

But, do keep in mind that people working on a current project may not be as apt to share, just because they don't need the bidding competition on eBay. That's not always true, as witnessed by Lee's AT-ST thread. But, you can't fault someone for not wanting to cut their own throat by making information public about a rare kit they still need.
