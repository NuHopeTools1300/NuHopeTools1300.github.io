I used what I personally think was probably used: a Letraset dry transfer sheet. The "Triangles" Y-Wing used registration marks (I have sourced the correct ones from my stash of 80's era Chartpak and Letraset sheets). The registration mark sheet and others have LOTS of elements that are bang-on correct for the dashes. Things like hyphens, lowercase or uppercase "I"s, etc. 

[IMAGE: letraset.jpg]

Funny enough, I reproduced them as decals and used those on my Blue 1.
