Yep - that is confirmed! I recently chatted with Joe Johnston (!!) about the Pirate Ship and he said:

"Hi Jason, the pirate ship wasn't very far along when we decided to change it into the blockade runner because of the SPACE 1999 ship, so I'm not really sure what changes, if any, were made other than the cockpit being changed to the "hammerhead"... the was because the set builders in the UK had already built or at least started construction on the full size set of Han Solo's cockpit, which was later grafted onto the new Millennium Falcon. Now that I think of it, escape pods were also added for the sequence where Threepio and R2 jettison themselves down to Tattooine. Btw, I'm not even sure that's me in the picture. I had a Pignose shirt but so did almost everyone else on the crew. It looks like my hair but it doesn't look like my body. 60% chance it's me, could be Dick Alexander who could have been wiring up engines. Thanks for the picture!"

Then later after I sent him a clear shot of his face with that crazy bowl cut he said "I look like Kim Cattrall." LOL!!!

He wants to see the Landspeeder when it's finished - YIKES!!!
