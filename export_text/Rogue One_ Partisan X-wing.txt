Finished my first studio scale X-wing today. Decided to experiment with the little seen/deleted scenes Saw Gerrera's Partisan unit from Rogue One. Built using an old Capt. Cardboard kit that I've had for way too long in a box. Very little source material, but that also gave me some freedom to play! Also the first time I used the Tamiya Panel Line Accents for most of the weathering paint, really liked it. 

Thanks to those that helped, Frank Cerney (Pilot head), Niels M. Frederiksen, Jason Eaton (decals).

More pics here: https://www.flickr.com/photos/lee_stringer/albums/72157685095589336
