These were taken in January 2008 at the Star Wars Exhibition in London.   The lighting is problematic, and I was winging it trying different  focal  lengths and shutter speeds to compensate.

Resolution is 3504 x 2336. Click on the thumbnail, then click again for the full resolution.

For easier searching I'll keep each model in a separate thread:
