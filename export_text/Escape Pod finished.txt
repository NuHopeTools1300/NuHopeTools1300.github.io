Thanks everyone! I really enjoyed this kit. I would call it an above average garage kit. I did add some modifications. Rolando did a great job scratchbuilding a lot of the details when he first made the Pod. But being the anal person I am I had to relplace some of the  scratchbuild details with the real kit parts.

I started with the usual black primer then white. I used a mixture of Floquil colors for the base. Mostly reefer white and CN Grey with a little foundation and tiny amount of SP lettering grey.  I used CSX Yellow (GLS) for the yellow stripes. I used washes of Liquitex Raw siena and oil raw umber. Weathered with Floquil weathered black, grimmy black, dust and primer. And used an HB artist pencil for a few spots.
