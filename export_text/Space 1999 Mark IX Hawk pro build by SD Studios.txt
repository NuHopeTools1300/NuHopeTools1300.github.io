Another masterpiece build by Steve Dymszo of SD Studios: the 16" Mark IX Hawk from Space 1999!

This was a commission built from an Alfred Wong kit. Those of you "in the know" will realize that this meant LOTS of fixing, accurizing, and heroic detailing work. I'd say Steve succeeded in turning an average (at best) kit into a fantastic display-worthy piece. 

(the thruster units are crooked in a few shots because I bumped them during shooting... rest assured the model is actually OK!)
