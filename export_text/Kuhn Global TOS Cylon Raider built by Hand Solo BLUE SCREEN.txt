This beastie was done last year by Bob (Hand Solo) with the original studio model as it’s example. Bob even placed the “ghost” Cylon insignia just to the right of the cockpit (pilot POV) just as the original had! He took artistic license with some panels to give the piece a little “realism”… something I often ask for. He did a GREAT job as usual!

As usual, the only enhancements were the stands being eliminated, the contrast slightly adjusted, and my water mark added.
