Hammer,

The kits you need and the part ID's are all listed in previous threads on this board. Put "Tie Bomber" into the search panel and read all the threads that come up. They will answer a lot of your questions, and some you haven't asked yet. 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]

The parts circled in red and green are from the Tamiya Yamato (original, not re-release), Tam flakvierling, Tam Flak Sd.Ah.51 & Tam british 25pdr. The blue circled part is from the same kit that the large grey part on top of the left tube is from (first photo).

Go to this site; http://www.studioscale.com/ and look through the kit-scans. You will see plenty of parts from the bomber there (if not all) and plenty of parts from SS models you didn't even know you wanted to build. :lol Once you get your eye in, you will be hooked. You get a much better sense of satisfaction if you can ID the parts yourself.

Cheers and good luck with the build, great start by the way,
