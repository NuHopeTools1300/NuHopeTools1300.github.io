Was just checking out Sci-Fi & Fantasy Modeller's website and saw that they are previewing an upcoming DVD named "Sense of Scale". It showcases interviews with 32 movie model makers on films like, Star Wars, Blade Runner, Moon, Star Trek, Close Encounters, Outland, Escape from New York, Abyss, Goldeneye and many more.

I watched their short trailer and was pleasantly surprised when I saw a short take on Lorne Petersons interview and he has the Escape Pod I presented to him at one of his book signings in the background.
