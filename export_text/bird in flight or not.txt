eh up all,hope you all had a good christmas and new year!

i have another update here i've done a bit more of the cockpit

to start off i painted up the cockpit tub in matt black and started to knock up the front console base,which i made from some 1mm styrene and painted up in tamiya dark iron.

[IMAGE: 286727-60e819f5fea32fa47beeec9917c18f02.jpg]

then i started on the top part of the console,same again usin 1mm styrene for the bottom and sides and some half mm for the top just so it was easier to put the fibre optics through.

[IMAGE: 286728-c47cb025a6b510df7c81b87245602b02.jpg]

then here you can see the two parts together and the fibre optics in.

[IMAGE: 286729-babff8c483459491d6660ae5c77d6db1.jpg]

and here it's in position,which was tricky to get the fibre optics in place.

[IMAGE: 286730-b83b724694bbbe320dd51ff457d46478.jpg]

so you may just be able to make out the 2 control levers which i made with some soldering wire and heating up one end to make it bubble up,the other flight controls in the next image i used some old guitar string from one of the kids old broken guitars(i think they'd been watchin the who)i slightly pulled the inner wires out of the string but it was very very fiddly and the steering controls where just some brass tube and styrene.

[IMAGE: 286731-94b1308132013a42ee334aff4772ac76.jpg]

and here i'd managed to get the four tiny flight controls on the console.

[IMAGE: 286732-8f417812f87686d52830db80f46bf389.jpg]

i painted up the steering controls but everytime i tried to take a photo of them fitted it looks to shakey or dead dark,so i gave up on that as an image,i will try again on my next post.

but before i finish this post i've made and finished the front seats which i made out of 1mm styrene and some styrene tube,here you can see the start of the build.

[IMAGE: 286726-d91d6a66e99eef814a97e06cfbf4bc05.jpg]

i finished one and gave it a coat of sand tan

[IMAGE: 286733-39782dcadfaf20a15d81e53eb8e22a60.jpg]

,but i hated the colour so i had a dig around and found some citadel kommando khaki,and i really liked the look of it,it may not be the correct colour but it looks like it fits!

[IMAGE: 286734-e7fb6a2defb433397b165bcef18ce340.jpg]

well thats it for this update i'm not gonna fit the front seats til it finish buildin the back ones just to make it easy for me to fit..

hope you like the progress so far...cheers for lookin in....thanks peza
