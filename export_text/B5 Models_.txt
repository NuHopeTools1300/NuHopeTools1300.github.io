The real dark secret took place when Netter Digital was formed by producers Doug Netter and John Copeland. They decided to end the relationship with Foundation Imaging. They took all the CG models and recruited some of the animators to go on there own. Netter Digital went under pretty fast but FI continued through the end of Star Trek DS9 and Voyager. We even did the directors cut of the Motion Picture. But the partners who owned FI disolved the company. But those CG models were around and are still around and can easily be had. I don't see why JMS had to keep the ego thing going. It's a shame, they could of had there models again to use.

Mic, these are in Lightwave format. They are not ready for any kind of rapid prototyping. They need to be reworked. Are you asking for renders? Or the data files? and Kip, Wolfster, has a nice kit of the Shadow ships fyi.
