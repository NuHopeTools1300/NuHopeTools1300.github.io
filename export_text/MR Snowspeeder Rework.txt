Thanks, the lighting wasn't so bad to do. There is quite a bit of room on both sides of the cockpit for led's, wires, and optics. The only tough spot is the upper panels to the left and right of the gunner. The panel is only about 1/16" thick and right up against the canopy. It also happens to have most of the optics running up there. Tight fit!

[IMAGE: lighting1.jpg]

The air brake and door had to be scratched as the part is almost a solid block of resin. That was fun LOL.
