Hello All,

Just finished up the Studio Scale Canaris Shuttle from TOS BSG.  This ship was seen in "The Man with Nine Lives" episode, with Fred Astaire. Though I came close to reproducing the original, there are a number of parts I either ID'd (with the help of some great friends!) or didn't have to put on. I wanted to use the excess parts in my stash and not go out and buy anything new.  So, it's not "exact". The main body halves though are the original parts from two Tamiya Ford F-1 Model Kits, then there is the 1/32 F-4 and the Fujimi F-15, along with the Sat V. All usual suspects for BSG kits. I also accidently cut the engine area a bit short. I still have a touch of weathering to do in the chins, but all in all I think it came out pretty good. I love the colors, though they wash out on the show.

It was an incredibly fun little project and rag tag ship to build, it now joins the rest of the fleet!

Thanks,

Sean
