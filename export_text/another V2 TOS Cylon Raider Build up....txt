Yeah, Home Deopt, Lowes, Sears, maybe even Walmart.....if your only buying 1 it should only be a few bucks, i suggest getting a 1/2 inch one because it gives the wires a little room to move in the cavity.  Again, take you time drilling the holes, use some blue tape to protect the body, a little extra care during that part will save you needing to make a repair later...

One other ? thing that someone asked about on this one the Raider can be removed from the base by disconnecting wire clips that are under the battery tray, in short i unscrew the battery tray, disconnect the wire clips, and screw the battery tray back on.  This allows the switch and battery to be hidden in the base, but if you wanted to there is enough room for the battery in the battery compartment, and you can put the switch in a number of spots under the body of the raider.  Just pick the configuration you are happiest with.
