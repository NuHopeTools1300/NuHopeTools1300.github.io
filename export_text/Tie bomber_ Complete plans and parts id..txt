────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The Vader Tie is the same scale as the standard Tie, X-Wing, Y-Wing, and the 5' Falcon. All about 1/24. 

The Tie bomber is a much smaller model in real life. It uses some parts from the MPC Darth Vader Tie kit. But I think that in the context of the movie, one half of the Tie bomber would be about the same diameter as a standard Tie. 

I know that doesn't quite work out proportionately, but It doesn't really make sense for it to be much larger or smaller than a standard Tie. That's my $.02
