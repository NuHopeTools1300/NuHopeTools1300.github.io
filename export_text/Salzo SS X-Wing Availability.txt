So ignore a few of the crooked panel lines,  still fixing a few of those.   It may not be super obvious the differences between this and the V3 fuselage,  but the whole side of the body is slightly "thicker"  and rounder,  just like the pyro castings.  It's only by like 1 or 2 mm overall but it makes it look much "beefier"  the way that I think it should look.  At the same time the part that intersects with the front of the canopy frame is slimmer,  if i remember right it's because of the correct curvature of the body.  The canopy is a bit different but a subtle change in just a few parts makes a big difference in the appearance.   Not to knock what Moe did,  his work was so extremely close that only a nut like me would consider messing with it.

Again,  subtle changes that may be more apparent in person than can show up in pics....

I am going to tweak the nose a bit too....

mike
