The 232 is pretty much done and is in need of a paint job. I put it together a little here and there as I had time. With a three month old and a 13 year old in the house, time to build gets very limited.

It is made mostly of resin parts (75% to 80%) that I molded and cast due to having more RTV around that actual donor kits. The engine will be lit and you can see the wires in the pictures. I figure it's better to have and hardly light up than wish that I had lit it in the end.

I'm going to paint the two Pods together when I finish the other one as I think that will be easier. If you see something that is off or wrong with the build, please let me know!

[IMAGE: DSC_0856a_zps284aa270.jpg]

[IMAGE: DSC_0861a_zpse5151b6d.jpg]

[IMAGE: DSC_0863a_zps0f23254c.jpg]

[IMAGE: DSC_0856_a_zps80cf2bdd.jpg]

The 223 Pod is from a kit that was offered a while ago. I went ahead and shaved some parts off so that I can upgrade the build with parts that weren’t included/ID’ed at the time I received the kit.

[IMAGE: DSC_0835a_zpsdf914494.jpg]

The bottom engine part is going to be taken off and replaced with a part that will fit the engine bell much better. I glued it on while thinking that I was going to use the engine bell that came with the kit, but I have since scrapped that idea. Now I will be using the rims from a JPS MK III. I also need to clean the edges of the bottom that meet the 223 body parts a bit more.

[IMAGE: DSC_0838a_zps3a002afa.jpg]

Some of the parts that came with the kit were broken during shipping so I decided to mold and cast donors from the actual donor kits that I already had along with parts that did not come with the kit. Who doesn’t like working with their own castings anyway?

I still need to clean these up:

[IMAGE: DSC_0827a_zps465d4c17.jpg]

[IMAGE: DSC_0832a_zps86b48221.jpg]

[IMAGE: DSC_0813a_zpsb44c3566.jpg]

So I still have more parts to acquire and mold before I am done putting this together. I plan on molding the Ferrari hood next (which I have), but I am in need of a Jagdpanther tank tread and the 1/25 Centurion part that people have been using on the back end to house the engine bell. I would either like to borrow these parts to mold (I would send you the part and a few castings back) or simply trade outright for the parts.

As always, let me know of errors you see in these builds!
