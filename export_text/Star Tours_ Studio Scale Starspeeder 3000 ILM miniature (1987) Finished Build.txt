This has been a project I've wanted to tackle for a while, the miniature Starspeeder 3000 built by ILM for the original Star Tours.  There were two sizes made, a 1/24 scale 'hero' and a few smaller 1/144 scale.  The smaller scale ones were made for the background shots in the final scene entering the spaceport hangar.  A few have been sold through PropStore of London but they've go for crazy money.  Still hoping to get an original one day but this will hold me over in the mean time.

The starspeeder has to be at least three times bigger than this! Haha I know, it's amazing the detail that resin printing can get and with little to no sanding where there arent supports.  

There were some random details that I missed and didnt realize until i started painting so working on the V2 now.  I somehow missed the second panel line near the front window and some lines near the back. Im also going to try integrating the back 1mm tubing as part of the print.  Made the top front windvane a bit thinner too.  Nothing a normal person wouldnt notice, but this is the studio scale section so every detail counts lol
