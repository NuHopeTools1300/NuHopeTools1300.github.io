Another excellent update! Again some of the best Y-wing work I've seen, so please forgive the extreme nitpickiness that is about to follow...

On these raised pieces on the edges at the back of the cockpit section, it looks like you currently have them symmetrical, but in the ANH ones it looks like the right side has more of that molded kit part cut off at the bottom... These photos are of Gold Leader, Gold 2, and Red Jammer. Not sure if that means the overall cockpit cross section is slightly asymmetrical, or if the kitbash detailing is just shifted slightly down on the right side, and they had to trim it a little more to fit. (Maybe it was originally a mistake...) 

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/1774654697421-png.2022267/]

In addition to being asymmetrical, I think you can see in the gold 2 and red jammer photos that that surface of that piece is not parallel to the edge of the cockpit below it, it is angled out a little bit.

Also going back to the underside greebling for a moment, here I have yours compared to a photo of the underside molded part from an auction from an ILMer's collection, and Gold Leader, with a few more minor tweaks. Mostly these are verrry minor, but there are a couple of times where the additional pipes they added to the final ones hid some of the kitbash parts from view, where seeing the clean molded underside reveals a bit more info.

For the red labelled parts, just small changes in shape. For the green, I think that center kit part should have a bit of a cutout out of it to make room for that box and pipe. The yellow ones, on the left side I think you had the right part but it looks like they trimmed it down to fit in that spot, and on the right side you have it right but it should be rotated a little.

For the part labelled in teal, whatever that kit part is is a little more complex and asymmetrical than what you currently have. The purple part, I just don't think there is a gap between those pieces, and lastly on that orange rectangle, I think you have the part right but have it centered, and it should just be slid over a little. 

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/1774655281390-png.2022269/]

Again excuse the over-attention to detail, it's pretty astounding how spot-on everything is!

Soo great! It's beautiful!

I do have a last final round of possible tweaks to suggest, I apologize for the last time for it and for many large image attachments. 
[IMAGE: yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7]

On your gun turret, it looks like you have both guns being symmetrical, but at the rear of the guns, I believe the left gun is the same as the right gun rotated 180°. So only the right gun should have that triangular part sticking up at the rear and a rounded part on the underside, while the left one has the rounded part sticking up and the triangular part facing down instead.

Near the middle of the guns, the Y-wings have some little nub sticking out that is absent in your model. Also, possibly the 2 rails that go halfway down the barrel have a little extra part projecting upwards, vs in your model they are flat (only annotated in the last of these images).

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/1774987148293-png.2023461/]

I also think that some of the beveled edges of the windows should be wider- just comparing the spacing of the flat parts of the middle window pillar with the beveled parts, they seem closer to the same width in the originals, while the flat parts are wider in yours. And the rear angle of the rear window should possibly be a bit closer towards being parallel with the rear of the cockpit? Halfway between the current angle and parallel, maybe? I am less sure of this.

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/1774988733458-png.2023465/]

I think that the bottom front edge of the 'chin' should be more horizontal/flat. It looks like yours continues gently curving down in the middle, and I think in the originals the underside of the cockpit transitions to being pretty flat at the front. Here are some front view pics of Gold 3 (in the 70s), Gold 2, and Gold 3 again (in the 2000s). (I didn't include Gold Leader in this compilation because in the modern auction photos it kind of looks like the top and bottom of that area are bowing inwards, which I don't think was intended...).

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/1774986141804-png.2023446/]

In the ends of the engines, there is a missing greeble (at least, there is a greeble there on Gold 2 and Leader). 

[IMAGE: https://www.therpf.com/forums/attachments/1774989529183-png.2023469/]

I thiink that's everything I can spot in these new renders. Again, phenomenal work!
