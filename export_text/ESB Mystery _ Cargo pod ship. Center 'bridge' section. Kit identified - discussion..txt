For a long while now I've been obsessing over the ESB cargo pod ship, seen briefly at the end of Empire. I'm unable to work, following a stroke last year, so I've had time to concentrate.

I'd like to thank members: sjanish, kh39, seven, propcollector, stonky, astrocat and lastly but by no means least Carter C for assisting with my requests for any pics they might have in helping identify kit parts. Thanks guys - you rock! 

There have been several amazing interpretations built by members here - Carter's , Lee's and Jason's to name a few.

Source material is very difficult to find. There are screen caps from the movie and the Japanese documentary, plus stills of the cardboard / paper photographs used in the production, which show the ship at an angle. 

We know there are many Airfix 1 :144 Saturn V parts used in the center and rear of the model, but the section sitting between the two 'pod' cargo containers has been a matter for artistic interpretation. 

I'm going to call this the 'Bridge' section for the purpose of identification. 

I believe it's comprised of that old ILM favourite, the Tamiya 1:35 KFZ 222. Namely the top hull half, flipped and rotated. The recess at the now forward facing rear of the part provides a convenient 'cockpit window' section.  

I think there's possibly another armored car section placed behind and on top of this part, creating the 'steps' we can see.

Thoughts guys?
