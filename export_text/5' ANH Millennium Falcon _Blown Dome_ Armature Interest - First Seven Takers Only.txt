I am here only soliciting general interest for the following armature materials for the 5-foot ANH Millennium Falcon, and only doing so in order to share and thus lower the cost: 

1.  Two 1/4" plexiglass vac-formed domes at 48" flat, blown to max height of 3.75, with resulting OD of roughly 47.5, with outer edge sanded flush to ground.

2.  One 1/4" plexiglass pizza-wedge of exact same contour and measurements, but totaling 1/3 of the dome area, to make the elevated engine panel section of the Falcon.

IF you are interested, please either post publicly or PM me as soon as possible.  Price likely in the $800 + shipping range.  (Do it yourself as a one-off and these are typically $3500 minimum)

Your interest in this (or lack thereof), may also determine whether I make an equally limited run (7 sets only) of MF greeblie details for the very pricey pieces only (not  a complete set) using two-part molds.  Because I have the pieces to make the Y-Wing, it turns out I already have most of the pieces to make a Falcon, and thus the potentiality of this offer. 

If sufficient interest is generated, I will post a thread in the Parts Run Forum, and THAT will determine the first-come/first-serve order of Dome sets produced.

Meanwhile, don't get TOO excited: Conservative estimates put the 5-foot Falcon at a 25k to 40k project, so don't fool yourself that if you've got the $800 you'll surely have no problem with the other $24,200...
