All,

Bought this kit when they came out early late 2011 and went right to work on it. Then I got frustrated with bending and melting far too many pipes that just ended up in the scrap bin. Got bitten with the resin and styrene bug this weekend and pulled the plastic off her to finish it up. The build was fairly straight forward with the incredible instructions supplied by Jason. If they weren't there, I don't know how I would have gotten this far. She is essentially done for now until I get my paint skills on par with what this kit deserves. I have the rattle can primer bit down to an art. Just need to work on stuff with color in it! No matter how hard I try though, I can't get the pipes to work for me where they wrap around the rear on each side. Anyone willing to help with this will be paid handsomely!
