Hi all,Ive started this a while back,its constructed in acrylic armature,Im going for idealize way on this so its not 100% accurate but hopefully didnt affect the overall look.

The idea was came from the drawing of the armature by QC when he shared it to the community before I got the chance on getting the metal armature offered by Mars once.'Thanks QC and those who contributed on the armature drawing and research,really appreciate the effort'.

Without wasting the laser cut armature,Ive sent all my plastic donor part to a friend for copy to use on the acrylic armature and saving the original plastic donor for my metal armature,Ive used only a few resin donor part so far as the casting Ive received were not quiet satisfied.
