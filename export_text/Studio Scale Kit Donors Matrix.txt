2025-10-04 Update!

Hey Builders!

I'm sharing the Excel workbook I use to keep track of donors for Star Wars Studio Scale builds. You can download it here:

Matrix Ver. 1.02 (2025-10-04)​

This was a passion project for me during the early days of the pandemic and kept me occupied for months. The bulk of the identifications are the result of everyone's contribution on this and other forums. Thanks to you all, with special thanks to Studio Kitbash, sjanish and rooker2k for their direct input. Feel free to download and share, but please include a citation and link to this thread if you post it elsewhere. A few things to note:

To sort/slice by a particular model, click the thumbnail at the top of the matrix. Hit the "reset" button anytime to return to the entire matrix.

I added columns to the right side for inventory tracking. These cells are all blank as provided. Click the "inventory" button to focus on this and populate.

There are some columns at the end of the columns with conditional formatting to count how many times a kit is used and for a relative cost comparison.

The workbook has VBA scripts that control the sorting and model selection. I built them myself, so they are safe.

I included props that use the Revell Visible V8 Engine, but this could use some work.

I had hoped to include the 5' MF based on the mapping by jcoffman99, but that is a project for another day.

I will keep this file updated as I work on it and should be uploading new versions every couple of months.

If you'd like to contribute or see mistakes/errors, post them here or send me an IM and I will include them in the next update.

Enjoy!

Marc
