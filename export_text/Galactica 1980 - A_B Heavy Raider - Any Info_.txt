Hello, all.  I know G1980 is generally a sore spot for most fans of TOS, but it did have the one interesting addition of the new Cylon A/B Heavy Raider.  Schematic here:  http://shipschematics.net/bsg/images/cylon/abfighter_cylon.jpg  Does anyone know what ever happened to the original miniature or have any pics of it besides what has been seen on screen?  It's been a while but, IIRC, It was initially seen briefly (and by briefly I mean no more than 2 seconds of screen time) in "The Super Scouts Pt. 1" as it was strafing the cargo freighter Delphi (which was really the Gemini Freighter from TOS), necessitating the kids on board to be moved safely to Earth where they engaged in their little low-grav antics. It saw a lot more screen time in "The Night The Cylons Landed Pt. 1". It was damaged in a skirmish with Vipers and re-entered in a slow orbit down to Earth.  Reference to it here on the Battlestar Wiki
