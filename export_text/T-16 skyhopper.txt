I did build it up.  I didn't do a highly detailed paint job on it, and I didn't know anything about do-it-yourself decals back then, so I hand painted the orangey markings, including those little "griffons" on the tail.

Come to think of it, I'm pretty sure that was the very first garage kit I ever worked on.
