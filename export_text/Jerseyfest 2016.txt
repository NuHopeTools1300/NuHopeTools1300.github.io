We were there on Sunday. Delivered two SS x-wing commission builds to Dao2. 

It was nice to go to a show again as we have not been to anything since moving up from Texas in 2003. We walked the room twice and since we just returned from our vacation, we had little/no money to spend. I did pick up two Dr. Flintbone kits of martian spy girl with female head and one with martian head. Wanted to buy so much more. It was great to see a lot of kits that we used to have. It was like a flashback.

Depending on her health, I would like to build a few kits for the contest next year. 

The room with all the built kits was great. Was nice to see all the different approaches that were used to get the final results.
