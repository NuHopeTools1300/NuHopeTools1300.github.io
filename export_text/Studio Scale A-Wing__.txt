He didn't fly the Y-wing either, but that's very "Star Wars". The A-wing IS an odd man out in many ways. It's too small; foreshadows the Prequel micro-fighter-itis disease - in Kenobi's words, no fighter that small can get this far into space on its own...yet we see them making the jump to hyperspace. It's a very McQuarrie design in a field dominated by Johnston originals or repackagings. It has even fewer straight lines than a B-wing. 

But it has a proper Star Wars colour scheme, looks fine on screen, and in SS would I have one? Yes please!
