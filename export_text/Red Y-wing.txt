The Red Y-Wing was made for ANH, but was never seen on film. The red Y was used for many promo shoots, that is why the old die-cast Y-Wing had red nose markings.   The one Bill George made was used for the end of Empire. He made his as a fan and when he ended up working for ILM they used his Y-Wing because none of the originals were available at the time.

The George Y was used in Jedi. The Y's at the end of ESB are the ANH. The Red Y was built for ANH but never used on screen.Many different details compared to the Gold Y's. As Flint mentioned it was used for promotional purposes and for the studio in London to make the 1.1 Y. 

The "turret" for the R2 on the ANH Red Y is the upper gun turret from the Tamiya 1/48 Lancaster Bomber.
