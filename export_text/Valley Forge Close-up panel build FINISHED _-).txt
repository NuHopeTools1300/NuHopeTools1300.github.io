Re: Valley Forge kit IDing

Absolutely swamped this week/weekend, so I don't know if i will be able to dig up kit scans.  Off the top of my head, and subject to memory lapses, you will need multiples of:

- Bandai's 1/30 Hueschrecke ( a truely weird weapon)

- Bandai's 1/30 Panzer IV Lang  L70 ( the one that has a fixed turret)

- Bandai's 1/15 Pz IV

- Plastruct ladder

- Plastruct plastic coated wire

Seriously, you will need to cast up multiples of a LOT  of  parts.  The otagonal plates are from the Hueschrecke.  The hulls on the left are from the Pz. IV Lang kit (trimmed).  Then there are wheel hubs, hatches and brake hatch covers from the bigger Pz. IV kit.

Good luck.  A couple of us gave up on this for a bunch of reasons, not the least of which was wall space.

Gene

2 hours masking, 60 seconds airbrushing, 5 minutes un-masking, rinse, repeat!

[IMAGE: 21162885266_25efd6dc05_b.jpg]

[IMAGE: 21196981481_39fdb20736_b.jpg]

It's very difficult to know exactly what colour the original miniature is.  It's certainly a light tan, not white or grey.

So I used Tamiya XF-55 Deck Tan mixed about 2:3 with flat white as a base.  Then there's a bunch with a little red/pink, some blues, and the rest are just darker and darker shades of the tan.
