Hi Guys,

I'd like to share with you my journey at scratch building a 66" TOS Enterprise with Shuttle Bay, Shuttle Craft and Bridge details. 

To help me visualize the model I printed out scale size plans on my plotter. The yard stick is for size reference.:love

[IMAGE: 66inchEnterpriseplans.jpg]

The primary hull will be shaped on a CNC router in two pieces, other parts will be turned on a lathe and smaller items simply hand made.

I'll be making molds of all the parts in order to have a hollow hull for lighting and to preserve all my hard work. I've built many other large models with finishes like what is seen below. These parts are right out of the molds, my TOS Enterprise model will be just like them in finish quality.

[IMAGE: example1.jpg]

[IMAGE: example3.jpg]

Because I'm making molds, I know the first question from everyone is going to be "are you going to make a kit?"

I'll share my build and answer your questions only if; 

1- You are polite and respect myself and others.

2- You don't ask if I'll be producing a kit. 

Thanks guys,

Stay tuned for more updates.

SB

The technique I used to shape the disk is the same technique and materials used to make ceiling medallions. If fact, you'll notice a striking shape resemblance when looking at certain medallion designs. I've seen a photo of Mel Keys shaping the 11ft model using the same technique.

To pay homage to Mr Datin and the crew I decided to build my model by hand instead of CNC help like originally planned.
