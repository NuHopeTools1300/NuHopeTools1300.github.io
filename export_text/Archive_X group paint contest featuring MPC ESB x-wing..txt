Archive-X recently hosted a group paint contest, the subject of which was the PropStore MPC X-wing.

ILM used MPC models for the minatures in quite a few of the scenes, so...it's an off the shelf kit, but it's screen prop, so I figured I'd share it here.

It was a great project and learning experience to attempt to replicate the ILM style! I don't really care if I win, I had a blast!

Don’t know about scale but it is the “regular” version, not the “snap kit”. 

If you want to be completely accurate you will want an early version of the kit which had engraved panel lines. Later versions had raised panel lines and a bit of added detail here and there.

I went for the oldest kit i could find, vintage A New Hope era. That is what would have been available around production of Empire and Jedi.
