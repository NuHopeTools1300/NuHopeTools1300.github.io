With the weathering on this, I wanted it to be more drastic than Rebel X-wings but still controlled in an ILM styling. In my head cannon the rebel ships saw space combat a lot more, while these partisan ones were mainly in an atmosphere, dealing with other kinds of grime. Also trying to keep in mind that these birds were housed in caves, sharing parts between 5-6 ships. I’m going to add some more discolored panels, and possibly some aurabesh graffiti. A couple hours of airbrushing and painting on chips with a sponge has gotten me here:
