Intersting note on the cannons.  From what I have found (pictures shared/purchased as well as a first generation casting of an original) the original cannons were total crap.  Seriousley.  They were integral to the wings and didn't survive the casting very well.  It raises the age old argument of intent vs actual.  If you believe in "intent" that remaking the guns makes all the sense, as obviousley Apogee didn't intend for them to be warped or have seams.  If you believe in actual, then the guns in my kit are (actually) a bit to nice.    :eek

I think (and I may be wrong) that this is one of my kits.  I currently own the masters that Icons used for their Viper.  A casting from these patterns is most likely what Monsters in Motion copied for their Viper.   Since Icons owned the patterns four seperate artisans have done work cleaning them up, fixing flaws, and replacing soft details to match the original show.  It's not perfect (yet) but in my opinion (and I'm biased) it's as close as you can get without spending a lot of money.
