Post #22  (forum post #662758)
Author    : EchoLeader
Date      : Sep 2, 2008 at 12:13 PM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Thanks Guy, the paint is not all that much. I custom mix my own colors using Liquetex Acrylic artist paints. Once I have the desired mix, I then airbrush it on. The black is just Rustoleum Flat Black. I give everything a coat of Testors Dull Cote. The weathering is done with Winsor & Newton oil paints. I used black and burnt umber diluted with odorless Terpenoid and washed in the depressions. I give everything a shot of Dull Cote to protect the finish afterwards.

That's it.
