I have an ICONS D-7, number 28 of 950. Bought it in like new condition from Entertainment Earth in 1999. The guy on the phone (pre-internet days) told me it was on display in the bosses office but all the original packing was included. It survived shipping with only a slight crack in the plexi case. ICONS plexi cases were notorious for arriving severly cracked. I paid $899 in 1999 for it and have seen them on ebay going from $800 to $1200. That's without the plexi case too. Mine hangs on the wall which solves the display spece issues.
