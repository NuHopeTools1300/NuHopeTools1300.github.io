If it's made for a production and used on screen for a movie, T.V. show, etc. it's a real screen used prop. If it's made for the production but never finished/filmed it's production made but not screen used. If it's made after the production is over, or cast up by crew without the intention of being filmed, I'd call that a replica. Even if it's cast out of the original production made molds.
