Hi,

I started working on my Salzo V5. I have been cleaning up the hull cast, re-threading some panel lines that were faded and now I'm ready to cut and glue the small raised details that are unique to each hero version of the fighter. But I have no idea what thickness I should use. Eyeballing it, I would say 1 mm seems like aa good fit, but I was wondering if exact thickness was known and sharable (plastruct reference maybe?).

I know it would not be a huge deal if I don't get the exact same thickness as long as it looks good, but on the other hand, if I get this super accurate kit and go through the pain of mesuring and recreating each panel, might as well try to get the thickness right too if possible.

Sorry if this has been asked before, I searched the board but could not find it.

Thank you
